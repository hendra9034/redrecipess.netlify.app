1. Prepare 3 ripe bananas, mashed
1. Make ready 3/4 cup sugar
1. Take 3/4 cup milk
1. Take 1/4 stick butter, melted
1. Prepare 3 medium size eggs

##### Steps to make Banana Oatmeal Bread with Pancake Mix:
