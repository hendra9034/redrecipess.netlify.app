The ingredients needed to make Best Pumpkin Bread Everrr!(::
  1. Prepare 6 tbsp unsalted butter, softened
  2. Make ready 1 cup sugar
  3. Get 1 egg
  4. Prepare 1 cup pureed pumpkin
  5. Make ready 1 cup all-purpose flour
  6. Get 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
  8. Make ready 1/4 tsp baking powder
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking soda
  10. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
Steps to make Best Pumpkin Bread Everrr!(::
  1. Preheat oven to 325Â° and grease a regular size loaf pan.
  2. Mix all dry ingredients in a small bowl and set aside.
  3. Cream butter and sugar together, then beat in the egg until it's a little fluffy. Then beat in your pumpkin.
  4. Slowly blend the dry mixture into the wet. Pour mixture into loaf pan and bake for an hour. Or when a toothpick comes out clean.
  5. IMPORTANT: wait for your bread to cool before removing! The first one I made I couldn't wait for and it was so moist that it fell apart!
