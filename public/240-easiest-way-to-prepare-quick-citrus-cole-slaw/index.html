The ingredients needed to make Citrus Cole Slaw:
  1. Make ready 2 cup shredded purple cabbage
  2. Make ready 3 cup shredded Napa Cabbage
  3. Prepare 1 cup Julienne cut carrots
  4. Take 1/4 cup Citrus flavored dressing. ( I used Panera Bread's brand "Tangerine Honey"
  5. Make ready 1 Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions to make Citrus Cole Slaw:
