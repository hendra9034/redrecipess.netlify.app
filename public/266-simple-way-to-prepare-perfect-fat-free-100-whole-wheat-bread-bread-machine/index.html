The ingredients needed to make Fat Free 100% Whole Wheat Bread (Bread Machine):
  1. Prepare 1 3/4 cups Water
  2. Make ready 4 1/3 cups Whole Wheat Flower
  3. Get 1/3 cup Vital Wheat Gluten
  4. Make ready 3 tablespoons Honey
  5. Take 1 1/2 teaspoons Salt
  6. Get 2 teaspoons Active Dry Yeast
Steps to make Fat Free 100% Whole Wheat Bread (Bread Machine):
  1. Add water
  2. Add honey to water
  3. Add flour, gluten, and salt
  4. Put an indentation in the center of the flour mixture
  5. Add yeast in the indentation
  6. Set machine to regular wheat cycle
