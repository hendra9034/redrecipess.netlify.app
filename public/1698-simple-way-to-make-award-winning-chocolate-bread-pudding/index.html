The ingredients needed to make Chocolate bread pudding:
  1. Make ready 180 ml milk
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  3. Make ready 100 g sugar (adjust as required)
  4. Take 20 g cornflour
  5. Get 2 brown breads chopped
  6. Make ready 50 g butter
  7. Get Handful cashew, almonds
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp milk for mixing cornflour
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp vanilla essence
  10. Get 1 pinch salt

Instructions to make Chocolate bread pudding:
  1. Heat a heavy bottom pan with milk till it comes to a boil. Keep the flame slow and gradually add cocoa powder and stir continuously so that it combines perfectly.
  2. Now in a small bowl mix cornflour and milk (at room temperature) nicely lump free. Keeping the flame slow, add this mixture into the cocoa milk and stir continuously.
  3. Before the milk starts thickening, immediately add butter, sugar, vanilla essence and a pinch of salt. Mix all ingredients well. Allow it to be on flame. It slowly starts thickening so stir with a whisk.
  4. Now add the small chopped brown breads and dry fruits into the chocolate custard and mix. The breads start absorbing the liquid and combines with rest of the ingredients.
  5. Watch for the perfect consistency and switch the flame off.
  6. Transfer the chocolate bread pudding into the serving dish. Garnish with cashews, almonds. Keep it in the refrigerator for cooling.
  7. As it cools down and sets, serve. You can serve it with ice cream too.
