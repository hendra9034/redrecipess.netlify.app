The ingredients needed to make Spiced Pumpkin Soup and Garlic Bread:
  1. Get 1 kg Butternut squash
  2. Make ready 1 litre chicken stock
  3. Make ready 3 tbsp Coconut oil
  4. Prepare 1 big yellow onion, diced
  5. Get 4 cloves garlic
  6. Get 3 tsp curry powder
  7. Get 1/2 tsp nutmeg powder
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp ginger powder
  9. Get 1/2 tsp chilli flakes/red pepper
  10. Make ready 1 tsp salt/to taste
  11. Make ready 1/2 tsp black pepper
  12. Make ready 1/2 cup coconut milk (I don’t use this)
  13. Prepare Garlic Bread
  14. Get 113 gr butter
  15. Take 4 cloves garlic
  16. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  17. Prepare 2 tbsp freshly chopped chinese parsley/coriander
  18. Take 1/4 cup grated parmesan
  19. Make ready 1 store bought baguette

Instructions to make Spiced Pumpkin Soup and Garlic Bread:
  1. Prepare the ingredients. Peel the butternut squash, cut cubed. Dice the onion, mince the garlic. - Heat the coconut oil in heavy soup pot or large dutch oven, stiry fry onion and garlic for a few minutes until fragrant.
  2. Add the cubed pumpkin/butternut squash, chicken stock, all spices and continue to boil until the squash is tender.
  3. Puree the soup in blender or food processor. I used hand blender. Once the soup is smooth and creamy, return it back to the pot. Stir in coconut milk if using, add salt and pepper to taste and heat it up before serving. Serve the soup warm, so much nicer when it is hot/warm.
  4. For the garlic bread, just combine all ingredients together. Slice the baguette and spread the butter mixture in both side of the sliced baguette and bake at 175 degree celcius for 10 mins
