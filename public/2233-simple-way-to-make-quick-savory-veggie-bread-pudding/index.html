Instructions to make Savory Veggie Bread Pudding:
  1. Preheat oven to 325°F
  2. Line an 11x14x3" deep cake pan with slices of stale bread.
  3. Beat together eggs, milk, oregano and cilantro.
  4. Pour half the milk/egg mix over the bread
  5. Add the filling, layering as you go along. Finish with the grated cheddar.
  6. Pour the remainder of the egg mix over the entire dish. Add the topping mix and Parmesan.
  7. Place in oven for 30 - 40 minutes or until the egg sets and top begins to brown.
  8. Remove from oven and allow to stand 10 minutes before serving.
