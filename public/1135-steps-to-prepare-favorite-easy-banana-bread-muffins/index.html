The ingredients needed to make Easy Banana Bread Muffins:
  1. Get 2-3 ripe bananas
  2. Prepare 1/3 cup melted butter
  3. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  4. Prepare Pinch salt
  5. Take 3/4 cup sugar
  6. Take 1 large egg, beaten (I used 1/4 cup egg white)
  7. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  8. Take 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour (I used GF flour)
Steps to make Easy Banana Bread Muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 and grease muffin pan
  2. In mixing bowl, mash bananas until completely smooth. Stir in melted butter.
  3. Mix in baking soda and salt. Stir in sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract. Mix in the Flour.
  4. Pour/Scoop batter into muffin tin. Bake for 12-15 min or until tester insert in center comes out clean.
  5. Remove from oven let cool and enjoy!
