The ingredients needed to make Cheesy pizza cups:
  1. Get 1 onion chopped
  2. Make ready 1 medium sized capsicum chopped
  3. Get 1/2 cup corn
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup Mozerella cheese
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp Mayonese
  6. Take 1 tbsp Oregano
  7. Prepare 1tbsp Chilli flakes
  8. Make ready As needed Pizza sauce or tomato sauce
  9. Make ready To taste Salt
  10. Get 1 tsp Pepper
  11. Prepare 10 slice white bread cut into rounds

Steps to make Cheesy pizza cups:
  1. Preheat the oven at 180° for 10 minutes.
  2. Bake it in oven for ten minutes.
  3. Grease the muffin moulds woth butter.
  4. Cut the rounds of bread and spread pizza sauce or tomato ketchep on it.
  5. Heat pan and add butter to it.
  6. Add garlic,onion,capsicum and corn. Saute it well.
  7. Season it with salt and pepper.
  8. After switching off the gas,add mayo to it.
  9. Season it with mozerella cheese and oregano and chilli flakes.
  10. Spread the mayo on the rounds of bread and keep it in muffin moulds giving it a shape of cups.
  11. Serve with sauce of ur choice.
