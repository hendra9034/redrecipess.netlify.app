The ingredients needed to make Zucchini Eggplant Banana Bread:
  1. Get 4 large eggs
  2. Make ready 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  3. Get 1 cup light brown sugar
  4. Take 1/2 cup melted butter, cooled
  5. Get 1/2 cup canola oil
  6. Get 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  7. Make ready 1 cup seeded, grated, unpeeled zucchini
  8. Get 1 cup mashed banana
  9. Make ready 1 cup peeled, grated eggplant (seeds are ok)
  10. Take 2 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  11. Take 1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  12. Prepare 1/4 tsp ground ginger
  13. Get 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  14. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  15. Make ready 1/4 tsp baking powder
  16. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  17. Take 1 tsp lemon zest (fresh is best)
  18. Take optional:
  19. Make ready 1/2 cup chopped walnuts, pine nuts or dried cranberries
  20. Make ready topping:
  21. Take 1/2 cup granulated sugar for sprinkling, also optional.
Instructions to make Zucchini Eggplant Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 325°F
  2. Place zucchini, eggplant, banana and zest in a large bowl. Add eggs and both sugars. Wisk well.
  3. Wisk in butter, oil and vanilla. Set aside.
  4. In a medium bowl sift or wisk together flour, salt, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg and ginger.
  5. Add dry to wet and stir until incorporated. Fold in nuts or cranberries if desired.
  6. Divide the batter between two smaller sized greased or sprayed loaf pans.
  7. Sprinkle tops with sugar.
  8. Bake for 45-60 minutes or until toothpick inserted into center comes out clean.
  9. Cool and serve.
