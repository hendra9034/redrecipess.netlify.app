The ingredients needed to make Argentine Red Shrimp With Tomatoes & Feta Cheese:
  1. Get 8-10 oz Argentine red shrimp raw peeled and deveined
  2. Make ready Olive oil for coating the shrimp
  3. Take Freeze-dried oregano or fresh
  4. Make ready Salt and Pepper
  5. Get 2 medium tomatoes peeled, cored, and sliced roughly chopped
  6. Prepare 1-2 Tbs olive oil
  7. Prepare 1/3 cup diced shallots
  8. Take 1-2 garlic cloves minced
  9. Take Salt and pepper
  10. Make ready Dash or so of white wine
  11. Prepare Red pepper flakes
  12. Get 1-2 oz. Feta cheese crumbled
  13. Take Freeze-dried mint leave flakes or fresh
  14. Get Cooking, assembly, and baking
  15. Make ready Preheat the oven to 400f
  16. Prepare In a bowl, add a little olive oil, salt, pepper, a few dashes of oregano, and toss the shrimp in the seasoned oil to coat and set aside
  17. Take Heat a medium saute pan to medium and add 1-2 Tbs Olive Oil
  18. Prepare Add shallots, garlic, and salt and pepper to taste, saute until soft, about five minutes
  19. Prepare Add the tomatoes, and red pepper flakes. Simmer, mashing the tomatoes a bit. Cover and cook down until thickened. Remove the lid and cook to a sauce
  20. Take Add a splash of white wine and simmer for a few minutes more
  21. Make ready Transfer the tomatoes, shallots, and garlic onto individual ovenproof serving dishes and spread across the bottom
  22. Prepare Layer the shrimp across the tomatoes and crumble the feta over the shrimp
  23. Get Put into the oven and cook about 10-15 minutes until bubbly
  24. Get Remove and sprinkle with Mint and serve along with crusty bread and White Wine
Steps to make Argentine Red Shrimp With Tomatoes & Feta Cheese:
  1. Raw Argentine Red Shrimp with shell looks different then most.
  2. Cooked down Tomatoes.
  3. Add the Shimp, scatter some Feta and put in preheated 400f oven.
  4. Dust with Mint aand serve.
