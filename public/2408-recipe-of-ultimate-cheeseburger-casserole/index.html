Instructions to make Cheeseburger Casserole:
  1. Heat oven to 375°F.
  2. Mix beef, 1/2 cup milk, crumbs, onion powder, eggs, salt, worcestershire sauce, garlic salt, mustard, & pepper. Spread mixture in shallow baking dish. Bake for 1 hr. Drain off fat.
  3. Arrange cheese slices over meat.
  4. Mix baking mix & 1/2 cup milk until soft dough forms. Spread dough out over cheese. Bake for 15 minutes longer.
