The ingredients needed to make Brown Sugar & Milk Wheat Bread Loaf:
  1. Take 270 grams Bread flour
  2. Get 30 grams Flourless Wheat Bran (roasted bran)
  3. Prepare 24 grams Brown Sugar (powdered)
  4. Prepare 5 grams Salt
  5. Get 30 grams Unsalted butter
  6. Get 190 grams Milk
  7. Get 120 grams Homemade natural yeast starter
  8. Prepare ↓ How to Make the Yeast
  9. Take 4 4/5 grams Dried yeast
  10. Take 196 grams Milk
Instructions to make Brown Sugar & Milk Wheat Bread Loaf:
  1. Knead all the ingredients together save for the butter. After kneading it, add in the butter and knead it again. Let it rise again.
  2. Gently coat your hands in flour and divide the dough into thirds. Form them into balls and let them sit for 20 minutes.
  3. Roll the dough out into rectangular shapes and then fold the right side and left side into their respective sides. Then roll up the dough starting from the bottom and seal it.
  4. Let the dough rise a second time until it's about 1.5~2cm from the top of the pan.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes in an oven at 300℉ (150℃). Then raise the temperature to 400℉ (200℃) and bake for additional 22-25 minutes. Once the steam is done rising out of the bread, take it out of the mold.
