The ingredients needed to make Wiener Sausage Bread:
  1. Make ready 200 grams Bread flour (Nippun Fluffy Bread Strong Wheat Flour is what I used)
  2. Make ready 10 grams Sugar
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp Salt
  4. Get 20 grams Butter
  5. Get 100 ml Water
  6. Get 2/3 tsp Dry yeast
  7. Prepare 6 stick Long weiner sausages
Instructions to make Wiener Sausage Bread:
  1. Add the ingredients into a bread maker and complete the first rising. Divide the dough into sixths, cover it with a tightly wrung out damp towel, and let it rest for 10 minutes.
  2. Roll out the dough into an oval shape. With a scraper (or a kitchen knife), make 5 horizontal slices on each side of the dough.
  3. Layer them on top of each other alternately. Seal the dough on the bottom.
  4. Let the dough sit for 30 minutes at 35℃ (95℉) for the second rising. Once it has expanded 1.5 times, put mayonnaise or ketchup and parsley on top.
  5. Bake them at 180℃ to 190℃ (350℉ to 375℉) for 15 minutes.
