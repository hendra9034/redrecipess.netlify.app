Steps to make Pumpkin Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 170°C.
  2. Cream shortening, sugar, eggs & pumpkin. Leave to stand.
  3. Sift the flour, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda & spices.
  4. Mix all ingredients well.
  5. Grease & flour 2 x 9" bread tins.
  6. Put mixture in bread tins & bake for 1 hour or until a skewer comes out clean.
  7. Cool on a wire cooling tray, then slice & serve.
