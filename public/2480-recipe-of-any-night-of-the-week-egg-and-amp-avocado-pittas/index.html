The ingredients needed to make Egg & Avocado Pittas:

  5. Prepare spring onions finely sliced
  6. Make ready lemon juice

Instructions to make Egg & Avocado Pittas:
  1. Place the eggs in a pan of boiling water - Boil for 10 minutes - Place the eggs in a bowl of cold water, peel the eggs and chop roughly
  2. Remove the peel and stone from the avocado and mash - Add the lemon juice to the mashed avocado
  3. Mix together the egg, avocado and spring onions - Grill the pitta breads for a minute on each side
  4. Fill with the salad leaves and egg mix
