The ingredients needed to make Quick Banana Nut Bread:
  1. Get 1 box cake mix (yellow, white, or butter)
  2. Make ready 3 eggs
  3. Get 1/3 C Oil
  4. Take 1 C Chopped Nuts
  5. Make ready 1/2 TSP Cinnamon
  6. Get 3 medium bananas (very ripe)
Steps to make Quick Banana Nut Bread:
  1. Preheast oven to 350
  2. Mash bananas in a medium bowl.
  3. Spray your loaf pan (will fill a loaf pan or 5 mini loaf pans)
  4. Mix eggs,oil, and bananas together.
  5. Add cake mix and cinnamon. Mix well.
  6. Batter will be thick. Add nuts if you want at this point.
  7. Bake for about 15 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean from center of loaf.
