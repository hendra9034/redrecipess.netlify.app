The ingredients needed to make Nicole's "Easy Banana Bread":
  1. Prepare 4 large Banana's
  2. Get 2 large White or Brown EGGS
  3. Make ready 1 tsp Baking Soda
  4. Take 1/4 cup Veg Cooking Oil
  5. Get 2 1/2 cup Shifted Flour
  6. Prepare 1 cup White Sugar
  7. Make ready 2 Bread pans
  8. Get 1 tbsp Shortening

Instructions to make Nicole's "Easy Banana Bread":
  1. Preheat Oven to 400°f
  2. Grease Two Bread Pans with the shortening,set to the side.
  3. In large mixing bowl, mix all wet ingredients, then slowly add all dry ingredients to the wet ingredients.
  4. pour half of the banana bread into each loaf
  5. Bake in oven for 25 minutes, then turn each loaf around and bake for another 25 minutes. Use a toothpick to check to see if each loaf us done. Poke in the middle,if it comes out dry there done.
  6. When done set to the side and let cool before you slice the loaf.
