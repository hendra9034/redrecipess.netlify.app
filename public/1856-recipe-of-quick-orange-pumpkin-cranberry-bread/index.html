Instructions to make Orange Pumpkin Cranberry Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350º.
  2. Mix dry ingredients in medium sized bowl. Set aside.
  3. Mix all wet ingredients with a mixer until well incorporated.
  4. Add orange zest and juice of the whole orange.
  5. Gradually add dry ingredients to wet mixture until well combined.
  6. Fold in 1/2 cup nuts and cranraisins.
  7. Pour batter into 2- 9 x 5 loaf pan greased with nonstick cooking spray. (yes, this makes 2 loaves)
  8. Bake for 1 hour and 20 minutes to 1 hour and 30 minutes until a toothpick inserted into the center of the loaf comes out clean.
  9. Once cooled, take the loaves out of the pans and let cool completely on a wire rack. Serve with warm butter or jelly. Enjoy!
