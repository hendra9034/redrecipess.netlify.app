The ingredients needed to make Tomato Soup with Bread:
  1. Make ready 5 smokey pork sausages
  2. Prepare 1 carrot
  3. Make ready 1/4 spam
  4. Get 1/2 chicken breast
  5. Take 2 tomatoes
  6. Take 5 garlics
  7. Get 1 brown shallots
  8. Prepare Salt
  9. Get Sugar
  10. Prepare White pepper
  11. Make ready 2 tsp nutmeg powder
  12. Get Chicken broth
  13. Take 3 tbsp tomato sauce (ketchup)
  14. Make ready Starch flour to thicken
Instructions to make Tomato Soup with Bread:
  1. Boil the tomatoes until it is deflated and soft. Then smash to get the juice. Afterwards, pour it to the boiling chicken broth.
  2. While waiting to boil the tomatoes, prepare all the ingredients. Cut all in the same size if possible. Bring all to boil.
  3. Fry garlic and shallots until it is golden brown, then add to the pot.
  4. Season with salt, sugar, pepper, nutmeg, and ketchup. Lastly, add starch flour to thicken.
