The ingredients needed to make Bread and Butter Pudding:
  1. Make ready Bread and Butter Pudding
  2. Take 6 slice of bread
  3. Get 80 grams of sugar
  4. Make ready 720 ml of milk
  5. Get 4 eggs
Instructions to make Bread and Butter Pudding:
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C
  2. Butter Bread and tear it into small pieces, place pieces in a greased muffin tin
  3. Beat eggs and sugar ,then pour in milk and mix. Then sprinkle desired amount of nutmeg and cinnamon
  4. pour over bread in muffin tin
  5. Bake until just firm, approximately 45 minutes
