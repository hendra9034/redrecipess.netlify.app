The ingredients needed to make Gluten Free Vegan Banana Flour Bread:
  1. Prepare 1 & 1/2 cup banana flour
  2. Get 1 & 1/2 cup mashed ripe bananas (about 3 medium bananas)
  3. Get 2 flax seed eggs**
  4. Take 1/4 cup nondairy milk of choice
  5. Take 1/2 cup date paste
Steps to make Gluten Free Vegan Banana Flour Bread:
  1. Turn oven on at 350°C
  2. Mix in all wet into banana flour, and pour the batter into a loaf cake pan.
  3. At this point, you could add nuts or seeds of choice on top.
  4. Bake the cake for about 50 minutes, and let it cool outside before storing.
  5. *mix 2 tbsp flax meal with 6 tbsp water and let it set in a cool place for 10 minutes (preferably the freezer).
