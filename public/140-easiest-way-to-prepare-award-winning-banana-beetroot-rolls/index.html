The ingredients needed to make Banana - Beetroot rolls:
  1. Prepare 1 Beetroot(boiled)
  2. Prepare 4 Raw Banana(boiled)
  3. Get 2 Potato(boiled)
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup Green Coriander
  5. Get 1 tbsp Pizza Seasonings
  6. Take 1 tbsp Red Chilli Powder
  7. Prepare Pinch Turmeric Powder
  8. Get 1 tbsp - Chat Masala
  9. Get 1/4 cup - Bread Crumbs
  10. Get to taste Salt as per taste
  11. Get as needed Oil to fry

Instructions to make Banana - Beetroot rolls:
  1. Great beetroot and potato. Add boiled raw banana.
  2. All green coriander and all spices. Mix well. Add bread crumbs and again mix well.
  3. Grease palm, and take ball from mixture and roll it. Make all rolls like it.
  4. Heat oil in a pan and fry all rolls. Banana- beetroot rolls are ready.
