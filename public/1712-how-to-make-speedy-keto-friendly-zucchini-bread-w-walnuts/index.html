The ingredients needed to make Keto Friendly Zucchini Bread w Walnuts:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup butter softened
  2. Prepare 3/4 cup erythritol sweetener
  3. Make ready 5 large eggs
  4. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Make ready 1 1/2 cup almond flour
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup coconut flour
  7. Prepare 4 tsp baking powder
  8. Take 1/2 tsp xanthan gum
  9. Make ready 1 tsp cinnamon
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  11. Make ready 2 cups zucchini/ grated/ excess water removed
Instructions to make Keto Friendly Zucchini Bread w Walnuts:
  1. Preheat the oven 350f. Grease a 9x5 loaf pan, line with parchment paper. In a large bowl, cream the butter and sweetener together until fluffy
  2. Add the eggs, on at a time stir in the vanilla mix well.
  3. In separate bowl stir together almond flour, coconut flour, baking powder, cinnamon, xanthan gun and salt.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, and stir to complete. Add walnuts.
  5. Add the grated zucchini, and gold gently.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared pan. Bake for 50-55 mins. Check with toothpick if inserted and comes clean.
