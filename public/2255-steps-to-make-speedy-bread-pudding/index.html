Instructions to make bread pudding:
  1. whip together eggs sugar vanilla
  2. add regular milk n condensed milk
  3. add in diced bread mix until bread is soaked
  4. toss in walnuts and raisins mix well
  5. let bread mixture sit for 15 minutes until all liquid is soaked in to bread
  6. butter and flour a two inch baking pan
  7. pour mixture in pan bake in oven on 250 for 30 minutes
