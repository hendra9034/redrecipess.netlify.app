The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Chip Pumpkin Nut Bread:
  1. Take 2 cup flour
  2. Take 2 tsp baking powder
  3. Take 1/2 tsp baking soda
  4. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  5. Take 1 tsp cinnamon
  6. Take 1 tsp pumpkin pie spice
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  8. Prepare 1 cup pumpkin puree
  9. Get 1 cup white sugar
  10. Get 1 tsp vanilla
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup milk
  12. Get 2 eggs
  13. Prepare 1/4 cup soft butter
  14. Get 1 cup nuts (pecan/walnut)
  15. Make ready 1 cup chocolate chips (your choice)

Instructions to make Chocolate Chip Pumpkin Nut Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Sift dry ingredients together in a bowl
  3. In a seperate bowl combine pumpkin, milk, sugar, vanilla and eggs then beat
  4. Slowly add dry ingredients and butter to the pumpkin mixture until well mixed
  5. Now add nuts and chocolate chips by hand
  6. Bake for 45-55 minutes
  7. *** Oven temps vary so check your loafs in the center
  8. *** Can make 1-2 loafs depending on how large you want them
  9. *** Also to prevent sticking, I line the bottom of pan with wax paper
