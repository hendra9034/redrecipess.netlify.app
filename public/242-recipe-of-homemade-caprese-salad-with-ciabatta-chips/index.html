The ingredients needed to make Caprese salad with ciabatta chips:
  1. Get 1/2 loaf panera ciabatta bread
  2. Make ready 1/2 tsp coarse salt
  3. Make ready 1/4 tsp pepper
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup grated parmesan
  5. Take 3 beefsteak tomatoes sliced 1/4 inch
  6. Get 3 tbsp fresh basil
  7. Get 1 crumbled goat cheese
  8. Take 2 tsp balsamic vinegar
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp EVOO

Instructions to make Caprese salad with ciabatta chips:
  1. preheat oven 375
  2. line cookie sheet with foil
  3. coat ciabatta with cooking spray
  4. sprinkle with 1/4 teas salt and 1/4 teas pepper
  5. bake 5-6 mins
  6. arrange to ties on platter, scatter remaining salt and pepper, tuck basil leaves between tomatoes. sprinkle goat cheese, whisk vinegar and oil, drizzle over salad, serve with ciabatta chips
