The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Zucchini Bread:
  1. Make ready 3 Eggs
  2. Prepare 2 cup Sugar
  3. Make ready 1 cup Canned Pumpkin
  4. Make ready 1 cup Butter or Margarine (melted)
  5. Take 1 tbsp Vanilla
  6. Get 3 cup All Purpose Flour
  7. Prepare 1 tsp Baking Soda
  8. Take 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
  9. Get 1/2 tsp Salt
  10. Take 1/2 tsp Ground Cinnamon
  11. Make ready 1/2 tsp Ground Nutmeg
  12. Get 1/2 tsp Ground Cloves
  13. Get 1 cup Shredded Zucchini
  14. Make ready 1/2 cup Chopped Walnuts
  15. Get 1/2 cup Raisons

Steps to make Pumpkin Zucchini Bread:
  1. In a mixing bowl combine eggs and sugar, add pumpkin, butter and vanilla.
  2. Combine dry ingredients.
  3. Gradually add pumpkin mixture and mix well.
  4. Stir in zucchini and nuts.
  5. Pour into two greased and floured 9"x5"x3" loaf pans.
  6. Bake at 350°F for 45-50 minutes.
  7. Cool in pans 10minutes.
  8. Remove from pans and cool on wire rack.
