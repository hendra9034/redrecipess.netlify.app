Steps to make Monkey Bread:
  1. Preheat oven 350° F
  2. Combine 1 cup sugar & 2 tsp. cinnamon in a zip lock bag.
  3. Cut biscuits into quarters, add to bag and shake until coated.
  4. Transfer biscuits to a greased 12in. tube pan.
  5. Melt butter, add sugar and remaining cinnamon and bring to boil. Pour over biscuits.
  6. Bake for 30 - 40 min. or until biscuits are puffed and liquid is carmelized.
  7. Let stand and cool before serving. Serve whole so people can pull apart.
