The ingredients needed to make Savoury Bread Pudding:
  1. Prepare 6 bread slices cut in small square pieces
  2. Take 1 cup cabbage grated
  3. Make ready 1 cup paneer cut in small pieces
  4. Make ready 1 cup grated cheese
  5. Make ready 2 tablespoons cream
  6. Prepare 1 cup milk
  7. Make ready 2 tablespoons yoghurt
  8. Make ready 1 cup butter or oil as per your choice
  9. Prepare 1 teaspoon black pepper
  10. Make ready 1 teaspoon salt
  11. Get 1 green chilli chopped
  12. Make ready 1 teaspoon red chilli
  13. Get 2 radish cut in small pieces and put in the vinegar for 2 hours
Steps to make Savoury Bread Pudding:
  1. Firstly put all ingredients, like cut the breads in small pieces, paneer in small cubes, grated cheese and chopped cabbage.
  2. Then grease the baking mould and put aside.
  3. Then take bowl put milk add butter, cream, yoghurt, black pepper, salt, green chilli, red chilli and mix it nicely.
  4. Then take grease mould first put bread pieces all around, then spread cabbage and Paneer.
  5. Then put milk batter and then put all around grated cheese. Like this process again repeat same.
  6. Then baked in microwave on conviction mode for 5 minutes. Your dish is ready for serving.
