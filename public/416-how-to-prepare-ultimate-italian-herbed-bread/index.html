The ingredients needed to make Italian herbed bread:
  1. Get Dry ingredients
  2. Prepare 6 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  3. Make ready 2 cup dry milk powder
  4. Take 1 tbsp salt
  5. Take 1/4 cup basil leaves fresh
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp rosemary leaves fresh
  7. Get 2 tbsp oregano leaves fresh
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp semolina flour
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp wheat protein
  10. Make ready Yeast proof
  11. Get 4 tbsp active dry yeast
  12. Take 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  13. Get 2 1/2 cup warm water
  14. Get 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Steps to make Italian herbed bread:
  1. Dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water to proof the yeast colonies.
  2. In mixing bowl combine dry ingredients
  3. Blend in oil until light and crumbly.
  4. Knead in yeast proof until smooth or about 8 minutes.
  5. Let rise 1 hour
  6. Punch down and roll out into 2 12x8 inch long rectangles. Roll up firmly like a log from the short end and place seam side down in loaf pan
  7. Bake 375°f for 35 minutes.
