The ingredients needed to make Fried Cooking Squash (Cucumbita sp.):
  1. Prepare 1 "cooking squash" (cucumbita sp., with white peel)
  2. Make ready 4 eggs
  3. Make ready 2 tsp salt
  4. Take 100 g bread crumbs
  5. Get 100 g all pupose flour
  6. Take Generous amount of sunflower seed oil for frying
Steps to make Fried Cooking Squash (Cucumbita sp.):
  1. The cucumbita sp. Is maybe a lesser known type of the squash family. It has a white peel, and in my country its quite popular for cooking thick soups, hence the name cooking squash
  2. Peel and slice the squash into thin slices, gently salt it, cover the slices with flour, then egg, lastly bread crumbs and fry them in hot oil.
