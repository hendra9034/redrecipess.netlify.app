The ingredients needed to make Best Banana Bread:
  1. Make ready 1 1/2 c.mashed ripe bananas
  2. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  3. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
  4. Take 1 1/4 c.flour
  5. Make ready 1 package instant banana pudding mix
  6. Get 1/2 c. vegetable oil
  7. Prepare 1/2 c. Apple sauce
  8. Take 1 tsp vanilla
  9. Make ready 1 1/2 c.sugar
  10. Prepare 2 eggs beat
  11. Take optional crushed nuts (about 1/2cup)
Instructions to make Best Banana Bread:
  1. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Mix all wet ingredients
  3. Mix all wet ingredients together with the dry, blend well.
  4. If you choose to add nuts do so at this time.
  5. Heat oven to 350°
  6. Pour mix into greased bread pan /muffin pan.
  7. Cook muffins about 24 minutes, or until tooth pick comes​ out clean.
  8. Cook in bread pan about 45 minutes, or until tooth pick comes​out clean.
  9. Enjoy.
