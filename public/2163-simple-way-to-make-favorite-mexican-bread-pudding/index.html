Instructions to make Mexican Bread Pudding:
  1. Preheat oven to 375
  2. In a big bowl or recipient crumble bread, into very small pieces
  3. In a separate recipient beat 3 eggs & add can of condensed milk, can of evaporated milk, 1 cup of regular milk, cinnamon, vanilla extract, walnuts, raisons, & half cup of butter & mix all together.
  4. Add mixture to crumbled bread & mix very well till all bread is all moistened.
  5. butter pan 13 × 9 in
  6. Add bread pudding mixture into buttered pan
  7. Bake time 1 hour & 30 min
