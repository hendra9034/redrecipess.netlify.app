The ingredients needed to make Healthy Whole Wheat Flour & Oatmeal Bread:
  1. Make ready 150 grams Bread (strong) flour
  2. Take 80 grams Whole wheat flour
  3. Take 50 grams Oatmeal
  4. Prepare 3 grams Salt
  5. Make ready 25 grams Brown Sugar (or white sugar)
  6. Prepare 30 grams Butter
  7. Get 200 ml Milk
  8. Take 3 grams Dry yeast
Steps to make Healthy Whole Wheat Flour & Oatmeal Bread:
  1. Weight out all of the ingredients. Place the yeast into the bread maker's yeast dispenser and place the rest of the ingredients into the bread case. Let the bread maker work it's magic until the first rising is complete.
  2. After the first rising, lightly release the air and form into two balls. With the seam facing down, cover with plastic and a damp towel and let rest for 20 minutes.
  3. After letting the dough rest, release the air again and roll it out into a rectangle. Fold the top and bottom sides towards the center and then roll it up from the side. Tightly close the seam and place in the bread pan.
