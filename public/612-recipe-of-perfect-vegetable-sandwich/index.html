The ingredients needed to make Vegetable sandwich:
  1. Make ready 4 slices white bread
  2. Take 2 eggs
  3. Make ready Handfull shallots
  4. Take 300 g cabbage
  5. Get 1 medium carrot
  6. Take 1 medium tomato/onion cut in rings
  7. Take Mayonnaise
  8. Make ready Butter for toast
  9. Make ready Cooking oil for frying
  10. Take Salt and pepper

Steps to make Vegetable sandwich:
  1. Shred your cabbage and grate the carrots.Put a little oil in the pan and fry the cabbage for 4 minutes.Season with salt and pepper.Set aside to cool down.
  2. Beat the eggs in a bowl and season with salt and pepper.Put in a handful of shallots and grated carrot in the eggs mixture.
  3. When the cabbage is cooled,mix it with the egg mixture.
  4. In a clean plan put some oil and allow it to heat.Use a tablespoon to scoop out the cabbage and egg mixture and fry it until the egg is cooked on both sides.
  5. When you're done with the eggs,toast your bread and spread a little butter.
  6. Now assembling the sandwich..Spread mayonnaise on one side of your toast,lay the cabbage and egg and lastly tomato and onion cut in rings.
  7. Cover your toast and cut into triangle shapes.Can be served hot or cold.
