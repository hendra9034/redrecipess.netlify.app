The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Bread:
  1. Prepare 1 1/4 cup sugar
  2. Take 1/4 tsp baking powder
  3. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp cloves
  5. Take 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  6. Get 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  7. Take 3/4 tsp salt
  8. Take 2 eggs
  9. Make ready 1 cup pumpkin
  10. Take 1 2/3 cup flour
  11. Get 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  12. Prepare 1/2 cup water
  13. Make ready 1/2 cup walnuts

Instructions to make Pumpkin Bread:
  1. combine sugar, baking powder, soda, cloves, nutmeg, cinnamon and salt. set aside.
  2. mix together: beaten eggs, flour, pumpkin, vegetable oil and water.
  3. add dry ingredients and fold in walnuts.
  4. divide into greased loaf pans and bake 1 hour at 350°F.
