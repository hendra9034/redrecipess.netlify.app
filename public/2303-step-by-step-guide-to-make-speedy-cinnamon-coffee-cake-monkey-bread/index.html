Steps to make Cinnamon Coffee Cake (Monkey Bread):
  1. Cut biscuits into quarters and shake in bag with, 1 Cup Sugar, 1 tsp Cinnamon and 1 tsp Nutmeg
  2. Put shaken Biscuits in a Bundt pan
  3. Heat, 3/4 cup Brown Sugar, 3/4 cup butter and 1 tsp cinnamon until melted. - Then pour mixture over the biscuits in the Bundt pan.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 35 minutes.
