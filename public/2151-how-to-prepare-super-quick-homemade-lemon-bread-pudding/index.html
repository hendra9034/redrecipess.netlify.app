The ingredients needed to make Lemon Bread Pudding:
  1. Get bread cubes, not fresh but slightly dried, the better the bread the better the pudding, though any bread will do
  2. Take lemon curd, I used homemade but store bought is fine
  3. Get large eggs, I used 5 small eggs
  4. Prepare milk, I used 2% but any would be fine, or a combination of milk and cream, the higher the fat the richer the pudding
  5. Prepare sugar
  6. Get vanilla extract, or lemon extract for more lemon flavor
  7. Take butter
  8. Prepare powdered sugar for dusting

Steps to make Lemon Bread Pudding:
  1. Preheat oven at 350°F
  2. Lightly grease a deep 9"x9" baking dish, or similar size, with butter
  3. In a large bowl, toss bread with lemon curd to coat
  4. In a separate bowl, wisk eggs, milk, sugar, and vanilla till combined and sugar is dissolved
  5. Add mixture to bread and toss so that all bread is covered, let sit 1 hour or overnight in refrigerator
  6. Bake 45 min covered with foil, then 15 minutes uncovered to brown the top
  7. Dust with powdered sugar
  8. Serve with fresh fruit
