The ingredients needed to make Guilt free Banana Bread:
  1. Make ready 1 cup flour
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup almond flour
  3. Make ready 3 ripe bananas
  4. Make ready 3/4 cup coconut palm sugar
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup coconut oil
  6. Make ready 1 Egg
  7. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  8. Make ready 1 tsp baking powder
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  10. Get 2 Tbsp Chia seeds
  11. Make ready Optional: 1/2 c raisins or chocolate
Steps to make Guilt free Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat oven 350 degrees
  2. Beat Together Sugar, Oil, and Egg on medium speed until smooth.
  3. Add in bananas to the batter and smash them with the fork until chunky
  4. Add the rest of the ingredients and mix with a spatula until well blended and you can't see any dry flour. Do not over mix.
  5. Bake at 350° in loaf pan lined with foil or baking sheet for 40-50 Minutes
  6. Check the bread to ensure its done by inserting a toothpick in the center of the loaf all the way in. If you pull it out without seeing wet batter, the bread is ready!
