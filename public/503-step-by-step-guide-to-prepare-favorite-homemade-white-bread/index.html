The ingredients needed to make Homemade white bread:
  1. Make ready 6 cup all purpose flour
  2. Take 3 tbsp sugar
  3. Prepare 2 tsp salt
  4. Take 2 packages fast rising instant yeast
  5. Prepare 2 cup water
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup milk
  7. Get 3 tbsp margarine

Steps to make Homemade white bread:
  1. Set aside 1 c of all purpose flour from total amount.
  2. Mix remaining flour, sugar, salt, and yeast in a large bowl.
  3. Heat water, milk, and margarine until hot to the touch, 125°-130°F.
  4. Stir hot liquids into dry mixture.
  5. Mix in reserved flour, enough to make a soft dough that does not stick to the bowl.
  6. Turn out onto floured board and knead until smooth and elastic, about 8-10 minutes.
  7. Cover dough and let rest 10 minutes.
  8. Cut dough in half and shape into loaves.
  9. Place in two, greased 9" x 5" loaf pans.
  10. Cover and let Russ in a warm place until double in size, about 40-50 minutes.
  11. Bake at 400°F for 30 minutes.
  12. Remove from pans and cool on wire racks.
  13. Makes two loaves.
