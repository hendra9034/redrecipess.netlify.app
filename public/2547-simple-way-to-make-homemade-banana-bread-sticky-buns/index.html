Steps to make Banana Bread Sticky Buns:
    1. Preheat oven to 450°F
  2. slice butter into 10 tabs.
  3. slice bananas into 1/2 inch pieces
  4. put one tab of butter into each muffin cup
  5. add about a tablespoon of brown sugar to each cup
  6. spinkle cinnamon in each cup
  7. put desired amount of walnuts
  8. cut each biscut in the middle making a hole to insert a banana piece(super easy)
  9. secure the ends of each biscuit
  10. set a biscuit in each cup (on top of the butter brown sugar..)
  11. place in oven for about 6-8 minutes.
  12. when done set a large plate or baking sheet over the top and flip it over quick. let it stand for about a minute, to let all the toppings drip down:)
