The ingredients needed to make Easy Banana Bread:
  1. Get 3 ripe bananas
  2. Prepare 1/3 cup butter, melted
  3. Take 1 cup granulated sugar
  4. Get 1 large egg
  5. Take 1 tsp vanilla
  6. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
  7. Take 1 pinch salt
  8. Take 1 1/2 cup flour
  9. Get 1/2 cup chopped nuts

Instructions to make Easy Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Mix butter with smashed banana, then beat in egg, sugar and vanilla.
  3. Sprinkle baking soda and salt over mixture, then mix in flour gradually. Fold in chopped nuts.
  4. Pour batter into greased 4×8 inch loaf pan and bake for 1 hour.
