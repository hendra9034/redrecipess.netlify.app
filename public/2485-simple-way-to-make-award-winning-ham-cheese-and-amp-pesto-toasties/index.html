Steps to make Ham, Cheese & Pesto Toasties:
  1. Preheat the oven to 200 oC
  2. Layer 2 slices of gluten free bread with the cheese, ham and pesto
  3. Sandwich together with the reamining slices of bread
  4. Beat together the egg, milk and seasoning
  5. Dip the sandwiches in the egg mix
  6. Fry for a couple of minutes on each side in a shallow pan over a medium heat
  7. Transfer to a baking sheet and bake in the oven for 5 minutes
  8. Serve with a side salad
