The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread (Nanny Mac's):
  1. Prepare 4 ripe bananas
  2. Get 1 beaten egg
  3. Take 1/2 cup melted butter
  4. Make ready 1 teaspoon baking soda
  5. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  6. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  7. Prepare 1 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  8. Get 1 vanilla pod
  9. Take 2/3 cup sugar
  10. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
Steps to make Banana Bread (Nanny Mac's):
  1. Preheat oven to 170°C.
  2. Mash bananas in a large bowl. Over ripe (very brown) bananas work best.
  3. Add melted butter to bananas. Mix well.
  4. Add 2/3 cup of sugar and vanilla to egg and banana mixture, cream together.
  5. Mix in baking soda, cinnamon, salt and nutmeg.
  6. Using a spatula or wooden spoon fold in flour until fully mixed.
  7. Grease loaf tins.
  8. Divide mixture into two small loaf tins (18 x 9 x 6.5 cm)
  9. Place tins in oven and bake for 40 minutes. Test to make sure it's finished by poking a toothpick into the center of the loaf, if it comes out clean, it's finished!
