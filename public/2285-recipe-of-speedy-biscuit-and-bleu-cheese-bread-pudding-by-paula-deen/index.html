Instructions to make Biscuit and bleu cheese bread pudding by Paula Deen:
  1. preheat oven to 350Â°
  2. crumble 1/2 cup of the bleu cheese into bottom of greased 9x13 pan
  3. crumble biscuits over the bleu cheese crumbles
  4. saute onions, green peppers, corn in butter until softened
  5. add the sauteed vegetables over the biscuits
  6. mix eggs, heavy cream, salt, pepper together. pour over biscuits and vegetables
  7. crumble the remaining of the bleu cheese on top
  8. bake 50-60 minutes until golden and set in the middle
  9. serve warm
