The ingredients needed to make "White Bread":
  1. Take 1 pack active dry yeast
  2. Make ready 2 1/4 cup warm water(110-115°)
  3. Get 3 tbsp granulated sugar
  4. Take 1 tsp salt
  5. Take 2 tbsp canola oil
  6. Take 6 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
Instructions to make "White Bread":
  1. In large bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water. Add sugar, salt , oil. Add 3 cups of the flour stir. Add in enough flour to form soft dough.This will make 2 loaves.
  2. Turn onto floured surface.knead for 8-10 min. Place in greased bowl. Let rise for about 1&1/2 hours.
  3. Punch down.Turn on lightly floured surface. Divide dough in half. Form into loafs. Place in greased 9 x5 loaf pans. Cover let rise in warm place. Let rise for about 30-35 min.
  4. Bake at 375° for 30-35 min. Or until golden brown. Remove from pans. Let cool on wire rack. ( I brushed butter on top of mine soon as it came out of oven( .
