The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Bread:
  1. Make ready 3 c turbino sugar or 2 c white and 1 c brown
  2. Get 1 c vegetable oil
  3. Make ready 4 eggs
  4. Take 2 c baked and pureed pumkin or 15 oz can
  5. Get 3 1/2 c flour
  6. Get 2 tsp salt
  7. Take 2 tsp baking soda
  8. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  9. Take 1 tsp nutmeg
  10. Make ready 1 tsp cinnamon
  11. Get 1 tsp ginger
  12. Prepare 2/3 c water
Steps to make Pumpkin Bread:
  1. Heat oven to 350*. Grease 2 regular loaf pans with oil or cooking spray. Do not flour.
  2. Beat sugar and oil, add eggs then stir in pumpkin. Set aside.
  3. Combine dry ingredients in a separate bowl.
  4. Alternately add dry ingredients and water to the pumpkin mixture mixing until just combined each time. Do not overmix.
  5. Pour batter into pans and place on cookie sheet in oven for about one hour or until wooden skewer comes out clean.
  6. Cool pans on rack for 10-15 mins, they will still be hot. Turn out loaves onto rack and continue cooling until just warm. Wrap tightly in plastic wrap and let sit overnight. Serve with whipped honey butter.
