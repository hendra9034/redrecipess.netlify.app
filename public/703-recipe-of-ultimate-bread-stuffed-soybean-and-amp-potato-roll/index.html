The ingredients needed to make Bread stuffed soybean & potato roll:
  1. Get as per quantity Bread
  2. Prepare 1 medium boiled potato smashed
  3. Make ready 1 cup boiled soybean
  4. Prepare 1 onion chopped
  5. Take 1 cube chopped ginger
  6. Take 3-4 cloves garlic chopped
  7. Make ready 2 green chills chopped
  8. Take 1 tbsp jeera powder
  9. Take 1 tbsp dhaniya powder
  10. Take 1 tbsp haldi powder
  11. Make ready 1 tsp black pepper powder
  12. Take to taste Salt according
  13. Prepare As required White oil for deep fry
  14. Get 1 egg
  15. Take 1/2 tbsp cornflour
  16. Get As required Breadcrumbs as per roll quantity
  17. Get As required Coriander leaves optional
  18. Get As required Kasundi & tomato sauce

Steps to make Bread stuffed soybean & potato roll:
  1. Cut all edges of bread & roll over it with belan.
  2. In mixer make a mixture of semi chopped soybeans.
  3. Heat pan & in little oil start frying onion,when onion turns transparent add chopped ginger,garlic,chills & fry for few minutes.
  4. When raw smell of garlic and ginger is gone now add smashed potato let it fry until colour little changes.
  5. Now add semi chopped soybeans & again fry for few minutes.
  6. Now one by one add jeera,dhaniya,black pepper, haldi powder & salt according to taste & quantity.
  7. Mix all ingredients well when a nice aroma comes from the stuffing put it in a plate & let it little cool.
  8. Now make solution of egg & cornflour. Keep aside.
  9. In a small bowl take little water & with the help of finger brush all the edges of bread slice.
  10. In the middle of the bread now add the stuffing & roll each bread. Seal it with water.
  11. Now dip each roll in egg solution & coat it properly with bread crumbs.
  12. Then pour adequate amount of oil & deep fry each rolls.
  13. Serve hot with kasundi & tomato sauce.
  14. Healthy and tasty tiffin for kids is ready.
