The ingredients needed to make Apart Banana bread with dried strawberry using a bread machine:
  1. Get 250 g bread flour
  2. Get 30 g butter
  3. Get 20 g sugar
  4. Get 3 g salt
  5. Take 2 bananas and fresh milk(total 210g)
  6. Prepare 3 g instant yeast
  7. Prepare 70 g dried Strawberrys
Instructions to make Apart Banana bread with dried strawberry using a bread machine:
  1. In your bread machine add all ingredients except dried Strawberrys.
  2. Set and start the machine to the dough cycle. You may need to add dried Strawberry (or any dried fruits) before first rise.
  3. After the first rise, remove from bread pan.Then decide 16 pieces and form each piece into round roll. (for 18cm x 18cm square bread pan)
  4. Second rise 40°C for 40mins.(Cover in a baking cloth or in the oven for rising course.)
  5. Bake at 180°C for 15 minutes. Enjoy!
