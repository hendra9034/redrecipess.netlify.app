The ingredients needed to make Banana walnut cranberry bread:
  1. Prepare 3 cups flour
  2. Take 1 and 1/2 cups sugar
  3. Take 6 med soft bananas
  4. Get 2 cups chopped walnuts
  5. Make ready 2 cups dried cranberries
  6. Get 4 sticks butter softened
  7. Prepare About 1 cup of milk
  8. Prepare 2 heaping table spoons of baking powder
