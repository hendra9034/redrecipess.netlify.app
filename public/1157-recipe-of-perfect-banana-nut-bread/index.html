The ingredients needed to make Banana Nut Bread:
  1. Prepare 2 c. All-purpose flour
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp. Baking soda
  3. Get 1/2 tsp. Salt
  4. Prepare 4 over ripped bananas
  5. Make ready 1 c. Sugar
  6. Take 3/4 c. Unsalted butter melted
  7. Take 2 large eggs
  8. Make ready 1 tsp. Pure vanilla extract
  9. Get 1/2 c. Chopped pecans

Instructions to make Banana Nut Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Lightly grease 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  2. In large bowl, combine flour, baking soda salt, bananas, vanilla, sugar, eggs, butter and pecans together.
  3. Pour batter into the loaf pan and set time for 1hr and 15 minuets.
