The ingredients needed to make Easy Banana Bread:
  1. Make ready 1 3/4 ap flour
  2. Get 3/4 sugar
  3. Get 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  4. Make ready 1 cup applesauce
  5. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon salt
  6. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  7. Take 2 extra ripe bananas
Instructions to make Easy Banana Bread:
  1. Mash bananas, mix the flour and baking soda together.
  2. Add sugar, salt, applesauce to bananas
  3. Add flour mixture in halves
  4. Preheat oven to 350
  5. Butter loaf pan
  6. Pour batter in and smooth out top
  7. Bake for 50 mins or until golden brown
  8. Nuts of your choice optional
