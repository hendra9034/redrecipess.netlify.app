The ingredients needed to make simple banana bread:
  1. Take wet mixture
  2. Get 1/2 cup butter room tempture
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup brown sugar
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup white sugar
  5. Prepare 2 large eggs seprated whites in bowl
  6. Take 4 tbsp milk,cream, or evaporated milk what ever you have on hand
  7. Prepare dry mixture
  8. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  9. Take 2 tsp baking powder
  10. Prepare 1 1/2 cup flour or unbleached flour
  11. Prepare 1 cup any nuts you choose
  12. Get bake ware
  13. Take 1 bread loaf pan. 5x9x2. oiled and coated with flour. you can use pan spray with flour all ready in it

Steps to make simple banana bread:
  1. pre heat oven to 350 for five minutes then drop to 300
  2. mix brown and white sugar in bowl till creamy
  3. add both egg yolks and mix
  4. mix in 4 tbsp of milk
  5. seprete bowl beat egg whites till stiff peaks form if eggs are not fresh you will not have a lot of volume in your whites. making your bread sink
  6. mix in flour,baking soda,baking powder to wet mixture dont over mix just enough that you dont see flour
  7. take egg whites and fold in to your mixture. do not bang bowl you will deflate whites mix well folding and twisting the bowl till whites are gone add nuts
  8. add to your pan place in oven for 45 minutes to 1hour
  9. check with toothpick for doneness there should not be any batter on it
  10. use knife along edges flip out on to rack to cool
