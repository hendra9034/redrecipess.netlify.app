The ingredients needed to make Banana Walnut Bread:
  1. Take 2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. Get 5/8 cup brown sugar
  3. Get 1 teaspoon baking soda
  4. Take 2 eggs, beaten
  5. Take 1/4 teaspoon salt
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup butter
  7. Prepare 4-5 overripe bananas
  8. Make ready Dash cinnamon powder
  9. Make ready Dash vanilla extract
  10. Take Handful walnuts
Instructions to make Banana Walnut Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt. In a separate bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture; stir just to moisten. Add cinnamon and vanilla extract. Pour 1/2 batter into prepared loaf pan, and add walnuts in between before filling the pan with the rest of the batter.
  3. Add a slice of banana at the top for decoration.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 55 to 60 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.
