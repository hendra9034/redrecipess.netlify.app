Steps to make Coffee bread pudding:
  1. Preheat oven to 180C
  2. Spread butter on bread
  3. Cut bread into 2cm cubes and lay on 12cm diameter baking tray
  4. Beat egg until whites and yolk is well mixed
  5. Pour beaten egg over bread
  6. Melt sugar in coffee, add milk to the mixture, and pour over bread
  7. Bake in oven for 30 minutes
