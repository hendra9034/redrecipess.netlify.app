The ingredients needed to make Best Home-made Bread:
  1. Get 3 tbsp yeast
  2. Make ready 2 1/2 tbsp honey (or 3 tbs white sugar)
  3. Get 1 cup warm water
  4. Make ready 3 eggs
  5. Get 3/4 cup melted coconut oil (or canola, vegetable, or olive oil)
  6. Make ready 5 cup warm water
  7. Make ready 6 cup whole wheat flour
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp salt
  9. Make ready 6 cup white flour (6+ cups)
  10. Take 1 cup shredded flax seeds
Instructions to make Best Home-made Bread:
  1. Mix yeast, honey and 1 cup warm water. Let stand for 10 minutes.
  2. Add flax seeds, eggs, and oil and mix well.
  3. Add 5 cups of warm water and whole wheat flour. Mix well.
  4. Add salt and mix well.
  5. Add white flour one cup at a time, mixing well each time. Add flour until the dough pulls away from the sides of the bowl and is soft and kneadable.
  6. Take dough and divide into six equal sections. Form a loaf with each section.
  7. Place loafs in oiled loaf pans and poke a few holes in a nice pattern with a knife (this allows the dough to rise without forming air pockets).
  8. Cover with clean towel and let rise until at least doubled their size in a warm place. (Letting them sit for 7-10 minutes in the oven on "warm" setting, covered with a towel, will help them rise better). After the 7-10 minutes take them out and let them rise on the counter until they are the appropriate size.
  9. Bake at 350°F until golden brown.
  10. I always mix and knead the dough in my Bosch machine and then bake in the oven. Much easier and yields much fluffier bread.
  11. After the bread is baked, take them out of the pans immediately and let cool. Slice the bread. Place loafs in ziploc bags. Because this recipe yields 6 loafs, I usually freeze 5 and just take them out as needed, defrosting and storing them in the refrigerator. Pop them in the toaster and they still taste fresh as ever. :)
