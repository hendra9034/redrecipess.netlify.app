The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Pesto Flatbreads:
  1. Make ready gluten free flatbreads or pitta breads
  2. Take tsps dairy free pesto (see recipe)
  3. Prepare eggs
  4. Make ready rocket
  5. Prepare cherry tomatoes halved
  6. Make ready dairy free cheese shavings
  7. Make ready salt and pepper for seasoning

Instructions to make Breakfast Pesto Flatbreads:
  1. Grill the gluten free flatbreads - if you are using a pitta bread make a small hole to deflate the pitta - Spread the dairy free basil pesto on top
  2. Heat some cooking oil in a large shallow pan - Crack in the two eggs and fry until the yolk is still runny - about 3 minutes
  3. Place the eggs onto each flatbread - Top with the rocket, tomatoes, dairy free cheese shavings and season with salt and pepper
