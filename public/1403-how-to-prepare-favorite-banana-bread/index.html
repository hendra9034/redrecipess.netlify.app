The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread:
  1. Take 3-4 Bananas (Medium size, overly ripe)
  2. Get 2 1/2 cup (300 g) Self-raising flour
  3. Get 1/2 cup (100 g) Brown sugar
  4. Make ready 1 teaspoon Baking soda
  5. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  6. Get 2 Eggs
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup Butter (soften)
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup Milk
  9. Make ready 1/2 tbsp Vanilla extract
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp Lemon juice
  11. Take 1/2 cup Walnuts or pecan
  12. Prepare 1/4 cup Chocolate chips
  13. Prepare 1/2 cup Blueberries

Instructions to make Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat oven 175C (350F)
  2. Oil spray or butter grease a 9"x5" loaf pan
  3. Mash bananas with fork then add lemon juice and set aside
  4. Medium bowl- mix dry ingredients (flour, baking soda, salt)
  5. Large bowl- wet ingredients Mix butter and brown sugar first +eggs +mashed bananas +vanilla +1/2 of milk
  6. Slowly sift, add and mix dry ingredients into wet ingredient bowl. add and mix the other 1/2 milk if needed. DO NOT OVER MIX! The bread will get tough.
  7. Optional: Walnut or pecan, chocolate chip, blueberries, apple and etc. Slowly fold into bowl, and pour into creased loaf pan. (Can also sprinkle on top)
  8. Bake for approx 55mins until toothpick comes out clean.
  9. ☆If the bread starts to get too brown, cover the bread with foil and bake until cooked. This will prevent bread goes darker.
  10. After bread is cooked, take it out of ovan and let it cool for 1hr before taking out of pan. ENJOY♡
