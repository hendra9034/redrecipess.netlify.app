The ingredients needed to make Apricot bread pudding:
  1. Prepare 50 grams ready to eat dried apricots
  2. Get 45 grams caster sugar
  3. Prepare 25 grams low-fat spread
  4. Get 450 ml (about 3/4 pint) semi skimmed milk
  5. Prepare 2 eggs
  6. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  7. Make ready 8 slice of fruit bread

Instructions to make Apricot bread pudding:
  1. Grease a baking dish with a teaspoon of the low fat spread and then use the rest to butter one slice of each slice of fruit bread.Cut each slice into triangles and arrange them in the bottom of the dish.Sprinkle the chopped apricots over the top.
  2. Beat the egg,sugar,milk and vanilla extract together in a bowl.
  3. pour the mixture evenly over the bread,cover with teatowel and leave to soak for 20 mins.Now turn over to 180c/355f/gas4.
  4. After 20 mins soaking put pudding into oven for 35-40 mins or until golden brown.
