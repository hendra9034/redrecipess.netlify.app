The ingredients needed to make Coconut Flour Banana Bread:
  1. Take 1 1/4 cup mashed banana (about 3 bananas)
  2. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup almond butter
  4. Prepare 2 eggs at room temperature
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup coconut flour
  6. Prepare 3/4 tsp baking soda
  7. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  8. Take 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
  9. Get 1 tsp walnuts (optional)
  10. Take 1/2 tsp cinnemon
Steps to make Coconut Flour Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line a 8x4 inch or 9x5 inch loaf pan with parchment paper and spray with nonstick cooking spray
  2. Combine bananas, vanilla and almond butter. Mix until combined smooth and creamy. Add in each egg- mix in one by one.
  3. Slowly add in dry ingredients
  4. Fold in chocolate chips and walnuts
  5. Spread in parchment line bread tin with cooking oil. Cook on 350 for 25 minutes or until fork comes clear.
  6. Reminder - tastes better the next day!
