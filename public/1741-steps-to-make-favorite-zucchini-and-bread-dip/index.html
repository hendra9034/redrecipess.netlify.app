Steps to make Zucchini and Bread dip:
  1. In medium saute or frying pan add julienned zucchini strips at medium heat
  2. Cover mixture in olive oil leaving about an inch in the pan
  3. Salt and pepper lightly
  4. Add fresh basil leaves twisted and pinched
  5. Crush tomato over top of mixture
  6. Stir and cover making sure zucchini is covered
  7. Heat oven to 400 degrees
  8. Heat bread 5-10 mins
  9. Reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes (zucchini should be crisp not soggy)
  10. Enjoy! Oil makes fantastic bread dip
