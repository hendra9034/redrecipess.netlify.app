The ingredients needed to make Yummy banana bread:
  1. Get 1 c soft butter (2 sticks

  5. Get 2 tsp lemon juice
  6. Take 4 c flour
  7. Take 2 tblsp baking powder
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
