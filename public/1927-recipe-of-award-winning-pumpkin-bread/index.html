Instructions to make Pumpkin bread:
  1. In a bowl add in the flour, salt,milk and flavor,In a separate bowl add the warm water,egg and sugar stir well,pour in the liquid mixture on to the flour and knead for 10 mints.Add the butter and knead again for 15 mints.
  2. Scoop some dough and mold into a ball then you press in the chocolate and mold into a ball again,use thread to role over the scooped dough.
  3. Arrange on a tray and keep in a warm place to rise for 40minutes.
  4. Brush with egg and bake.
  5. Serve with tea or cold drink.
