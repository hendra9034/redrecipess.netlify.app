Steps to make Bangin' Bread Pudding:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Spray 9x13 baking pan with nonstick spray
  3. Cut loaf into 1 inch chunks. Set aside
  4. In a large bowl, whisk cream, eggs, and egg yolks together
  5. Add sugar, vanilla, and spices to mixture
  6. Add the bread and filling and let soak for 10 minutes until bread has soaked liquid
  7. Pour contents into baking pan and cover with parchment paper. Cover pan with aluminum foil
  8. Place in center of oven and bake for 30-40 minutes
  9. Remove foil and parchment after 35 minutes and cook for another 15 minutes or until golden brown
  10. Serve warm.
