The ingredients needed to make Best Ever Moist Banana Bread! üçå:
  1. Make ready 2 Cups Flour
  2. Make ready 2 Teaspoons Baking Powder
  3. Make ready 1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  4. Get 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
  5. Take 1 Cup Sugar
  6. Take 1/2 Cup Milk
  7. Take 1/2 Cup Oil
  8. Get 3-4 Ripe Bananas
  9. Get 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  10. Prepare 2 Eggs
  11. Prepare 1 Cup Chocolate Chips and/or Nuts (Optional)
Steps to make Best Ever Moist Banana Bread! üçå:
  1. Stir together flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt
  2. In a separate bowl, cream bananas and sugar
  3. Add milk, oil and vanilla
  4. Add eggs one at a time
  5. Slowly incorporate dry ingredients into wet ingredients
  6. Optional - Add 1 Cup of Chocolate Chips and/or Nuts
  7. Grease loaf pan and bake at 325 for approximately 65-75 minutes
  8. Enjoy!!
