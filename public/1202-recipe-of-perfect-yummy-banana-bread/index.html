The ingredients needed to make Yummy banana bread:
  1. Take 1 c soft butter (2 sticks
  2. Get 2 c sugar
  3. Prepare 4 eggs
  4. Get 8 bananas mashed
  5. Make ready 2 tsp lemon juice
  6. Get 4 c flour
  7. Get 2 tblsp baking powder
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
