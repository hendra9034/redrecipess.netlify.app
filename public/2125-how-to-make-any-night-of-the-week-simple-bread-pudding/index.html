Steps to make Simple Bread Pudding:
  1. Cut breads into cubes.
  2. Spread bread cubes in an 20 cm / 8 inch round pan
  3. Sprinkle Sugar, Salt & Raisins.
  4. Pour milk and add butter
  5. Microwave for 4 minutes, until the butter melts.
  6. Stir eggs with fork untill combine well. Pour over bread.
  7. Microwave for 10 minutes.
  8. Sprinkle milk powder & cinnamon.
  9. Serve warm or chilled. When cooked the center may still be slightly soft. But it wiill set up as pudding cools.
