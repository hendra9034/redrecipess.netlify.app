The ingredients needed to make chocolate zucchini bread:
  1. Prepare white flour
  2. Make ready unsweetened coco powder
  3. Take ground cinnamon
  4. Get baking soda
  5. Prepare baking powder
  6. Take salt
  7. Get white sugar
  8. Get eggs
  9. Prepare vegetable oil
  10. Make ready vanilla extract
  11. Get shredded zucchini (about 2 med. size zucchinis)
  12. Prepare semi sweet chocolate chips
  13. Take chopped walnuts (optional)

Instructions to make chocolate zucchini bread:
  1. pre heat oven to 350* F. and grease and flour 2 9x5 bread pans
  2. in large bowl mix flour, coco powder, cinnamon, salt, baking soda and powder
  3. in another bowl beat well eggs and sugar. add oil and vanilla, stir well, add zucchini
  4. stir in flour mixture. coat chocolate chips with flour and then mix in, add nuts.
  5. spoon evenly into pans. bake 55-60 minutes. poke with knife should come out clean but remember there will be melted chips. cool for 10 minutes then remove from pan
  6. freezes really well!
