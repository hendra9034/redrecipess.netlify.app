The ingredients needed to make Nutella and Banana Bread Pudding:

  2. Get 1/4 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  3. Make ready 4 pieces bread roughly chopped into small cubes
  4. Get 1 egg beaten
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup Nutella
  6. Take 1/2 banana cut into small pieces
