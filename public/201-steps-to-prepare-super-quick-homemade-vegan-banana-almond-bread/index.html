The ingredients needed to make Vegan Banana Almond Bread:
  1. Get 4 medium size of overripe banana
  2. Get 2/3 cup canola oil (160 ml)
  3. Prepare 1 1/4 cup all purpose flour (180 gr)
  4. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking soda
  6. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  7. Get 1 tsp vanilla essence
  8. Make ready 1 tsp cinnamon powder
  9. Prepare 1/3 cup granulated sugar (80 gr)
  10. Get 1/3 cup toasted almond (80 gr)
Steps to make Vegan Banana Almond Bread:
  1. Preheat oven into 350 °F or 180°C.
  2. Toast the almond about 10 minutes or until it fragrances, while preheating the oven. Chopped them coarsely. Set aside.
  3. Prepare the tray for the banana bread, grease it with butter and dust some flour on it, or just use non-stick spray.
  4. Mash the banana using a fork, set aside.
  5. Add oil and sugar, whisk them until incorporate. Set aside.
  6. In other bowl, sift dry ingredients such as all purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, vanilla essence, cinnamon powder, and salt. And add them into the wet batter.
  7. Fold the wet and dry ingredient gently using spatula until all combined.
  8. Add chopped toasted almond. Fold them until all mix well.
  9. Pour the batter into the tray, bake it about 45-50 minutes.
  10. Check using a toothpick if it comes out clean without any wet batter it means your bread is done. But if it comes out with wet batter add about 5-10 more minutes.
  11. Tips: for smaller oven or if you use small electric oven, bake it using 325°F or 160°C
