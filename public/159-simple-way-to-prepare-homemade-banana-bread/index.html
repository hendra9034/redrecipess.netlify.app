The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread:
  1. Get 250 g brown sugar- I use a mix of raw and dark brown sugar
  2. Make ready 350 g mashed banana
  3. Make ready 220 g natural yoghurt
  4. Get 4 free range eggs- gently beaten
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  6. Take 340 g self-raising flour
  7. Make ready Greased loaf tin
  8. Make ready Oven preheated to 170 degrees celcius

Instructions to make Banana Bread:
  1. Mix first five ingredients together in a bowl
  2. Gently fold in the flour
  3. Pour into a greased loaf tin or cake tin
  4. Bake for approximately 40minutes until slightly brown and cooked through- use the skewer test.
  5. Cool slightly and turn out onto a wire rack to cool
  6. Can be served warm or toasted
