The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Crumpets with Roasted Wheat Germ:
  1. Prepare 70 grams ★ Cake flour
  2. Prepare 70 grams ★ Bread flour
  3. Take 10 grams ★ Wheat germ (roasted)
  4. Make ready 1 tsp ★ Sugar
  5. Take 3 grams ★ Dry yeast
  6. Prepare 2 grams Salt
  7. Get 200 ml Milk
Instructions to make Breakfast Crumpets with Roasted Wheat Germ:
  1. Put the ★ ingredients in a bowl. Warm the milk to body temperature and pour about half on the sugar and dry yeast.
  2. Add the rest of the milk and salt, and mix with a whisk until the mixture becomes smooth.
  3. Cover with cling film, and allow it to rise in a warm place for about 1 hour until it has doubled in size.
  4. Heat a frying pan over high heat, then place it on top of a damp kitchen cloth to lower its temperature.
  5. Melt a small amount of butter in the frying pan, and pour in the batter from Step 3 to form 8 cm circles.
  6. Fry for about 2 minutes over low-medium heat, and when some bubbles appear on the surface, flip it over and fry for a minute. Fry the others in the same way.
  7. Spread butter, and add maple syrup or jam on top to taste, and enjoy.
