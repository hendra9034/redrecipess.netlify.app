The ingredients needed to make Masala Potatoes Sandwich:
  1. Take 4 slice white bread
  2. Make ready 1 chopped onion
  3. Make ready 4 boiled potatoes
  4. Make ready 1/2 spoon Ginger garlic paste
  5. Get 1/2 spoon red chilli flakes 1
  6. Take 1/4 spoon Chat masala
  7. Make ready 1/2 spoon Kitchen King
  8. Prepare 1/4 spoon Kashmiri mirch
  9. Get to taste Salt
  10. Prepare as needed Butter
  11. Take 2 spoon Tomato sauce
  12. Make ready 1 spoon red chilli sauce
Instructions to make Masala Potatoes Sandwich:
  1. Grate boiled potatoes and add all ingredients (all masala). Mix them well. Stuffing is ready.
  2. Take 1 slice of bread and spread over butter, Tomato & Red chilli sauce on it. Also spread over the potatoes stuffing mixture and cover it by buttered 2nd slice of bread.Apply same process for remaining bread.
  3. Keep the stuffed bread on sandwich maker and put butter on both side of bread. Cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Now cut cooked sandwich from middle and serve with Tomato & Chilli sauce. Masala Potatoes Sandwich is ready.
