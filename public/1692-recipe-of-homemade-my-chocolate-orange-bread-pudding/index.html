The ingredients needed to make My Chocolate Orange Bread Pudding:
  1. Prepare 3/4 French stick loaf
  2. Make ready 400 g Tin of Custard choc orange custard make it with plain too
Steps to make My Chocolate Orange Bread Pudding:
  1. Add the broken up bread to a 6 inch round oven dish
  2. Next pour over the custard out the Tin evenly add to a preheated heated oven 175°C for 15 minutes or roast on ninja setting for 10 minutes 170 °C
  3. When it's cooked take out let it settle then break up a choc chip biscuit all over the top.
  4. When adding to dishes to serve add a tsp clotted cream or whipped Cream on the side and Serve.
