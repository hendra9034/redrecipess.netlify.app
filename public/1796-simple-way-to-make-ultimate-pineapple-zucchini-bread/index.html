The ingredients needed to make Pineapple Zucchini Bread:
  1. Make ready 3 eggs
  2. Make ready 2 cup sugar
  3. Prepare 2 cup grated, drained zucchini
  4. Take 3 cup flour
  5. Prepare 1 cup crushed drained pineapple
  6. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  7. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  8. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  9. Make ready 1 cup walnuts
  10. Make ready 2 tsp vanilla
  11. Get 1 cup oil
Steps to make Pineapple Zucchini Bread:
  1. Beat eggs, sugar, vanilla, and oil until fluffy
  2. Add zucchini, flour baking powder, salt and baking soda.
  3. Stir in pineapple and nuts
  4. Bake in 2 inch greased and floured loaf pans.
  5. Bake in preheated 350°F oven for 1 hour
