The ingredients needed to make Easy Banana Raisin Bread:
  1. Take 2 cups white flour
  2. Get 2 tsp Baking Powder
  3. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  4. Prepare 3/4 cup sugar
  5. Take 2 large eggs
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  7. Take 1/4 cup 2% milk
  8. Make ready 1 tsp pure vanilla
  9. Take 2 ripe bananas
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup sultana raisins
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp. sugar

Instructions to make Easy Banana Raisin Bread:
  1. Heat oven to 350F
  2. Combine dry ingredients in one bowl and wet in another
  3. Combine both bowls and blend until smooth
  4. Pour into a 6"x10" loaf pan lightly coated with vegetable spray and flour. Sprinkle white sugar on top
  5. Bake at 350F for about an hour or until a toothpick comes out clean when inserted. The sugar crust should be caramelized
  6. Cool completely before serving
  7. Serve with sweet butter or fruit spread
