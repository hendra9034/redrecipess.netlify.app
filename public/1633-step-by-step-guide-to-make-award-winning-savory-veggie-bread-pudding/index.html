The ingredients needed to make Savory Veggie Bread Pudding:
  1. Take Filling
  2. Get 1 cup sliced leeks
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
  4. Get 1 clove crushed fresh garlic
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup minced sweet peppers
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
  7. Make ready 1 bunch fresh cilantro chopped coarse
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup sliced black olives
  9. Prepare 1/4 cup julienned sun-dried tomatoes in olive oil
  10. Get 1/2 cup milk
  11. Prepare Topping
  12. Take 1 tsp chopped fresh chives
  13. Prepare 1 tbsp fresh oregano
  14. Prepare 1/2 tsp summer savoury
  15. Get 1/2 cup real parmesan cheese
  16. Get 1 tbsp fresh rosemary
  17. Make ready Pudding
  18. Take 6 slice stale bread
  19. Prepare 6 eggs
Instructions to make Savory Veggie Bread Pudding:
  1. Preheat oven to 325°F
  2. Line an 11x14x3" deep cake pan with slices of stale bread.
  3. Beat together eggs, milk, oregano and cilantro.
  4. Pour half the milk/egg mix over the bread
  5. Add the filling, layering as you go along. Finish with the grated cheddar.
  6. Pour the remainder of the egg mix over the entire dish. Add the topping mix and Parmesan.
  7. Place in oven for 30 - 40 minutes or until the egg sets and top begins to brown.
  8. Remove from oven and allow to stand 10 minutes before serving.
