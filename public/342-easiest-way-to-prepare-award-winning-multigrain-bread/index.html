The ingredients needed to make Multigrain Bread:
  1. Get 150 gms Flour
  2. Get 100 gms Wheat flour
  3. Prepare 80 gms Multigrains
  4. Prepare 10 gms Salt
  5. Prepare 300 ml Water or as needed
  6. Get 10 gms Yeast
Steps to make Multigrain Bread:
  1. Mix all the ingredients and make a dough
  2. Soak multigrain 50 gms in a bowl with water for 30 mins. Then strain the water from grains.
  3. Mix all the grains in flour and add water as per requirement.
  4. Mix well for 20 mins. Knead then rest for 15 min keep aside.
  5. Then after 15 mins shape the dough in roll shape give one big mark cut in the center of loaf.
  6. Apply Milk wash and sprinkles some multigrain on top of it.
  7. Then again rest for 10 mins.
  8. After 10 mins bake it preheated oven.
  9. Bake at 200c for 30 mins.
