The ingredients needed to make Banana walnut cranberry bread:
  1. Make ready 3 cups flour
  2. Take 1 and 1/2 cups sugar
  3. Get 6 med soft bananas
  4. Get 2 cups chopped walnuts
  5. Prepare 2 cups dried cranberries
  6. Take 4 sticks butter softened
  7. Make ready About 1 cup of milk
  8. Make ready 2 heaping table spoons of baking powder
