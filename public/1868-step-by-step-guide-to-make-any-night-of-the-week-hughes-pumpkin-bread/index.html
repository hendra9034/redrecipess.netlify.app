Instructions to make Hughes pumpkin bread:
  1. Grease and flour insides of a 9x5 loaf pan
  2. In large mixing bowl, stir together: flour, baking powder, baking soda, sugar, and spices
  3. Add oil, water, pumpkin, and eggs blending at medium speed until mixture is smooth.
  4. Stir in dates and walnuts and turn into prepared loaf pan
  5. Baked in a preheated 350° oven for 1.5 hours
  6. Loosen sides from pan with small spatula and turn bread out onto cooling rack
  7. Turn loaf right side up (loaf will have a crack in the top)
  8. Cool completely and wrap in plastic film.
  9. Can be stored on the counter for a few days or refrigerated for up to two weeks.
