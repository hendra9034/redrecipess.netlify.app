The ingredients needed to make Gluten free Mcdonald's Chicken select strips:
  1. Prepare boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut in 1 inch pieces or strips
  2. Take beaten eggs but I use eggless eggs
  3. Get garlic powder
  4. Prepare onion powder
  5. Take dried dill
  6. Prepare salt
  7. Take pepper
  8. Take gluten free flour
  9. Prepare italian seasoning gluten free bread crumbs
  10. Get Vegetable Oil or Peanut Oil for the frying
Steps to make Gluten free Mcdonald's Chicken select strips:
  1. Preheat oil in a large skillet or deep fryer over medium heat or to 350 degrees.
  2. Combine chopped chicken, eggs and spices (for the wet ingredients) in a large bowl.
  3. Mix well and let set for about 15 minutes.
  4. Combine dry ingredients together. Then take strips out and bread in gluten free flour and spices
  5. Drop one by one in hot oil and fry until they're golden. And when they're done, drain on paper towels.
