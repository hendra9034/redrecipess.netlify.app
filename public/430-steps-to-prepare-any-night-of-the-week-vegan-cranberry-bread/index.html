The ingredients needed to make Vegan Cranberry bread:
  1. Get 2 cup whole wheat pastry flour
  2. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  3. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  4. Take 1 cup Orange juice
  5. Take 1 1/2 cup cranberries
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup walnuts
Instructions to make Vegan Cranberry bread:
  1. Food process all ingredients together
  2. Spray your pan 9x9 or muffin pan
  3. Bake @ 350 for 30 to 40 mins
