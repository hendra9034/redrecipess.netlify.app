The ingredients needed to make Haps Banana Bread:
  1. Prepare 1 bread loaf pan, greased
  2. Get 1 cup sugar
  3. Take 1 stick butter
  4. Take 3 very ripe bananas, mashed with fork
  5. Get 2 eggs, beaten
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp vanilla
  7. Prepare 2 cup flour
  8. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
  9. Prepare 1 pinch salt
  10. Take 1 9x13 cake pan filled halfway with hot water
Instructions to make Haps Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Cream butter & sugar
  3. Mix flour, soda and salt, set aside
  4. Add beaten eggs, mashed bananas & vanilla to butter & sugar, mix with a whisk
  5. Add flour mixture to the bananas mixture, using a wooden spoon mix together just until mixed, do not mix too long
  6. Using a scraper pour batter into the bread pan, then set bread pan into the 9x13 pan of water(cooking the bread in a water bath keeps the butter from browning too dark.)
  7. Bake for 1 hour to 1.5 hours. When a knife comes out clean it's done
  8. Remove from pan immediately. Cool on a wire rack
  9. Can be doubled easily
