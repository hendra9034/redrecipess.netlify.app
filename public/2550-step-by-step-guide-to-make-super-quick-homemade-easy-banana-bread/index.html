The ingredients needed to make Easy banana bread:
  1. Get Dry ingredients
  2. Get 3 cups flour
  3. Make ready 1 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  4. Get 1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  5. Make ready 1 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  6. Get Wet ingredients
  7. Get 6 mashed bananas
  8. Get 3/4 cup vegetable oil
  9. Get 1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  10. Take 3 eggs
  11. Get I 1/2 cups sugar

Steps to make Easy banana bread:
  1. Mash the 6 bananas till smooth. Add the other wet ingredients and combine to form a consistent wet mixture.
  2. Mix all the dry ingredients as you sieve into a bowl.
  3. Add the dry ingredients (in three batches) to the wet as you fold in one direction. Do not overmix because you'll form a chewy cake.
  4. Add the batter into a loaf tin or two mini tins
  5. Bake into a preheated oven at 180°C for 40-45 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  6. Allow to cool in the tins for ten minutes then place onto a cooling rack until it cools completely. ENJOY.
