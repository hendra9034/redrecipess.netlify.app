The ingredients needed to make Scalloped potato casserole:
  1. Get 2 boxes scalloped potatoes plus ingredients to make each box ðŸ‘‡
  2. Prepare Milk, butter and boiling water follow box directions
  3. Get 1 lb lean ground beef
  4. Take 1/2 cup diced white onion
  5. Take 1 Tablespoon green bell pepper minced
  6. Make ready 1 Tablespoon red bell pepper minced
  7. Get 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  8. Take 1/2 teaspoon black Pepper
  9. Get 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  10. Prepare 1 (2.8 oz) pack bacon pieces
  11. Prepare 1 1/2 cups shredded chedder cheese
  12. Make ready 1/2 cup Italian or plain style panko bread crumbs

Instructions to make Scalloped potato casserole:
  1. Pre heat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Make scalloped potatoes according to per box. Boiling water, milk and butter and set aside
  3. Cook and crumble ground beef in a skillet and add in onion, peppers, salt, pepper and garlic powder. Cook till done then drain off grease.
  4. Add cooked ground beef to dish and mix in bacon pieces
  5. Pour on scalloped potatoes and mix up slightly
  6. Bake for 15 minutes
  7. Then top with cheese and bread crumbs and bake for 30 more minutes
