The ingredients needed to make Paneer potato cutlets:
  1. Make ready 250 grams Paneer
  2. Get 1 Onion chopped
  3. Take 2 Boiled potatoes, mashed
  4. Prepare 1 bowl Boiled Rice
  5. Make ready As per taste Salt
  6. Take 1 tsp White pepper
  7. Prepare 1 tsp Mango powder
  8. Prepare 1 tsp Ginger powder
  9. Make ready handful Coriander leaves
  10. Make ready 2 Green chillies,chopped
  11. Make ready 2 tbsp Flour
  12. Get some bread crumbs

Steps to make Paneer potato cutlets:
  1. Take a pan add mashed potato and rice mash paneer onion green chilli coriander leaves salt and white pepper powder, mango powder, ginger powder and mix well
  2. Shape it as per your choice.
  3. Take a bowl add 2 tbsp flour and add some water mix well and dip in cutlets and coat with bread crumbs and deep fry.
  4. Enjoy with your family.
