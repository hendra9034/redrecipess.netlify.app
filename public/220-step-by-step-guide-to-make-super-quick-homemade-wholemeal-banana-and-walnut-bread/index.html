The ingredients needed to make Wholemeal Banana and Walnut Bread:
  1. Take 1/4 cup hot water
  2. Prepare 1 teaspoon bicarbonate of soda (baking soda)
  3. Prepare 2 eggs
  4. Get 1/2 cup honey
  5. Prepare 1/3 coconut oil
  6. Get 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  7. Get 2 mashed bananas
  8. Get 1 3/4 cups wholemeal flour
  9. Take 1/2 teaspoon salt
  10. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  11. Take 1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom
  12. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
Instructions to make Wholemeal Banana and Walnut Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 165°C (330°F) and grease a loaf pan.
  2. Combine bicarbonate of soda with hot water and set aside. - - *Bicarbonate of Soda is Baking Soda for other parts of the world.
  3. Lightly beat eggs and mix in with coconut oil, honey, vanilla extract and banana.
  4. Add wholemeal flour, spices and salt to the wet mixture and combine bicarbonate of soda/water and make a smooth batter.
  5. Stir in the walnuts until evenly distributed.
  6. Pour into greased loaf pan and cook for 55 minutes at 165°C (330°F)
