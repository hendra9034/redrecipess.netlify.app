The ingredients needed to make Almond banana bread:
  1. Take 2-3 ripe bananas
  2. Prepare 3/4 cup brown sugar
  3. Make ready 1 stick butter
  4. Take 1 cup almond flour
  5. Make ready 1 cup all-purpose flour
  6. Make ready 1/2 tbsp salt
  7. Get 1 tbsp baking powder
  8. Prepare 1 bowl cherries (optional)
  9. Prepare 1 bowl almond (cut)
