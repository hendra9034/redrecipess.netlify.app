The ingredients needed to make Zucchini Bread:
  1. Take 3 Eggs
  2. Prepare 2 cup Zucchini, finely grated
  3. Get 1 tsp Salt
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp Baking powder
  5. Take 1 cup Oil
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp Vanilla
  7. Get 1 tsp Baking soda
  8. Get 1/2 cup Nuts, chopped (optional)
  9. Take 1 1/2 cup Sugar
  10. Get 3 cup Flour
  11. Take 1 tbsp Cinnamon
Steps to make Zucchini Bread:
  1. Beat eggs, add oil & sugar and mix well.
  2. Add zucchini and vanilla - mix.
  3. Sift together flour, salt, baking soda, cinnamon and baking powder. Add dry ingredients to egg/zucchini mixture.
  4. Add nuts, if desired.
  5. Bake in (2) greased bread pans at 325°F for 60-75 minutes until done.
