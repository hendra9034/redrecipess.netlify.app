Steps to make Bread lettuce roll with cream and pumpkin seeds Green chutney:
  1. Put all the ingredients of chutney in grinder jar(except lemon juice)and grind it.After grinding the chutney, add lemon juice.our healthy green chutney is ready.
  2. Take two pieces of whole grain bread and cut it's edges.
  3. Press it with a rolling pin.
  4. Put fresh cream on the top of the bread.
  5. Put Green Sauce of Pumpkin seeds over it.
  6. Take two lettuce and cut the hard portion of the leaves.After serving the sauce, place the lettuce on top of it.
  7. Repeat cream and sauce over it.
  8. Sprinkle black pepper powder over it.
  9. Roll it and cut it from the middle with a sharp knife.
  10. Keep it in a plate. Our healthy yummy bread lettuce roll is ready.Eat it with tomato ketchup.
