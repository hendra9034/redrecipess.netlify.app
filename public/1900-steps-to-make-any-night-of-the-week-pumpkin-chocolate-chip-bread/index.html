The ingredients needed to make pumpkin chocolate chip bread:
  1. Take 2 1/2 cup white flour
  2. Take 1 cup whole wheat flour
  3. Make ready 3 cup sugar
  4. Take 2 tsp baking soda
  5. Take 2 tsp cinnamon
  6. Take 1 tsp nutmeg
  7. Take 1 tsp salt
  8. Take 16 oz cook or canned pumpkin puree
  9. Get 1 cup oil
  10. Take 4 each eggs(beaten lightly)
  11. Get 2/3 cup water
  12. Prepare 1 cup semi sweet or milk chocolate chips

Instructions to make pumpkin chocolate chip bread:
  1. preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Grease 2-9x5 inch loaf pans or 3-8x4 inch loaf pans
  3. mix flours, sugar, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt together in a large bowl; set aside
  4. mix pumpkin, oil, eggs, and water together and stir into dry ingredients
  5. stir in chocolate chips
  6. place better into prepared pants. Bake 60 to 70 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean
