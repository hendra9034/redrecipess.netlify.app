The ingredients needed to make Banana Oats Bread:
  1. Make ready 1 and 1/2 cups flour
  2. Make ready 1 cup oats
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup sugar
  4. Make ready 1 teaspoon Baking soda
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon Baking powder
  6. Prepare 1 egg
  7. Get 3/4 cup Milk
  8. Get 1/3 cup oil
  9. Get 3 tablespoon cocoa powder
  10. Take 1/2 teaspoon salt
  11. Get Vanilla essence
  12. Get 1 cup smashed banana

Instructions to make Banana Oats Bread:
  1. Beat the egg, banana, sugar, oil and milk in a wide bowl.
  2. Mix the flour, oats, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
  3. Blend it with the egg mixture. Add the vanilla essence and mix it well. Add the cocoa powder and mix it well
  4. Transfer the batter into a greased baking pan. Bake it for 200°C for 15 to 20 minutes
