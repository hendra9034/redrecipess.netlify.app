The ingredients needed to make Banana Nut Bread:
  1. Prepare 2 cups self rising flour
  2. Get 1 cup brown sugar
  3. Get 2 eggs
  4. Make ready 1 tsp cinnamon
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup oil or shortening
  6. Prepare 2 tsp softened butter
  7. Prepare 1 cup rough chop walnuts
  8. Take Dash salt
  9. Get 3 overly ripe bananas
  10. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps to make Banana Nut Bread:
  1. Preheat oven 350° take eggs, vanilla, butter, brown sugar, oil, all wet ingredients and mix in large bowl
  2. In separate smallbowl add dry ingredients flour, salt, cinnamon
  3. Mash bananas and add to wet ingredients and using a hand mixer combine
  4. Pour dry ingredients into wet and stir till combined. Fold in walnuts at this point. Using a loaf pan sprayed or coated in shortening bake for 45 to 50 mins
