The ingredients needed to make Easy failproof banana bread:
  1. Make ready 2 cup self raising flour
  2. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  3. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  4. Take 1 tsp cinnamon(optional)
  5. Prepare 3 large very ripe bananas
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup sugar
  7. Get 2 eggs
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup melted butter or margarine
  9. Make ready pinch salt
  10. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  11. Take 1 tsp almond extract

Steps to make Easy failproof banana bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 160C
  2. In a bowl sift all dry ingredients except sugar and set aside
  3. In another bowl mash bananas until watery and add sugar and mix. Add eggs and mix again. Gradually add melted butter and essences.
  4. Make a well in the centre of dry ingredients. Pour in wet ingredients
  5. Gently fold in the mixture with a spatula until incorporated or batter is thick and chunky
  6. Put mixture into a greased and floured pan. Bake for 30-40 minutes until a toothpick inserted in centre comes out clean
  7. Remove from pan. Cool and enjoy!
