The ingredients needed to make Healthy Banana Bread:
  1. Take 1 1/4 cup flour
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup non packed brown sugar
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Take 1/2 tsp baking soda
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp baking powder
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup apple sauce
  8. Take 1/2 cup walnuts
  9. Prepare 1 large egg
  10. Take 2 medium egg whites
  11. Take 3 large bananas
  12. Get 1 cup uncooked old fashioned oats
  13. Prepare 3/4 cup ground flax seed

Steps to make Healthy Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350F. In a large bowl, stir together dry ingredients, then add applesauce and eggs.
  2. In a smaller bowl, mash the bananas. Add bananas, oats, and walnuts to other mixture.
  3. Spray loaf pan with cooking spray, pour batter into pan and bake until firm to the touch, about 45-55 minutes. Remove from oven and let stand for 5 minutes before flipping it out on a cooling rack.
