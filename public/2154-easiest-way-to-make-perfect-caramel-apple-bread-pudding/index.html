The ingredients needed to make Caramel Apple Bread Pudding:
  1. Get 1 cup Cinnamon or unsweetened applesauce
  2. Prepare 1 cup 2% milk
  3. Get 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  4. Get 1/2 cup egg substitute
  5. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  7. Prepare 5 cup cinnamon raisin bread torn in small pieces
  8. Make ready 3/4 cup granny smith apple peeled and diced small
  9. Take 1 Whipped cream and caramel topping for topping

Instructions to make Caramel Apple Bread Pudding:
  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. In a sprayed 9x13 pan, place bread pieces evenly.
  3. Combine first six ingredients well and pour over bread in pan.
  4. Evenly sprinkle apple over all and press down to wet all ingredients.
  5. Bake 40-50 min or until knife in center comes out clean.
  6. Serve warm with caramel drizzle and whipped cream.
