The ingredients needed to make THE BEST Banana Nut Bread!:
  1. Make ready 3 med to lg over-ripe bananas
  2. Get 1 1/2 cups flour
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  4. Make ready 1 egg
  5. Get 3/4 cups sugar
  6. Prepare 1 tbs ground cinnamon
  7. Get 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  8. Get 1/4 tsp baking soda
  9. Make ready 4 whole shelled walnuts, crushed
Instructions to make THE BEST Banana Nut Bread!:
  1. Preheat oven to 350° and grease loaf baking dish
  2. Peel bananas and place in large mixing bowl. Mash bananas using a fork until smooth.
  3. Add remaining ingredients and whisk together until well-combined
  4. Spoon into greased baking dish and place in oven for 50-55 minutes.
  5. Remove from oven and let sit for 10 minutes in baking dish.
  6. Using a butter knife, run the knife along all sides to loosen from baking dish. Turn dish upside down on a cooling rack and let cool for at least 20 minutes.
  7. Enjoy!
