The ingredients needed to make Easy banana bread:
  1. Take 2-3 very ripe bananas
  2. Get 1/3 cup melted butter
  3. Make ready 1 teaspoon baking soda
  4. Prepare Pinch salt
  5. Make ready 3/4 cup sugar
  6. Make ready 1 large egg, beaten
  7. Prepare 1 1/2 cup flour
  8. Take 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  9. Prepare (Optional) chocolate chips

Instructions to make Easy banana bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350, and butter a 4x8 loaf pan
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash bananas and mix in melted butter
  3. Mix in baking soda, salt
  4. Stir in sugar, egg and, vanilla
  5. Mix in flour and chocolate chips (optional)
  6. Pour into loaf pan and bake for 50-60 minutes!
