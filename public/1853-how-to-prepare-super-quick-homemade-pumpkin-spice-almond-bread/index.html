Instructions to make Pumpkin Spice Almond Bread:
  1. preheat oven to 350
  2. add flour, salt & both baking soda & powder to a bowl mix well set aside.
  3. add eggs, sugar, extract and mix well. add in pumpkin spice, pumpkin puree, butter & flour mixture mix well. Now incorporate almonds
  4. spray well your pumpkin mold with cooking spray. add even amounts of mix to both molds bake for 50-60 minutes.
  5. frost as desired. .I used the ganach heating cream till just comes to a simmer pour over chips stir till combined and smooth & spread in middle to hold together 2 pieces. let set in fridge to firm. reheated same procedure and poured on top of pumpkin, used sprinkles to cover as you like.set in fridge to set
