The ingredients needed to make Zucchini Bread:
  1. Take 1 1/2 cup flour
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp baking power
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking soda
  4. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
  6. Make ready 2 eggs
  7. Prepare 1 1/4 cup splenda
  8. Take 1/3 cup oil
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup applesauce
  10. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla
  11. Prepare 1 cup shredded zucchini

Instructions to make Zucchini Bread:
  1. preheat oven 350°
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, baking powder,salt, & cinnamon
  3. Set aside. Beat eggs & splenda on Med speed add oil, applesauce, & vanilla beat Med speed 1 min

  6. bake for 40 min
