Steps to make Zucchini Bread:
  1. Mix all dry ingredients together in a large mixing bowl (kitchen aide bowl).
  2. Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients. Whisk in moist ingredients. Stir until just moistened.
  3. Add zucchini and walnuts. Stir thoroughly. Make sure to scrape the very bottom of the bowl with spatula, as dry ingredients stick to it often.
  4. Spread into greased 9 x 5 x 3 loaf pan.
  5. Bake at 350 for 1 hour and 15 min. Check first at 1 hour. If knife comes out clean, it is done.
