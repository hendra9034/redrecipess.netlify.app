Steps to make Berry bread pudding:
  1. Combine berries & syrup in saucepan
  2. Reduce until starting to thicken/bubble
  3. Separate solid & liquid, cool down in fridge/freezer
  4. Cut bread to fit muffin tray (4 bases, 4 lids), grease tray
  5. Soak bread (bases) in liquid and put into tray
  6. Spoon solid into bases
  7. Soak bread (lids) in liquid and cover filling
  8. Cover tray with board, weigh down and refrigerate overnight
  9. Turn pudding out of tray, serve with ice cream or custard
  10. 205 calories per serve
