The ingredients needed to make Fruit Muesli German Country-Style Bread:
  1. Prepare 80 grams ○Muesli
  2. Get 25 grams ○Milk
  3. Make ready 160 grams ●Whole wheat flour
  4. Get 85 grams ●Bread (strong) flour
  5. Prepare 13 grams ●Raw cane sugar
  6. Take 5 grams ●Salt
  7. Make ready 3 grams ●Dry yeast
  8. Get 13 grams Shortening
  9. Make ready 175 grams Water (cold)
  10. Make ready 1 Unsalted butter for proofing bowl
  11. Prepare 1 Muesli for proofing bowl
  12. Prepare 1 Olive oil for scoring

Instructions to make Fruit Muesli German Country-Style Bread:
  1. In a preparation bowl, mix together the muesli and milk. Leave the muesli to soak up the moisture for 10 minutes.
  2. In another bowl, add whole wheat flour, bread flour, raw cane sugar, salt, and dry yeast and mix with a whisk while incorporating some air.
  3. Coat a 18 cm bowl with a thick coat of butter for proofing.
  4. Add muesli to the proofing bowl, and turn the bowl around to let the muesli stick to the sides.
  5. Add the mixture from Step 1, Step 2 and water in a home baking machine. Start the dough function. Add shortening after 5 minutes.
  6. When finished, de-gas lightly and rest the dough for 15 minutes.
  7. After resting, flatten out the dough into 15 cm rounds by pounding with your hand.
  8. Roll up the dough into a ball, and seal the bottom tightly.
  9. Put the dough in the bowl from Step 4, seam side up. Let it rise until it doubles in size
  10. Start preheating the oven to 220℃ at the right moment.
  11. When the proofing is done, smooth out the dough by pushing with the palm of your hand.
  12. Flip the dough out onto a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
  13. Score the dough 5mm deep.
  14. Pour a little bit of olive oil into the cuts. Put it in the oven, lower the oven temperature to 190℃, and bake for 25 minutes.
  15. When done, cool on a cooling rack.
  16. The finished bread is moist and a little dense. I like to spread some butter and enjoy with a cup of cafe au lait.
