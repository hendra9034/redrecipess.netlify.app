The ingredients needed to make Gluten Free Vegan Corn Bread:
  1. Get 1/2 cup Corn meal
  2. Take 1/2 cup Gluten free flour
  3. Make ready 4 tbsp Sugar
  4. Make ready 1/4 tsp Xanthum gum
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp Baking powder
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp Salt
  7. Take 1 cup Non dairy milk
  8. Take 1 Flax seed egg or egg alternative
  9. Take 4 tbsp Oil
