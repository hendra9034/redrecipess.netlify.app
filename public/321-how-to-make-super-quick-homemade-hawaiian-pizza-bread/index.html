Instructions to make Hawaiian pizza bread:
  1. Arrange bread on a baking pan lined with baking paper. Grease
  2. Spread marinara sauce on the bread
  3. Add grated mozzarella cheese
  4. Then add your sliced ham and pineapples
  5. Add the green bell peppers
  6. More mozzarella cheese!
  7. Bake until cheese melts
  8. Serve warm
