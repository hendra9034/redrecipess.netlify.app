Steps to make Chocolate chip pumpkin bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Mix all ingredients together in except chocolate chips and nuts in a large bowl (4 quart or larger) until mixture is no longer clumpy.
  3. Stir in chocolate chips and nuts.
  4. Grease and flour 6 mini loaf pans or 3 - 9x5 loaf pans and divide mixture into each pan.
  5. Bake in oven for 45 to 60 min or until toothpick comes out clean.
