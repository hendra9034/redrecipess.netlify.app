Steps to make bread pudding:
  1. preheat oven to 350°f
  2. get a 13 x 9 pan
  3. butter 4 slices of bread (appearantly mcgavins bread is best but use what u wish) on both sides and lay on bottom of pan. leave bread whole but can tear pieces and move them to make fit. make sure bottom has little to no open spaces
  4. rinse raisins, then put in bowl with water and put in microwave for 2 and a half minutes, drain
  5. put raisins on top of bread in pan spreading them evenly
  6. butter rest of bread on both sides and cover over raisins
  7. put milk in microwave and heat until a film appears on top. while its heating beat eggs with hand mixer on high for 1 minute
  8. once the milk is done take 1 cup of it away leaving 4 cups, set aside
  9. mix eggs and sugar into the 4 cups of milk
  10. pour egg, milk and sugar mixture over bread in pan
  11. now pour the 1 cup milk u set aside ontop
  12. sprinkle top with as much cinnamon as u like
  13. put in oven and bake for 1 hour
  14. after baking let sit to cool slightly. once ready u serve by flipping a desired piece upside down on plate
  15. add half and half cream if desired
