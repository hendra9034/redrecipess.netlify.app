The ingredients needed to make Gluten free Sloppy Joes:
  1. Take of lean ground hamburger meat
  2. Get of finely chopped onion
  3. Take 14 oz can of tomato sauce
  4. Prepare of ketchup
  5. Make ready of mustard
  6. Take gluten free hamburger buns or gluten free slices of bread

Steps to make Gluten free Sloppy Joes:
  1. in the frying pan cook the beef and onion on medium high heat until the beef is brown. wash the hamburger with hot water to drain the fat off.
  2. return pan to heat and add tomatoes, ketchup, and dry mustard. stir well.
  3. turn the heat to low and simmer for about 15 minutes or until the liquid is absorbed.
  4. serve on gluten free hamburger buns or bread.
