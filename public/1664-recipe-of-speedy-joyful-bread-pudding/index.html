Steps to make Joyful bread pudding:
  1. Preheat oven 400
  2. Crumble day old wheat bread slices
  3. Smash bananas by hand
  4. Mix crumbles and bananas together til mushy mixed
  5. Sprinkle cranraisins over loaf
  6. Add 3 dollops of butter on top
  7. Trangle cheese and lay over on top
  8. Cook approximately 25 min
