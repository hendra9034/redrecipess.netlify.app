The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread Muffins:
  1. Get 4 over ripe bananas
  2. Get 2 eggs (beat)
  3. Prepare 4 tablespoons melted butter
  4. Get 1/2 tsp vanilla
  5. Take Dry Ingredients
  6. Take 1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  7. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup brown sugar
  9. Get 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  10. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  11. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  12. Take 1 tsp cinnamon

Steps to make Banana Bread Muffins:
  1. Start oven to 345 degrees
  2. Mash bananas (leave some chunks), then mix in beat eggs, melted butter, brown sugar and granulated sugar, after combined mix in vanilla
  3. In another bowl mix all dry ingredients today
  4. Mix dry ingredients into wet ingredients, just until we’ll combined do not over mix
  5. Put liners in muffin pan, spray lightly with canola oil (any pam is fine), then using a 1/4 measuring cup measure out 1/4 mix into each liner
  6. Bake for 25 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean
