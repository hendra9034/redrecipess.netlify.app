The ingredients needed to make Dhakaya Jali Kebab:
  1. Prepare 500 grams minced beef / lamb
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp Tomato sauce
  3. Make ready 1/2 tbsp Cinnamon powder
  4. Get 1/2 tbsp White pepper powder
  5. Make ready 1/2 tbsp Cardamom powder
  6. Get 1/4 tbsp Mace powder
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp Ginger paste
  8. Take 1 tbsp Garlic paste
  9. Take 1/2 tbsp Red chili powder
  10. Get 2 tbsp Onion chopped
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp Coriander leaf
  12. Make ready 1/2 tbsp or more Green chili chopped
  13. Prepare 1 slice bread soaked in water and drained
  14. Prepare to taste Salt
  15. Make ready 1 egg
  16. Make ready 2 tbsp Biscuit crumbs
  17. Make ready For dipping & frying
  18. Make ready 2-3 eggs beaten
  19. Prepare Bread crumbs
  20. Prepare Oil
Steps to make Dhakaya Jali Kebab:
  1. Mix everything well in a bowl.
  2. Make small ball and then flatten like a thick cutlet with hands.
  3. Fry the kebab chop / cutlet in deep oil. Before dropping the cutlet in the oil lightly soak the cutlets in beaten egg.
