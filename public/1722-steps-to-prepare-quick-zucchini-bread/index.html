The ingredients needed to make Zucchini Bread:
  1. Prepare 2 cups sugar
  2. Take 3 eggs
  3. Take 2 tsp vanilla
  4. Take 1 cup vegetable oil
  5. Make ready 2 cups shredded and drained zucchini
  6. Prepare 1 cup crushed pineapple
  7. Take 3/4 tsp nutmeg
  8. Take 1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  9. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  10. Make ready 2 tsp baking soda
  11. Take 3 cups flour
  12. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  13. Prepare 1 cup chopped pecans
  14. Make ready 1 cup raisens
Instructions to make Zucchini Bread:
  1. Beat together in a large mixing bowl, sugar, eggs, vegetable oil and vanilla.
  2. Add zucchini and crushed pineapple and beat.
  3. Add nutmeg, cinnamon, baking powder, baking soda, flour and salt and beat.
  4. Mix in chopped pecans and raisins.
  5. Grease 2 loaf pans. Bake loaves at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
  6. Remove after 5 minutes from loaf pan and let cool on a baking rack.
