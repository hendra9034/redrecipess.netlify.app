The ingredients needed to make Strawberry banana bread:
  1. Prepare 3 ripe bananas
  2. Prepare 2 eggs
  3. Make ready 2 tsp baking powder
  4. Get 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  5. Get 1 1/4 cup almond meal
  6. Prepare 1/4 tsp baking soda
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup strawberries
  8. Take coconut oil (to grease loaf pan)
  9. Make ready 1/4 tsp cinnamon

Steps to make Strawberry banana bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and grease loaf pan with coconut oil
  2. Combine the baking powder, almond meal, strawberries, baking soda, cinnamon, bananas and eggs and mix well.
  3. Add chopped pecans, mix and pour into the loaf pan.
  4. Bake for 45 min or until toothpick comes out dry
  5. Let bread sit for 10 min and remove to completely cool.
