The ingredients needed to make Peggys Bread Pudding:
  1. Prepare No fail pudding base
  2. Take 1 cup cream
  3. Take 1 cup packed dark brown sugar
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  5. Take 1 dash cinnamon
  6. Make ready 1/4 cup water
  7. Get 1 stick of butter at room temperature
  8. Prepare 7 egg yolks
  9. Get my stale bread mix
  10. Make ready 4 day old donuts
  11. Get 4 day old wheat rolls
  12. Take 4 large day old cinnamon rolls
Instructions to make Peggys Bread Pudding:
  1. preheat oven to 375
  2. grease and flour bunt cake pan or 9 by 13 pan
  3. cut up the stale bread into one inch cubes and set aside
  4. in a blender add all of the no fail pudding ingredients and liquify
  5. pour liquid over bread cubes and toss lightly
  6. poor evenly into pan and bake for 35 to 45 minutes
  7. as long as you use the no fail pudding mix you are free to choose any types of bread or leftover donuts ,cinnamon rolls, sweet rolls, stale light bread mix it up and make it your own
  8. I learned to make the pudding base by watching my grandma so I dedicated the recipe to her… that's why I called at Peggy's bread pudding
