The ingredients needed to make Monkey Bread:
  1. Get tubes of pilsbury biscuits
  2. Prepare melted butter or marg (either works)
  3. Take syrup (pancake or maple)
  4. Take packed brown sugar
  5. Get Cinnamon
  6. Take chopped walnuts (optional)
  7. Take raisins (optional)

Instructions to make Monkey Bread:
  1. combine melted "butter" and syrup in small bowl and set aside.
  2. In another bowl combine Brown Sugar and cinnamon. (the nuts and/or raisins can be added into this if desired)
  3. prepare a bundt pan or angel food pan and spray it with a non stick spray.
  4. take approximately half the syrup mixture, and pour that into the pan and sprinkle half the brown sugar mixture in the pan as well
  5. Lay the biscuits on bottom of pan, overlaping edges (close together). to form the ring.
