The ingredients needed to make Healthy Microwaved banana oat bread:
  1. Take 3 tablespoons homemade oat flour
  2. Take 1/3 of a large very ripe banana
  3. Prepare 1-2 Teaspoons peanut butter
  4. Get 1/4 teaspoons baking powder
  5. Prepare 1 T raisin or chocolate chips
  6. Make ready 1 pinch salt
  7. Prepare 1 tablespoon milk or soymilk
  8. Make ready Optional: some blueberries to put on top

Instructions to make Healthy Microwaved banana oat bread:
  1. In medium sized mug, mash the banana very well
  2. Stir in the peanut butter with a fork
  3. Stir in the milk
  4. Add the oat flour, baking powder, and salt
  5. Briefly mix the dry ingredients together with a fork to disperse the salt snd baking powder
  6. Fold the mixture until a thick flowing batter forms
  7. Stir in the raisins or chocolate chips
  8. Top the bread with blueberries or more chocolate chips or raisins
  9. Microwave for 1 minute and 30-40 seconds in a 1200 watts microwave or until the center of the bread is no longer wet and tacky
  10. Too the cooked bread with slices of banana
  11. Enjoy!!!!
