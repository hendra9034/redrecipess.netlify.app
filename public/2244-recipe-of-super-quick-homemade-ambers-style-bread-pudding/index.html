Steps to make Ambers Style Bread Pudding:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°f.
  2. Cook the 2 boxes of Vanilla pudding together, as directed on the box.
  3. Butter a dish (of your choice)
  4. Cut or break the sweet Hawaiian rolls into bite-size pieces & put in the buttered dish.
  5. Mix the milk & sugar then dribble over the bread. Toss slightly, making sure bread is evenly covered.
  6. Pour the vanilla pudding over the bread, covering evenly.
  7. Bake for about 30minutes or until the top has a golden color.
