The ingredients needed to make EASY banana bread (nut free):
  1. Take 4 extra ripe bananas
  2. Make ready 1/3 cup milk
  3. Take 2/3 cup sugar (you can use brown sugar for a richer darker banana bread)
  4. Get 3 eggs
  5. Make ready 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  6. Prepare 2 and 2/3 cup bisquick
  7. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

Steps to make EASY banana bread (nut free):
  1. Mash bananas with a fork and mix in eggs, vanilla, milk and oil
  2. Incorporate the bisquick into the wet mixture and pour in a greased loaf pan. Bake 55-60 minutes or until toothpick comes out mostly clean.
