The ingredients needed to make Delicious Cast Iron Banana Bread:
  1. Take 1 stick margarine or butter
  2. Take 2 eggs
  3. Get 1 1/2 cup sugar
  4. Prepare 3 medium banana
  5. Get 1 tsp vanilla
  6. Get 1 1/2 cup self-rising flour
  7. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  8. Prepare 1 tsp nutmeg
  9. Get 3/4 cup chopped walnuts(optional)
  10. Prepare 1/4 cup semisweet chocolate chips(optional)
Instructions to make Delicious Cast Iron Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 325°F. In a large mixing bowl mix butter, eggs, sugar, bananas, vanilla, flour, nutmeg, and baking soda
  2. Pour batter into 2 greased cast iron loaf pans.
  3. Add chocolate chips or nuts and gently stir. I only put the chips in one.
  4. Bake for 1 hour. Will be nice and brown.
  5. Cool for 10 to 15 minutes and remove bread from loaf pans. Wrap in aluminum foil. Will keep in refrigerator for a week or two.
