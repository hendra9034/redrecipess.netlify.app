The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread:
  1. Prepare 3/4 bananas
  2. Get 4 eggs
  3. Prepare 2 scoops peanut butter
  4. Make ready 2 scoops honey
  5. Prepare 2 cups gluten free flour
  6. Make ready 1 spoon baking powder
  7. Get 1 spoon vanilla extract
  8. Make ready 1/3 cup walnuts
  9. Prepare 2 spoons sweet chocolate powder
  10. Take 1/2 spoon salt

Steps to make Banana Bread:
  1. Pre heat oven 170C
  2. Smash the bananas very well and when they are ready, mix them with the eggs, honey and peanut butter
  3. Add the flour, baking powder, salt & the chocolate powder. Mix well and add the walnuts & mix again.
  4. Add the mixture into the mold and heat it for 50-55 min. Enjoy üòç
