The ingredients needed to make Banana Nut Bread:
  1. Get 5 tbsp butter
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup sugar or sugar substitute
  3. Take 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  4. Get 1 egg
  5. Take 3 tsp egg replacement powder
  6. Get 4 tbsp water
  7. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
  8. Take 1 1/2 cup mashed, very ripe bananas
  9. Get 1 3/4 cup Rice flour (or flour of your choice)
  10. Get 1/2 tsp xanthum gum (if using regular all purpose flour don't need this ingredient)
  11. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  12. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  13. Prepare 1/4 tsp baking powder
  14. Prepare 1/2 cup heavy cream
  15. Get 1/3 cup chopped walnuts

Instructions to make Banana Nut Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Spray bottom only of 9x5x3 loaf pan with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. Beat butter in large bowl with an electric mixer set @ medium speed until light & fluffy. Add sugars. Beat well. Add egg. Mix well.
  3. In small bowl, mix egg replacement & water together well. (Could replace these 2 ingredients with 2 egg whites.) Add to other mixture. Then add vanilla. Mix well.
  4. Add mashed banana, & beat on high speak 30 seconds.
  5. Combine flour, xanthum gum, baking soda, salt, & baking powder in medium bowl. Add flour mixture alternately with cream, ending with flour mixture. Add walnuts to batter, mix well.
  6. Pour batter evenly into prepared loaf pan. Bake 1hr 15 minutes, or until browned & toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
  7. Cool bread in pan on wire rack 10 minutes. Remove bread from pan; cool completely on wire rack.
