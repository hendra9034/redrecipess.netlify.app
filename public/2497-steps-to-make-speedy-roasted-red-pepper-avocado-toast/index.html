Instructions to make Roasted Red Pepper Avocado Toast:
  1. Drizzle the bread with olive oil
  2. Toast the bread until golden brown
  3. Meanwhile, slice the red pepper thin and halve the avocado
  4. Slice the avocado within the skin
  5. Place the toasted bread on a plate. Arrange the red pepper on the bread. Then, with your finger separate the avocado from it's skin and arrange slices on the red pepper.
  6. Sprinkle with salt, herbes de provence, and parsley leaves
