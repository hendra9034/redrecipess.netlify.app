The ingredients needed to make Stuffed Bell Peppers:
  1. Prepare (1) Onion, diced
  2. Take (5) Garlic cloves, minced
  3. Make ready (5) Tbsp chili powder
  4. Prepare (2) Tbsp vegetable oil
  5. Take (4) Large Bell Peppers, mixed colors
  6. Get (2) slices White sandwich bread, torn into pieces
  7. Get (1/2) cup milk
  8. Make ready (1) lb ground chuck, browned
  9. Get (1) cup corn, can or frozen
  10. Take (2) cups shredded cheese, Jack and Colby
  11. Get (1 1/2) cup cooked rice
  12. Take (2) Tbsp minced canned chipotle chilie in adobo sauce
  13. Prepare (3) tsp salt
  14. Get (1) tsp pepper
  15. Get (6) Tbsp minced fresh cilantro
  16. Take (1) Green onion, chopped
Instructions to make Stuffed Bell Peppers:
  1. Microwave onion, garlic, chili powder, and oil in a bowl, stirring occasionally, until onion is softened, about 3 minutes.
  2. Cut top 1/2 inch off each pepper, removing core and seeds.
  3. Save 3 of the pepper tops. Chop finely, discarding stems.
  4. Mash bread and milk into paste in large bowl.
  5. Mix in onion mixture, chopped pepper tops, ground chuck, corn, 1 cup cheese, cooked rice, chipotle, salt & pepper.
  6. Mix together with gloved hands until well incorporated.
  7. Stuff meat mixture into peppers
  8. Place 1/3 cup water in slow cooker.
  9. Place stuffed peppers upright in cooker.
  10. Cover and cook 4-6 hours on LOW until fully cooked.
  11. Add additional cheese and green onion to the top the last hour or so of cooking.
  12. Gently lift peppers from cooker with tongs or slotted spoon, and transfer to a serving plate.
  13. Enjoy
