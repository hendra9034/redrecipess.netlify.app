The ingredients needed to make Zucchini Bread:
  1. Take 1 cup Oil
  2. Prepare 2 cup Sugar
  3. Make ready 3 Eggs
  4. Make ready 2 cup Grated raw Zucchini
  5. Take 3 cup Flour
  6. Prepare 3 tsp Cinnamon
  7. Prepare 1 tsp Baking Soda
  8. Take 1/2 tsp Baking Powder

  5. Pour into 2 greased bread pans
