Steps to make Bread pudding 2:
  1. Preheat oven 350.
  2. Break bread into sm. Pieces into 8 in pan. Drizzle melted butter over all and sprinkle w/raisins.
  3. In bowl mix eggs, milk, sugar, cinnamon and vanilla. Mix well. Pour over bread and push down w/fork til bread soaks up mixture.
