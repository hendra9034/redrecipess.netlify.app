The ingredients needed to make Peppered Beef, Eggs, and Cheese Sandwich:
  1. Make ready 2 tablespoons butter
  2. Take 2 large eggs
  3. Prepare 1/3 pound sliced beef loin
  4. Get 1/4 pound seriously sharp Vermont white cheddar cheese
  5. Get 3 slices bread
  6. Make ready 1-1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  7. Make ready To taste salt
  8. Make ready 3 teaspoon mayonnaise divided
Steps to make Peppered Beef, Eggs, and Cheese Sandwich:
  1. Toast the bread. Beat 2 eggs separately. Slice the beef loin very thin.
  2. Brown the butter fry the eggs each separately. Now season the beef and lay seasoned side down. Then season the top as it fries. Add mayonnaise to the three slices of bread. Add the beef, and cheese.
  3. Add eggs on top of the cheese. Now stack the sandwich. Serve I hope you enjoy!!!
