The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon Bun Bread Pudding:
  1. Get 1 egg
  2. Get 1 1/4 cup milk
  3. Take 2 not so fresh cinnamon buns (cuz the fresh ones r too good, to not b eaten)
  4. Get 1 cinnamon & brown sugar, if u want a little more flavour
  5. Get 1 butter
Instructions to make Cinnamon Bun Bread Pudding:
  1. preheat oven to 350
  2. cut cinnamon buns into small pieces & put into buttered loaf pan
  3. whisk egg & milk together adding sugar & cinnamon & pour over pan
  4. bake for approx 30 mins
  5. Last & most important step, Enjoy
