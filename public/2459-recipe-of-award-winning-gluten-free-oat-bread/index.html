Steps to make Gluten free Oat Bread:
  1. Pre heat your oven at 180 degrees Celsius
  2. In a bowl, mix all the dry ingredients together.
  3. In a separate container mix milk and eggs
  4. Add the mixture to the dry ingredients and fold gently. Do not over mix.
  5. Pour the batter in a lined baking pan and add some rolled oats on top for decoration purposes.
  6. Bake for 30-40 minutes until a fork goes through and comes out clean.
