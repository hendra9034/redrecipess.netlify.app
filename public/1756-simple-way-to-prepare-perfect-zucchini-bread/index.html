The ingredients needed to make Zucchini Bread:
  1. Make ready 3 cups flour
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 cups sugar
  3. Get 3 teaspoon cinnamon
  4. Get 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  5. Get 1 teaspoon baking soda
  6. Take 3 eggs
  7. Prepare 2 cups grated zucchini
  8. Get 1 cup oil
  9. Take 3 teaspoon vanilla
  10. Prepare 1 cup walnuts (optional)
Instructions to make Zucchini Bread:
  1. Beat eggs. Add oil, zucchini, vanilla and mix well.
  2. Add remaining ingredients and mix well.
  3. Grease and flour 2 bread pans unless you are using Pam spray.
  4. Fill both bread pans evenly.
  5. Bake at 325° for 1 hour or toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Take bread out of pan and cool.
