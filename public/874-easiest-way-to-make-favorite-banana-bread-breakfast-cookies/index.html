The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread Breakfast Cookies:
  1. Take 2 large spotty bananas, mashed
  2. Make ready 1 1/4 cup quick cooking oats
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup old fashioned rolled oats
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon
  5. Take 1/2 cup coarsely chopped walnuts or pecans
Steps to make Banana Bread Breakfast Cookies:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Line a large baking tray with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat and set it aside. In a large bowl, mix all of the ingredients together until well combined.
  2. Scoop them onto the prepared baking tray, then flatten and shape them into cookies (these won't spread in the oven, so you can place them fairly close together). Bake for 12-14 minutes, until they begin to turn golden brown and are set.
  3. Let them cool completely on a wire rack. To store, keep them in an airtight container for 3-4 days.
