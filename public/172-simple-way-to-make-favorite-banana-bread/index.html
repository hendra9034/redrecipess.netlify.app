The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread:
  1. Make ready 6 very ripe bananas
  2. Make ready 1 cup melted butter
  3. Get 2 teaspoon baking soda
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp honey
  5. Get 1/2 teaspoon salt
  6. Make ready 1 1/4 cup brown sugar
  7. Take 3 large egg beaten
  8. Prepare 3 teaspoon vanilla extract
  9. Take 3 cup white flour

Instructions to make Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 and butter a 4x8 loaf pan
  2. Put all ingredients in a blender and mix until all ingredients are in liquid form
  3. Put into loaf pan and bake for 50-60 minutes
  4. Take out and let it cool. Serve and enjoy!
