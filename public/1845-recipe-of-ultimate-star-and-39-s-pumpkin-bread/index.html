The ingredients needed to make Star's Pumpkin Bread:
  1. Take 3 cup all-purpose flour
  2. Take 3 cup sugar
  3. Make ready 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  4. Make ready 2 tsp baking soda
  5. Take 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  6. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp salt
  7. Prepare 1 cup canola oil
  8. Take 3/4 cup water
  9. Take 2 cup pumpkin
  10. Make ready 4 eggs
  11. Take 1 cup chopped nuts (as desired)

Instructions to make Star's Pumpkin Bread:
  1. Mix dry ingredients in large bowl set aside
  2. In separate bowl mix wet ingredients on low until well blended about 1 minute
  3. Fold wet ingredients into dry ingredients a little at a time
  4. Then beat on low for 5 minutes until all ingredients are combined well.
  5. Pour into two regular sized loaf pans and two mini loaf pans, bake at '350f for 40-45 minutes
  6. Be sure to check the loaves after 40 minutes as the smaller loaves will bake faster and are usually done.
