Steps to make Redd's whole wheat apple bread:
  1. Preheat oven 375°
  2. Finely chop apple
  3. Whisk together dry ingredients and form well in center
  4. Pour beer into well and use fork to press edges of flour into the beer
  5. Continue until a ragged looking dough forms and add chopped apple
  6. Take dough with your hands and make a loose ball, then knead about 15 times
  7. Form loaf on buttered and lightly floured baking sheet, or if you wish a bread pan, and let sit for 15 min
  8. Put in oven for 45 min until the top has goldened
  9. Cool for 10 - 15 and butter the top
  10. Enjoy
