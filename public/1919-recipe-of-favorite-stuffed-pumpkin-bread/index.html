Steps to make Stuffed Pumpkin Bread:
  1. In a cup take warm milk,add sugar and yeast.Mix and cover and leave for 5 minutes.
  2. In a large bowl,take all-purpose flour.Add salt and half egg.Preserve half egg for egg -wash.Mix and make a well in the centre.
  3. Add yeast mix to the flour.
  4. Mix and knead to form a soft dough with the help of warm water.
  5. Knead for 5 minutes,tuck and put in the bowl.Cover and keep the bowl in a warm place for 80-90 minutes.
  6. After 90 minutes take out dough.Knead again for 10 minutes.Divide the dough into 3 equall parts.
  7. Flatten each ball,put a cube of cottage cheese in the centre of dough.Roll with hands to form a ball,seal properly.
  8. Now tie each ball with a cotton string to form a pumpkin like structure. Put the balls in a baking tray.Cover with a damp cloth.Rest for 10 minutes.
  9. Meanwhile preheat an oven at 180 °c for 10 minutes.Now balls are risen a little.Give an egg wash to them.
  10. Bake at 180°c for 20 -25 minutes.I've baked for 22 minutes.
  11. Chech and take out the bread and cool on a wire rack.
  12. When cool,cut and remove the string.
  13. Serve hot.
