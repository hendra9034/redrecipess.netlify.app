The ingredients needed to make easy bisquick banana bread:
  1. Get 2 1/3 cup bisquick
  2. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  3. Prepare 1/3 cup veg. oil
  4. Prepare 3 eggs
  5. Make ready 3 large bananas
  6. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla
  7. Take 3/4 cup chopped nuts
