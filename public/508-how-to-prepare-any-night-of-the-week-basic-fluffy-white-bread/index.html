The ingredients needed to make Basic fluffy white bread:
  1. Make ready 2 packages red star yeast
  2. Make ready 2 cup water
  3. Make ready 2 cup dry milk powder
  4. Take 1/2 cup oil
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp semolina
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp salt
  8. Take 3 cup all purpose flour
  9. Take 1 tbsp wheat protein

Steps to make Basic fluffy white bread:
  1. Dissolve yeast in 1/2 cup warm water.
  2. Combine milk, remaining water, oil, sugar, and half of the flour in a mixing bowl.
  3. Mix in the yeast and water proof
  4. Knead in remaining flour and knead for 6 to 8 minutes
  5. Let rise 1 hour
  6. Punch down and roll out into 2 12x8 inch rectangle.
  7. Roll firmly into a log from the short end. Place on pan seam side down.
  8. Key rise another hour
  9. Bake 375° f for 30 to 35 minutes
