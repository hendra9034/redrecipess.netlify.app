The ingredients needed to make Simple veg sandwich:
  1. Get 4 bread slices, brown / white bread
  2. Make ready 1/2 capsicum
  3. Get 1/2 cup corn
  4. Get 1 tsp crushed pepper
  5. Get 1/2 tsp mixed herbs, optional
  6. Prepare to taste salt
  7. Take 2 tbsp tomato sauce
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp green chutney
  9. Take 1/4 cup mozzarella or cheddar cheese, grated or cheese slices
  10. Prepare 2 tsp butter
