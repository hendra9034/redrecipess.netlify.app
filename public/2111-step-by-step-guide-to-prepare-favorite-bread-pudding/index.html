Instructions to make Bread Pudding:
  1. Preheat oven to 325. Grease a large casserole dish with butter.
  2. Cut bagels into cubes and place in a large bowl.
  3. In another large bowl combine the milk, white sugar, eggs, raisins, vanilla and salt and whisk well to combine.
