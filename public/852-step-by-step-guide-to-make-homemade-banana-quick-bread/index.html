The ingredients needed to make Banana Quick Bread:
  1. Prepare 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  2. Take 1/2 cup honey
  3. Take 2 eggs
  4. Take 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  5. Take 1/4 cup milk
  6. Get 1 tsp baking SODA
  7. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  8. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  10. Get 1 3/4 cup flour
Instructions to make Banana Quick Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 325 F, grease 9x5 loaf pan
  2. In large bowl, whisk together oil and honey
  3. Add eggs, whisk
  4. Add bananas and milk, whisk
  5. Add baking soda, vanilla, cinnamon, salt. Whisk.
  6. With large spoon, incorporate flour.
  7. Pour into pan. Knock on counter to settle.
  8. Sprinkle with cinnamon.
  9. Bake for 55 minutes. Check with cake tester.
  10. Let cool in pan for 10 minutes.
  11. Remove from pan. Let cool on counter 20 minutes.
  12. Slice and serve.
