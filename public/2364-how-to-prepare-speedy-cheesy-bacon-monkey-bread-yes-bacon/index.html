The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Bacon Monkey Bread! YES BACON!!!:
  1. Make ready of bacon, cooked and crumbled
  2. Prepare grated parmesan cheese
  3. Take onion, finely chopped (i just used the dry minced stuff)
  4. Make ready 10oz cans refrigerated biscuits
  5. Get melted butter
  6. Get any type of cubed cheese you prefer
Instructions to make Cheesy Bacon Monkey Bread! YES BACON!!!:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a 12 cup bundt pan with cooking spray.
  2. In a med bowl combine the bacon, parmesan cheese and onion. Mix well and set aside.
  3. I didn't put cheese in each of my biscuits, I just did every other biscuit. So for the ones with cheese inside just press the biscuit out with your hands, put cheese inside and wrap up tightly. With the other biscuits I just cut into fourths. Do can by can so its cut into thirds. Dip each piece of biscuit into melted butter, put first layer into pan and sprinkle with half the bacon mixture. Repeat this process with other two cans of biscuits, ending with the biscuits on top. I also sprinkled some garlic powder on top for extra flavor.
  4. Bake for 35-40 min or until golden Brown. Remove from oven and let cool 10 min. After cooling invert onto a serving platter and serve warm. Great with soup or any meal for that matter. Or just eat like is!!
