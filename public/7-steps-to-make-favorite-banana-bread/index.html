The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread:
  1. Get 3 ripe bananas
  2. Make ready 3 cups rolled oats (flour)
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup almond milk
  4. Take 1/3 cup maple syrup
  5. Make ready 2 eggs
  6. Get 1 1/2 tbsp cinnamon
  7. Make ready 1/8 tsp salt
  8. Prepare 3/4 tsp baking soda
  9. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  10. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  11. Take 1 tbsp chia seeds (optional)
  12. Get 1/2 cup walnuts, chopped (optional)
Instructions to make Banana Bread:
  1. Blend the oats until they are oat flour
  2. Mash the bananas
  3. Add all of the ingredients together and mix well
  4. Oil a loaf pan, and pour mixture in
  5. Bake at 350F for 45-55 minutes
