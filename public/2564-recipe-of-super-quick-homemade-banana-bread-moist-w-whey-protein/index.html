The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread (Moist) w Whey Protein:
  1. Take 3 banana
  2. Take 2 eggs
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup olive oil
  4. Get 2 tablespoons honey
  5. Get 30 gr whey protein
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup yogurt
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup chia seeds
  9. Make ready 1 teaspoon baking powder
  10. Make ready 1 1/2 cup flour

These Healthy Banana Protein Muffins are the perfect nutritious breakfast! Gluten/dairy free and packed with protein, they're delicious + easy to make! It's vegan, although I'm sure you could use whey protein and these would work perfectly fine! I would not use unflavored protein powder in this.

Instructions to make Banana Bread (Moist) w Whey Protein:
  1. Pre heat oven 170C
  2. Smash bananas until smooth
  3. Mix all ingredients in a bowl and mix
  4. Pour the mixture into the tray and put it in the oven.
  5. Heat the mixture for 50-55 min
  6. Enjoy!
