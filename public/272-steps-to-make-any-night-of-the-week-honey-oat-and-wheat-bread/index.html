The ingredients needed to make Honey Oat and Wheat Bread:
  1. Take bread
  2. Prepare 1 1/4 cup warm water (+3 tbsp) (NOT HOT WATER)
  3. Take 1/4 cup honey
  4. Take 1 1/2 tsp yeast
  5. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp milk powder
  6. Get 1 cup rolled oats
  7. Make ready 1 cup all-purpose white flour
  8. Take 2 cup whole wheat flour
  9. Take 2 tbsp vital wheat gluten (makes the loaves lighter)
  10. Make ready 2 tbsp butter or margarine
Steps to make Honey Oat and Wheat Bread:
  1. Add 1 1/4 cups water and honey to bread machine and mix well
  2. Add yeast to let rise and close lid. Do not turn on machine.
  3. In a medium size bowl mix together white and whole wheat flour, salt, milk powder and wheat gluten.
  4. Add to bread machine and top with butter/margarine. I set my bread maker to sandwich setting, light crust and 2.0 lb loaf.
  5. Turn on and let it go. You may have to add an additional 3 tbsp of water while it mixes if it is too dry.
