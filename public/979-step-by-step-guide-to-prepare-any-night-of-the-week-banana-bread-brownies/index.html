The ingredients needed to make Banana bread brownies:
  1. Take 2 eggs
  2. Get 3 bananas, mashed
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 cups sugar
  4. Make ready 1 cup sour cream
  5. Take 1/2 cup butter, softened
  6. Get 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  7. Make ready 2 cups all-purpose flour
  8. Get 1 teaspoon baking soda
  9. Get 3/4 teaspoon salt
  10. Take 1/2 cup walnuts,chopped
  11. Take Frosting
  12. Take 1/2 cup butter
  13. Prepare 3 cups powdered sugar
  14. Get 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  15. Make ready 3 tablespoons milk

Steps to make Banana bread brownies:
  1. Heat oven to 375°F. Grease a 13 x 9 inch pan.
  2. In a large bowl, beat together sugar, sour cream, butter, and eggs until creamy. - Blend in bananas and vanilla extract. - Add flour, baking soda, salt, and blend for 1 minute. Stir in walnuts. - Spread batter evenly into pan. Bake 25 minutes or until golden brown.
  3. Cool slighty (warm but not hot) and frost with frosting.
  4. For frosting, - Heat butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. - Watch carefully when it reaches a medium brown color turn off heat, whisk in powdered sugar at a time, adding milk as it thickens, then add in vanilla. - Pour over the warm banana bread brownies immediately, and smooth with a spatula, butter knife, or frosting spatula. - Cool completely before cutting.
