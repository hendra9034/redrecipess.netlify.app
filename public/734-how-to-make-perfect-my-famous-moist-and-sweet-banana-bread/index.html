The ingredients needed to make My famous moist and sweet banana bread:
  1. Prepare 1 3\4 cups of flour
  2. Prepare 2 teaspoons cake spice (optional)
  3. Take 3 teaspoon pumpkin spice (optional)
  4. Make ready 2 teaspoon baking soda
  5. Make ready 1 tablespoon baking powder
  6. Get 1 teaspoon salt
  7. Get Wet ingredients
  8. Prepare 1 1\2 cups of butter
  9. Take 1 cup sugar depends on your taste
  10. Prepare 2 teaspoon pure vanilla essence
  11. Take 3 cups mashed bananas
  12. Take Time of the method
  13. Make ready Oh I forgot the eggs add two eggs

Instructions to make My famous moist and sweet banana bread:
  1. Mix the flour with the cake spice,pumpkin spice, salt, baking soda and baking powder together until combined
  2. Now cream butter and sugar until light and flufy
  3. Add the eggs one by one and keep creaming
  4. Add the vanilla and the mashed bananas
  5. When finished add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and fold it in the wet ingredients
  6. Butter a long loaf pan and add the batter to the pan
  7. Bake until golden brown
