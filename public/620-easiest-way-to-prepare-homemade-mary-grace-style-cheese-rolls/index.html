The ingredients needed to make Mary Grace style cheese rolls:
  1. Get Bread
  2. Get 113 g unsalted butter
  3. Make ready 100 g white sugar
  4. Prepare 2 egg yolks
  5. Get 480 g APF
  6. Make ready 8.5 g instant dry yeast
  7. Take 120 g fresh milk
  8. Make ready 240 g water
  9. Prepare 6 g salt
  10. Prepare 1 cup mashed potatoes
  11. Take Toppings and Filling
  12. Prepare Butter, softened
  13. Prepare Granulated sugar, for topping
  14. Make ready Cheese, for filling
  15. Prepare 1 egg
  16. Get 1 tbsp water
Instructions to make Mary Grace style cheese rolls:
  1. Mix wet bread ingredients together, then gradually add in dry. Knead well.
  2. Cover and let rise 1 hour 30 minutes until doubled.
  3. Divide into approx 40g portions and roll with a piece of cheese inside, approx 4 inches long and 0.5 inches wide and tall
  4. Let rise 30 minutes. Apply egg wash.
  5. Bake in a preheated oven at 190 degrees Celsius for 20-25 minutes.
  6. Cool slightly, then top with softened butter and sprinkle or dip the tops in granulated sugar
