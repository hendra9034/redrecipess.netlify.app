The ingredients needed to make Jamaican Cocktail Bread Pudding:
  1. Get 12 slice Bread (Cut in small cubes)
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp ground cinnamon
  3. Make ready 1 can fruit cocktail mix (drained)
  4. Get Custard
  5. Prepare 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  6. Get 2 quart Cow's Milk
  7. Take 5 medium eggs
  8. Prepare 2 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  9. Make ready 1 can evaporated milk
  10. Make ready 1 2/3 tbsp vanilla
  11. Take 1/2 cup rum or rum cream
  12. Take 1/2 cup sherry
  13. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  14. Get Glaze
  15. Get 1/2 cup orange juice
  16. Make ready 1 tbsp butter
  17. Prepare 2 tbsp sugar
  18. Prepare 1/2 oz rum (optional)
Instructions to make Jamaican Cocktail Bread Pudding:
  1. Heat oven at 350 ° F
  2. Grease a med bread pan with butter
  3. Combine bread cubes, cocktail mix and cinnamon in a container.
  4. Place coated cubes and fruits in Greased pan.
  5. Heat and cool milk then mix with all other ingredients for custard.
  6. Pour milk mixture over bread cubes.
  7. Allow to rest for 15-20mins so cubes may absorb the custard.
  8. Bake for 45-50mins
  9. Combine ingredients for glaze over flame in a small saucepan until slightly thicken.
  10. Take pudding from oven, brush with glaze and put to cool.
  11. ENJOY with a scoop of your favorite ice cream :-)
