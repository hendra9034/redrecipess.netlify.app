The ingredients needed to make Caramelized Banana Bread:
  1. Take 70 g flour
  2. Take 30 g almond flour
  3. Make ready 1 tsp baking powder
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Prepare 2 tsp cinnamon (optional)
  6. Take 3 tbs sugar
  7. Prepare 90 g butter (brought to room temperature)
  8. Prepare 1 egg
  9. Take 1 banana
  10. Make ready For the caramelized banana
  11. Get 1 banana
  12. Make ready 4 tbs sugar
  13. Prepare 2 tbs butter or coconut oil
Instructions to make Caramelized Banana Bread:
  1. For the caramelized banana, heat the oil and sugar in a small pan until the sugar melts on medium heat.
  2. Add a banana sliced lengthwise and cook on low heat until both sides have caramelized.
  3. Preheat your oven to 180C/350F. Combine the dry ingredients in a small bowl.
  4. In a large bowl, cream the butter, and gradually add the sugar.
  5. Add the egg, lightly stir, then add half a caramelized banana and another banana and mix until combined. Fold in the dry ingredients.
  6. Pour the batter into a pan and bake for about 40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
