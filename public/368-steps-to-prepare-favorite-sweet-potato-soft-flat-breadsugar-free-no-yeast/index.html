The ingredients needed to make Sweet potato soft flat bread(sugar free, no yeast):
  1. Prepare 50 gms all purpose flour
  2. Make ready 50 gms whole wheat flour
  3. Prepare 100 gms sweet potatoes boiled and mashed
  4. Prepare to taste salt
  5. Get as needed Water
  6. Prepare 1 tbspn olive oil

Steps to make Sweet potato soft flat bread(sugar free, no yeast):
  1. In a mixing bowl combine the flours, salt and oil
  2. Add the sweet potato mash while warm and knead a soft dough using very less water. (use a fork to mix initially if you aren’t able to handle the warm potatoes)
  3. Dust the working board with some flour and roll out thin circular tortillas/ chapatis in batches
  4. Toast them on a skillet on medium-low heat till done/cooked on both sides
  5. Serve warm with a curry or use them as wraps with your favourite stuffing. They taste just as great on their own too. Bon appetit!
  6. Tip:You can even refrigerate them for upto 2 days airtight and microwave them before use.
  7. Tip: The nutritional value in this flat bread makes it a good option for your kid’s lunch box
