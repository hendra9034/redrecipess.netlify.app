Steps to make Paneer rail:
  1. Only white parts of the breads are used.
  2. With the help of toothpicks train compartments are made
  3. With the help of toothpicks sliced banana are kept at the front.
  4. With the help of toothpicks homemade paneer cubes are kept at the sides of the compartments.
  5. Then sliced banana are kept at the sides too
  6. Boiled eggs, tomato slices and paneer cubes are kept in the middle of the compartments.
  7. Kept tomato slice at the back of the train.
  8. Coriander leaves and brown part of the breads are used for garnishing purpose.
