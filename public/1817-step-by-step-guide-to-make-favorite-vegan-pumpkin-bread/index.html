The ingredients needed to make Vegan Pumpkin Bread:
  1. Take 2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup organic sugar
  3. Take 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  4. Take 3 tsp baking powder
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp ground cloves
  6. Take 4 tsp ground cinnamon
  7. Take 1/2 tsp ginger powder
  8. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  9. Get 1 can pumpkin puree (13.5oz)
  10. Take 1/4 cup melted coconut oil
  11. Make ready 1/2 tsp vanilla
  12. Prepare Ground flax egg
  13. Take 1 Tbsp ground flax seed
  14. Get 3 Tbsp water
  15. Prepare For topping
  16. Prepare Pumpkin seeds
