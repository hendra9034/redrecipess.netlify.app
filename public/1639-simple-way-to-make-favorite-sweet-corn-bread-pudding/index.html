The ingredients needed to make Sweet corn bread Pudding:
  1. Get sweet corn bread pudding
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup onion
  3. Make ready 1 oz unsalted butter
  4. Take 1/2 tsp thyme
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp rosemary
  6. Take 1 can creamed style sweet corn
  7. Get 1 cup heavy cream
  8. Get 2 eggs
  9. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  10. Get 1/2 cup yellow cornmeal
  11. Get 1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese
  12. Take 1 tsp salt
  13. Make ready 1 pinch black pepper
  14. Make ready 3 cup cubed french bread

Instructions to make Sweet corn bread Pudding:
  1. First preheat your oven to 350°F
  2. Next take out a pan to saute the onions with the butter and herbs until desired texture. (preferably when softened)
  3. combine corn, eggs, cream, baking powder,cornmeal,cheese, salt,and pepper into a large mixing bowl. Then combine the cubed bread.
  4. pour the combined batter into the skillet with the onion mixture. Bake for about 40-50 minutes, or until golden on top. let sit and cool before serving.
