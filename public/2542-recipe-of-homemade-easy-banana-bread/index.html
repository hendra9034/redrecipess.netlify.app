The ingredients needed to make easy banana bread:
  1. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  2. Make ready 1 cup milk
  3. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  4. Get 1 cup sugar
  5. Prepare 4 bananas
  6. Get 2 cup flour
  7. Get 100 grams butter
  8. Prepare 3 eggs
  9. Get 1 tsp vanilla essence

Steps to make easy banana bread:
  1. Mash bananas until soft.
  2. mix sugar & butter
  3. add eggs , 1 at a time & mix until smooth
  4. mix mashed bananas to the egg & butter mixture.
  5. pour in milk & vanilla & mix well.
  6. sift flour with soda & baking powder & mix with everything .
  7. place in greased bread tin & bake for 20 - 30 mins.
