The ingredients needed to make Cherry-orange bread pudding:
  1. Make ready Bread pudding
  2. Take 6 cup old bread (cubed)
  3. Get 2 tbsp melted butter
  4. Get 4 eggs
  5. Get 2 cup milk
  6. Take 3/4 cup sugar
  7. Take 2 tsp vanilla extract
  8. Get 1 tsp cinnamon
  9. Make ready dash salt
  10. Make ready Cherry-orange sauce
  11. Make ready 1 cup dried cherries
  12. Get 1/2 cup sugar
  13. Get 1/2 cup citrus juice
  14. Take dash salt
  15. Prepare 4 tbsp orange marmalade

Steps to make Cherry-orange bread pudding:
  1. Preheat oven to 350. Put bread into a loaf pan and drizzle butter over it.
  2. Beat eggs, then mix in milk, sugar, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt.
  3. Pour the mixture over bread and press down so that all bread is soaked.
  4. Bake the pudding at 350 for 45 mins.
  5. In a small pot mix all the sauce ingredients and bring to a low boil using medium heat.
  6. Turn the heat down to low and simmer for 5 mins, stirring constantly.
  7. Allow sauce to cool to room temperature.
  8. Slice the bread pudding and top with sauce. Serve with vanilla ice cream.
