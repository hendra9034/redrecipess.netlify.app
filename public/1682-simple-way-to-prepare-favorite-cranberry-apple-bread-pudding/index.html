The ingredients needed to make Cranberry Apple Bread Pudding.:
  1. Prepare 6 cup Milk
  2. Take 6 cup Bread (Cubed)
  3. Make ready 4 tbsp Butter
  4. Make ready 6 large Eggs
  5. Take 1 cup Brown Sugar
  6. Take 1 tsp Salt
  7. Get 1 tsp Cinnamon
  8. Take 1 tsp Nutmeg
  9. Get 1 tbsp Vanilla
  10. Get 2 medium Apple (Chopped)
  11. Prepare 1 cup Dried Cranberry (or other Dried Fruit of choice)
Steps to make Cranberry Apple Bread Pudding.:
  1. Preheat Oven 300°F Fahrenheit.
  2. On the stove top, in a large saucepan, over medium heat, heat milk until bubbly. Remove from heat.
  3. Put bread cubes in a large bowl. Pour warm milk over bread. Set aside.
  4. In a small bowl blend eggs and butter.
  5. Add brown sugar to egg mixture and stir until smooth.
  6. Stir salt, cinnamon, nutmeg and vanilla into egg mixture.
  7. Pour egg mixture over bread mixture and fold in fruit.
  8. Pour bread pudding batter in a bunt pan (or any pan you have that will hold the mixture and give 2" space from top.) Put pan in preheated oven for 45 minutes or until mixture is golden brown on top.
  9. Remove from oven and let cool for 10 minutes. Serve warm with homemade whipped cream.
