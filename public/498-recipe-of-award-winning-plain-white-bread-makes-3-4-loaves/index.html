The ingredients needed to make Plain white bread (makes 3-4 loaves):
  1. Take 9 cups white flour
  2. Make ready 2 2/3 cups very warm water
  3. Take 3/4 cups milk
  4. Prepare 3 tbsp shortening
  5. Take 4 1/2 tbsp quick rise yeast
  6. Make ready 1/3 cup sugar
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  8. Get 2 tbsp honey
  9. Get margarine

Instructions to make Plain white bread (makes 3-4 loaves):
  1. Mix all the ingredients together except the margarine. I like to use a bread machine to kneed the bread for me but you can knead it by hand. Knead for 10 minutes until the dough stretchy and elastic.
  2. Turn your oven on for 3 minutes and then shut off just to get it a little bit warm. Divide your dough into two or three equal balls. Rub each ball all over with margarine or butter. Place each ball into a greased loaf pan and squish it down.
  3. Place the loaves into the oven for 15 to 20 minutes and let rise until it's the size that you want it to be. Remove from oven.
  4. Preheat oven to 405 Degrees. Cook loaves on center rack until when tapped it sounds hollow inside.
  5. Cool on a baking rack. Optional: brush tops with butter. Enjoy!
