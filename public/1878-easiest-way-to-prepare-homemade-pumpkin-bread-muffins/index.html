Instructions to make Pumpkin Bread/muffins:
  1. preheat oven to 350
  2. in large bowl beat eggs, sugar, vanilla, pumpkin and oil until blended
  3. slowly add flour mix to the large bowl while blending
  4. in a small bowl whisk flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and pumpkin spice
  5. butter bread pan/ or place cupcake holders in a cup cake pan
  6. if you are making muffins fill holders 3/4 full for bread fill about half full
  7. mix optional ingredients in with spoon before putting in pans
