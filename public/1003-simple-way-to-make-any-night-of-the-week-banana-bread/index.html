The ingredients needed to make Banana bread:
  1. Get 1/2 cup Salted Butter
  2. Make ready 1 cup White Sugar
  3. Make ready 2 Eggs
  4. Get 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  5. Take 1 1/2 cups All-purpose Flour
  6. Make ready 1 tsp Baking Soda
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  8. Take 1/2 cup Sour Cream
  9. Prepare 2 Brown Bananas

Instructions to make Banana bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F
  2. In large bowl smooth together melted butter and sugar.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla, and mix well.
  4. Combine in baking soda, salt and flour.
  5. Fold in sour cream and bananas.
  6. Bake at 325 for 70 minutes.
  7. Let cool at least 15 minutes before slicing.
