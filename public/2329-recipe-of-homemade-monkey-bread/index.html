Instructions to make Monkey Bread:
  1. Cut each biscuit into 4 sections. Pre-heat oven to 350 °
  2. Add sugar and cinnamon into ziploc bag. Insert biscuits in ziploc bag make sure each piece is coated.
  4. Melt butter and add brown sugar into a bowl. Mix until well blended. Set aside.
  5. Add each piece of coated biscuits to baking pan. Add walnuts and raisins. Layer it with biscuit sections.
  6. Pour brown sugar mix over biscuits. Cook for 30 minutes.
