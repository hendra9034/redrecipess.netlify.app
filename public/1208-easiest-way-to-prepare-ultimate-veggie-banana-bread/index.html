The ingredients needed to make Veggie banana bread:
  1. Make ready 4 bananas
  2. Get 1/3 cup coconut sugar
  3. Prepare 1/3 cup oil
  4. Make ready 2 cups flour
  5. Get 1/2 bag baking powder
  6. Prepare 2 spoons crushed linseed
  7. Prepare 1 spoon cinnamon
  8. Take Pinch salt
  9. Make ready Your favorite nuts and seeds
  10. Take Egg substitute (1 egg)
  11. Get 1 spoon chia seeds
  12. Make ready 3 spoons water
