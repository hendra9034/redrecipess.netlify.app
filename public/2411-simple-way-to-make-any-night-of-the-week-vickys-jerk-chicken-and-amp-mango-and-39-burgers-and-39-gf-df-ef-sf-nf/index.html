Instructions to make Vickys Jerk Chicken & Mango 'Burgers', GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Crush the jerk seasoning ingredients together with a pestle and mortar or combine them in a spice grinder. You can multiply the recipe and store the seasoning in a sealed jar
  2. Mix the oil, jerk seasoning and the juice from 1 of the limes together
  3. Flatten the chicken slightly by placing each breast between sheets of cling film and bash them with a rolling pin. Don't go crazy, just take them to a uniform thickness so they cook evenly
  4. Toss the chicken in the marinade and let sit for 10 minutes
  5. Heat a griddle or frying pan and cook the chicken for 5 minutes on each side or until cooked through. Toast the buns on the cut sides
  6. Spread each bun with a tablespoon of mayo then assemble with the chicken, mango, lettuce and tomato, squeezing the leftover lime juice over before putting the bun tops on
