Steps to make Bread Pudding:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Break bread into small pieces in an 8" square baking pan. Drizzle melted butter on top of bread and if desired garnish with raisins.
  3. In a mixing bowl combine eggs, sugar, cinnamon and vanilla. Whisk until well incorporated. Add milk and mix until combined.
  4. Pour mixture over bread. Distribute evenly. Take a fork and gently soak bread into mixture.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes or until bread springs back when lightly touched.
  6. Enjoy!!
