Steps to make Bananas Foster Bread Pudding:
  1. Preheat oven to 325°F.
  2. Cream together butter and brown sugar.
  3. Add eggs one at a time until fully incorporated.
  4. Add cinnamon and vanilla.
  5. Add sour cream, half & half, milk, and rum until fully blended.
  6. Combine liquid mixture with sliced bananas and bread and fold together until bread soaks up most of the liquid.
  7. Pour mixture into greased 9 x 13 baking dish.
  8. Place in oven for 90 minutes or until top id golden brown, and pudding is firm and set.
  9. Allow to cool for at least 20 minutes before cutting.
