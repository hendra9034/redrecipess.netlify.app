The ingredients needed to make Whole Wheat Bacon and Cheese Bread:
  1. Take 170 grams Bread (strong) flour
  2. Get 30 grams Whole wheat flour
  3. Prepare 10 grams Sugar
  4. Make ready 3 grams Salt
  5. Make ready 5 grams Skim milk powder
  6. Prepare 2 grams Instant dry yeast
  7. Prepare 120 grams Water
  8. Get 1/2 tsp Cumin powder
  9. Get 1/4 tsp Black pepper
  10. Prepare 10 grams ○Shortening
  11. Prepare 40 grams ○Bacon
  12. Make ready 1 ○Pizza cheese
Instructions to make Whole Wheat Bacon and Cheese Bread:
  1. Cut the bacon into small strips. Mix everything but the ○ ingredients together. Bring the dough together and mix in the shortening and bacon as well.
  2. Let it rise until it doubled in size.
  3. When the dough is ready, divide into 6 and let rest for 15 minutes.
  4. Roll into nice round balls and let rise (2nd rising).
  5. Brush with as much or as little of the egg wash as you like, cut a cross in the top with kitchen scissors, and top with cheese.
  6. Bake in an oven heated to 220°C for 13 minutes.
