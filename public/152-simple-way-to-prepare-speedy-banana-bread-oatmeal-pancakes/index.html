The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread Oatmeal Pancakes:
  1. Make ready 2 Large Bananas
  2. Take 3 Egg Whites
  3. Take 1/4 tsp. Baking Powder
  4. Take 1 C. Quick Cooking Oats
  5. Get 1/4 C. Nonfat Yogurt
  6. Get 1/4 tsp. Ground Cinnamon
Steps to make Banana Bread Oatmeal Pancakes:
  1. Peel bananas. Place them in a medium bowl and mash.
  2. Add in the egg whites, yogurt and baking powder, mix well until combined.
  3. Add oats and cinnamon, mix well.
  4. Heat a skillet on medium heat, place a spoonful or two of batter in skillet
  5. Spread into a pancake shape. Cook about 2 minutes on each side of until done. Enjoy!
