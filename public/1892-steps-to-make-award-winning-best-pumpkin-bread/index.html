The ingredients needed to make best pumpkin bread:
  1. Get 3 cup sugar
  2. Make ready 1 can 15 oz pumpkin
  3. Get 1 cup veg oil
  4. Take 3 eggs
  5. Make ready 3 cup flour
  6. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  7. Take 1/2 tsp baking powder
  8. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  9. Take 1 tsp cinnamon
  10. Get 1 tsp nutmeg

Instructions to make best pumpkin bread:
  1. makes 2 loaves
  2. preheat oven to 325
  3. grease and flour 2 bread pans
  4. combine wet ingredients and sugar in 1 bowl
  5. combine dry ingredients in separate bowl
  6. sift dry ingredients into wet ingredients and blend with fork until smooth
  7. bake 1hr 10min or until toothpick comes out clean
