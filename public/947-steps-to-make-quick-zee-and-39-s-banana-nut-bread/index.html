The ingredients needed to make Zee's Banana Nut Bread:
  1. Get 8 large bananas
  2. Prepare 3 cup all-purpose flour
  3. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
  4. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  5. Make ready 1 1/2 stick butter
  6. Make ready 1 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  7. Get 2/3 tsp salt
  8. Take 2 eggs
  9. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla or banana extract
  10. Take 1/2 cup milk
  11. Make ready 1 1/2 cup chopped nuts
Steps to make Zee's Banana Nut Bread:
  1. If you're like me and have a pecan tree now would be the time to chop them if you have store brought pieces skip this step
  2. Mash bananas, make sure to have some small chunks.
  3. Preheat oven to 350
  4. Mix together sugar and butter.
  5. Mix in eggs, extract, and milk
  6. Mix together baking powder, baking soda, flour, and salt. Then add to butter mixture
  7. Add in bananas and pecans
  8. Spray your baking sheet, pan or whatever you use with cooking spray. Add mixture.
  9. Cook for 45 mins. If it isn't done in the middle I check it every 5-10 minutes.
  10. ENJOY!!!!!
  11. I sometimes put a pan with about 4 cups of water on the bottom rack just in case but you don't have to do this.
