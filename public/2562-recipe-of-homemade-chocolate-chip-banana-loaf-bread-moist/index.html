The ingredients needed to make Chocolate chip banana loaf bread (moist):
  1. Prepare 180 g All-purpose flour
  2. Get 100 g sugar
  3. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
  4. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  5. Get 3 mashed bananas
  6. Get 1 egg (lightly beaten)
  7. Prepare 75 g butter (melted)
  8. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  9. Get 100 gr chocolate chips

I tried this and it was amazing. Super moist and deliciously sweet banana bread made in one bowl with dark chocolate chips! Grease loaf pan with butter or oil. Mix flour, sugar, baking soda and salt in a mixing bowl.

Instructions to make Chocolate chip banana loaf bread (moist):
  1. Pre heat oven 350°F
  2. Grease loaf pan with butter or oil.
  3. Mix flour, sugar, baking soda and salt in a mixing bowl.
  4. Mix mashed bananas, egg, melted butter and vanilla extract in a separate bowl.
  5. Pour banana mixture into flour. Stir until the batter becomes smooth.
  6. Add 3/4 of the chocolate chips. Stir.
  7. Pour the batter into the pan.
  8. Sprinkle with the remaining chocolate chips.
  9. Bake at 350°F for 50 minutes or until a toothpic comes out dry and clean.
