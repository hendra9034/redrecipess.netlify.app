The ingredients needed to make Zucchini Bread:
  1. Take 3 Eggs
  2. Get 2 cup Sugar
  3. Prepare 1 cup Veg oil
  4. Get 1 tbsp Vanilla
  5. Get 2 cup Grated zucchini
  6. Make ready 3 cup Flour
  7. Prepare 1 tsp Salt
  8. Get 2 tsp Baking powder
  9. Take 1 tbsp Cinnamon
  10. Prepare 1 cup Chopped nuts (opt)

Steps to make Zucchini Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°
  2. Beat eggs til frothy
  3. Add sugar, veg oil, vanilla
  4. Beat til thick lemon color
  5. Add grated zucchini
  6. Sift together flour, salt, baking powder,& cinnamon
  7. Add to mixture then fold in nuts
  8. Pour into 2 loaf pans
  9. Bake for 1 hour
