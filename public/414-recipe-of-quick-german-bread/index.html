The ingredients needed to make German bread:
  1. Get 250 grams whole wheat flour
  2. Make ready 250 grams all-purpose flour
  3. Prepare 1 packages instant yeast
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp sugar
  5. Take 1 tsp salt
  6. Take 2 tbsp oil
  7. Take 350 ml water

Instructions to make German bread:
  1. Mix all ingredients together and knead them well in a bowl - add more flour if needed
  2. Cover the bowl and let it rest for around an hour or till the dough doubles it's size
  3. Prepare a bread pan (9x5 inches) with grease or butter and put the dough in. Let it rest for anouther 20-30 minutes (covered)
  4. Cover the dough slightly with water and flour
  5. Put into the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes at 430ºF
