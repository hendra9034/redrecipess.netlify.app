The ingredients needed to make Picky kid Banana bread:
  1. Prepare 2 over ripened bananas
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Take 1 egg white
  4. Take 2 tbsp oil
  5. Take 1/2 cup flour
  6. Get 1/2 tsp baking powder
  7. Prepare 1/4 tsp baking soda
  8. Take 1/8 tsp salt
  9. Take 1/8 tsp cinnamon
  10. Take Pinch nutmeg
  11. Get 1 tsp vanilla
  12. Get Optional- chocolate chips
