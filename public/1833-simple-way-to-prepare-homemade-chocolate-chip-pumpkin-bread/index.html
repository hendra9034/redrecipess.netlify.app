The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Chip Pumpkin Bread:
  1. Prepare 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  3. Get 1 1/8 tsp cinnamon
  4. Get 1/2 tsp plus 1/8 tsp nutmeg
  5. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Take 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  7. Make ready 1/4 tsp ground ginger
  8. Take 1 cup granulated sugar
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup butter
  10. Take 2 large eggs
  11. Take 3/4 cup pumpkin puree
  12. Make ready 1 cup chocolate chips
  13. Take 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  14. Make ready 2 tbsp milk

Steps to make Chocolate Chip Pumpkin Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, 1 tsp cinnamon, 1/2 tsp nutmeg, the salt, cloves, & ginger in a medium bowl & mix well.
  3. Combine granulated sugar & butter in a large bowl. Beat with an electric mixer set at high speed until creamy. Beat in eggs. Add the flour mixture alternately with the pumpkin, mixing well after each addition. Stir in chocolate chips.
  4. Spoon batter into prepared loaf pan. Bake until a toothpick inserted comes out clean, about 55 to 60 minutes. Cool loaf in pan for 5 minutes. Remove to a wire rack to cool completely.
  5. For glaze, combine the remaining cinnamon, remaining nutmeg, powdered sugar, & milk in small bowl & mix well. Drizzle over cooled loaf.
