The ingredients needed to make Savory Bread Pudding:
  1. Prepare 3 Eggs
  2. Prepare 1 cup Greek Yogurt
  3. Prepare 3 tsp Chicken bouillon
  4. Prepare 1 1/2 cup Grated mozzarella cheese
  5. Get 1 cup Crumbled Feta Cheese
  6. Prepare 10 loaf Stale White bread
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup Chopped Cilantro
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp Garlic powder
  9. Prepare 3/4 tsp Onion Powder
  10. Get 1 Salt
  11. Prepare 1 Pepper
  12. Make ready 1 cup Any chopped meat you like
Instructions to make Savory Bread Pudding:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Mix eggs, yogurt, salt, pepper, onion powder, garlic powder, bouillon and cheeses.
  3. Cut the bread to small bite pieces. I used my own honey cinnamon dinner roll for this so it's more incorporated since it crumbled more.
  4. Dump the bread pieces to the egg mixture, add a little bit of water if it's too thick
  5. Pour the mixture to the casserole pan, and put it in the oven for 20 minutes
  6. Done :) serve hot
