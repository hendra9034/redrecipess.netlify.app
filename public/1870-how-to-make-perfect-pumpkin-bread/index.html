The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Bread:
  1. Get flour
  2. Make ready sugar
  3. Get butter softened
  4. Take baking soda
  5. Take salt
  6. Make ready ground cinnamon
  7. Prepare ground nutmeg
  8. Take pumpkin or one 15 oz can pumpkin
  9. Make ready eggs
  10. Get milk

Steps to make Pumpkin Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Grease two 9x5x3 inch loaf pans
  3. In large mixing bowl beat butter and sugar on medium speed. Add eggs beat well. Set aside
  4. In large bowl combine flour,baking soda,salt,cinnamon and nutmeg
  5. Slowly add flour mixture and milk to sugar mixture beating on low just till combined
  6. Beat in pumpkin
  7. Spoon batter in prepared pans
  8. Bake for 55 to 65 minutes or until tooth pick inserted in middle comes out clean
  9. Cool on wire racks for 10 minute
  10. Remove from loaf pans and finish cooling on wire rack
