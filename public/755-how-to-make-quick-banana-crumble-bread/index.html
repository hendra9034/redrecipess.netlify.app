The ingredients needed to make Banana Crumble Bread:
  1. Take Mix these and set aside
  2. Prepare 3-4 medium or large bananas
  3. Get 2 Tbsp brown sugar
  4. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Make ready 3/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  6. Prepare Cream together first 3 ingredients, then add eggs
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup butter, softened
  8. Take 1 cup sugar
  9. Take 2 Tbsp sour cream or Greek yogurt
  10. Prepare 2 eggs, room temp
  11. Get Whisk in a separate bowl
  12. Prepare 2 cups all-purpose flour
  13. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  14. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  15. Prepare Crumble
  16. Prepare 1 tsp cinnamon
  17. Prepare 1 cup flour
  18. Get 1 cup brown sugar
  19. Get 6 Tbsp butter, cold
Instructions to make Banana Crumble Bread:
  1. Mash bananas, brown sugar, vanilla extract, and ground cinnamon.
  2. In a large bowl, cream butter and sugar together and sour cream until light and fluffy. - Add in eggs and beat until mixed.
  3. Sift in flour, baking soda, and salt. - Stir until just combined. Add in sour cream and stir.
  4. Pour in mashed bananas, and mix until combined.
  5. Divide batter into 2 loaf pans
  6. Combine crumble ingredients. Cut in butter with a pastry cutter, and mix until the topping looks like coarse crumbs. Sprinkle coating evenly over the batter in both pans.
  7. Bake at 325F for about 35-40 minutes or until cake tester comes out clean with just a few moist crumbs stuck to it. - Allow to cool for 10 minutes. Move to a wire cooling rack. - Slice and enjoy! Helen Klassen
