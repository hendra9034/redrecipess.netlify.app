The ingredients needed to make My style Irish white soda bread:
  1. Get 4 cups plain flour
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup porridge oats
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 cup buttermilk(u may need more liquid if it looks dry)
  4. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  5. Take 1 tsp salt
  6. Take Flour for dusting
