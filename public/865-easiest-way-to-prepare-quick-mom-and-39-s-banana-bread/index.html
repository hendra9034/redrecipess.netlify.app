The ingredients needed to make Mom's Banana Bread:
  1. Make ready Cream Together:
  2. Get 4 oz butter
  3. Make ready 8 oz sugar
  4. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla
  5. Take Fruit Mixture:
  6. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  7. Take 1 tsp soda
  8. Take 3 bananas, browned
  9. Make ready Wet:
  10. Take 2 eggs
  11. Prepare 1/4 c sour cream
  12. Make ready Dry:
  13. Make ready 11 oz flour
  14. Take Optional:
  15. Prepare 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  16. Make ready 2 c chocolate chips
  17. Get 1 t cinnamon
  18. Take 1/4 t cloves
Steps to make Mom's Banana Bread:
  1. Set oven to 350 F.
  2. Measure all ingresients
  3. Cream together sugar, vanilla, salt and butter until fluffy. ~3 minutes!
  4. Add soda to the bananas separately. Then, add this into the mix
  5. Add sour cream, and one egg at a time. Mix until just combined.
  6. Add flour in small increments until fully incorporated. Should be thick and slightly tacky
  7. Optional, mix in the walnuts by hand
  8. Grease pan and bake. Bake times vary depending upon pan.
  9. Bundt pan = 35 to 40 minutes. ****Mini loaf pans = 22 minutes. ****Mini muffin pan = 15 minutes.
