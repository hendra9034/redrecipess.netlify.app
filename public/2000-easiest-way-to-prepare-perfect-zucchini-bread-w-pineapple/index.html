The ingredients needed to make Zucchini Bread w/ Pineapple:
  1. Make ready flour
  2. Prepare sugar
  3. Get nuts
  4. Get zucchini
  5. Make ready drained crushed pineapple (with 1/4 raisins)
  6. Prepare drained crushed pineapple (without raisins)
  7. Make ready salad oil
  8. Take baking soda
  9. Take cinnamon
  10. Prepare nutmeg
  11. Make ready baking powder
  12. Get salt
  13. Make ready eggs
  14. Prepare vanilla
Instructions to make Zucchini Bread w/ Pineapple:
  1. Mix all dry ingredients—set aside
  2. Beat eggs until frothy
  3. Beat in oil—vanilla—sugar until thick and foamy
  4. Stir in dry ingredients— zucchini— pineapple and egg mixture.
  5. Place in 2 greased and floured loaf pans at 350 for 1hr or when toothpick comes out dry.
  6. If using raisins—add 1/4 cup in step 4.
