The ingredients needed to make Banana Nut Bread Baked Oatmeal:
  1. Prepare 3 cups old fashioned oats
  2. Take 1/2 tsp. Cinnamon
  3. Take 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  4. Make ready 1 tsp baking powder
  5. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  6. Take 2 large eggs
  7. Get 1/4 cup pure maple syrup
  8. Take 1/4 cup Stevia brown sugar
  9. Take 2 chopped bananas
  10. Take 2 tsp vanilla
  11. Make ready 1 cup skim milk
  12. Take 1/4 cup canola oil
  13. Make ready 1/3 cup mini chocolate chips
  14. Prepare 1/3 cup chopped pecans
Steps to make Banana Nut Bread Baked Oatmeal:
  1. Mix oatmeal, cinnamon,nutmeg, salt, baking powder together thoroughly in a large bowl.
  2. Beat eggs in medium bowl then mix in banana, maple syrup, stevia brown sugar, and vanilla stir until well combined. Then slowly whisk in oil and milk. Add liquid to oatmeal mixture and stir well. - Add the chocolate chips and nuts, stirring in well.
  3. Spray a 12 hole muffin pan with cooking spray and evenly divide the oatmeal.
  4. Bake in 350 degree oven for 25- 30 minutes or until lightly browned and moderately firm. - Cool in pan for 10 minutes then remove to wire rack and cool. - Muffins should be stored in airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.
