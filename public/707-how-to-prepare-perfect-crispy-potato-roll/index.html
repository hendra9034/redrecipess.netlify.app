The ingredients needed to make Crispy Potato Roll:
  1. Take 1 cup-mashed boil potatoes
  2. Get 6 slices-bread(white/brown)
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp-chopped ginger
  4. Take 1/2 tsp green chilli
  5. Get 2 tsp chopped onion
  6. Prepare 1 tsp capsicum
  7. Prepare 1 tsp carrot
  8. Get 1 tsp coriander
  9. Get 1/3 tsp chaat masala
  10. Get 1/3 tsp cumin powder
  11. Prepare to taste Salt and red chilli powder
Steps to make Crispy Potato Roll:
  1. Take a bowl put all veggies,spices mix it together nicely
  2. Firstly cut edges of bread secondly sprinkle some water on the bread slices and squeeze the excess water press the bread slices with help of the palm
  3. Put some mixture on squeezed bread slice and roll it make all rolls rest it for 10 minutes (rest makes more crispy)
  4. Fry the rolls till it becomes golden brown
