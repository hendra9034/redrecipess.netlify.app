Instructions to make Pumpkin Bread/Muffins:
  1. Sift together dry ingredients, including sugar, into a mixing bowl.
  2. Make a well in the dry ingredients and add all remaining ingredients. Mix together until smooth.
  3. Put batter into 3 conventional bread loaf pans that have been greased and floured (non-stick spray will not work). Alternatively, drop by tablespoonfuls into a mini-muffin tin.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees F for one hour, or until toothpick comes out clean. If making mini-muffins, bake for 12-15 minutes. Cool slightly in pans, then turn onto racks to finish cooling.
  5. Wrap in foil or plastic and store in the refrigerator, or freeze if desired.
