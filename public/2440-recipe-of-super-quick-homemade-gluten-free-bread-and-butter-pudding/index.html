Steps to make gluten free bread and butter pudding:
  1. pre-heat oven to 180°F Celcius.
  2. crack eggs into bowl , add sugar and vanilla extract
  3. Whisk egg mix with milk .
  4. butter both sides of each slice of gliten free bread (cut bread on diagonal or squares anyway to fit your oven proof bowl)
  5. Fit bread into oven proof bowl scattering layers of raisins inbetween and ontop .
  6. pour over liquid mix and let it abzorb before pouring in some more
  7. OPTIONS - brush top of bread with marmalade of apricot jam
  8. OPTIONS - Sprinkle top with brown sugar for lovely golden crusty top
  9. OPTIONS - use chocolate drops instead of raisins
  10. Bake until top is golden and custard is set . press on your pudding with a spoon firmly and if it bounces back its done .
