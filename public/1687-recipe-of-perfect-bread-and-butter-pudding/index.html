The ingredients needed to make Bread and butter pudding:

  2. Get 3 oz dried fruit
  3. Make ready 1 oz one and half oz of sugar
  4. Get 2 medium eggs
  5. Make ready 3 quarters of a pint of milk

Steps to make Bread and butter pudding:
  1. cooking time: 45 minutes. preparation time: 15 minutes. oven temperature: very moderate 325 to 350°F gas mark 3
  2. but bread and butter in to triangles
  3. put in to a heat proof dish and add dried fruit
  4. beat eggs with sugar and pour on warm milk then strain over bread and butter
  5. bake until just firm and set move the pudding towards the top of the oven before serving to encourage bread and butter to crisp and brown if baking dish fairly deep it may take longer
