The ingredients needed to make Cauliflower Crusted Pizza Bites- Keto:
  1. Prepare 1 head cauliflower
  2. Prepare 1 egg
  3. Take 1 cup Grated Parmesan
  4. Take Season with Italian herb and panko bread crumbs to taste (smell)
  5. Take Cheese for topping
  6. Take 1/2 cup marinara
  7. Take Pepperoni if desired
Instructions to make Cauliflower Crusted Pizza Bites- Keto:
  1. Preheat oven to 400
  2. Cut florets off cauliflower and mince in blender
  3. Pour into microwavable bowl and heat in high for 4 minutes
  4. Pour into a towel and squeeze out as much moisture as possible (a kitchen towel works great but it may take more than one) until dough forms
  5. Mix the parmesan and seasonings into the dough evenly
  6. Roll into small flat patties and place on greased cookie sheet
  7. Bake for 20 minutes or until golden colored
  8. Remove from oven and top with marinara, remaining cheese, and pepperoni if desired and bake until toppings are melted or crisp (about 5-7 minutes)
