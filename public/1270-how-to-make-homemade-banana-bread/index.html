The ingredients needed to make Banana bread:
  1. Prepare 2 cups almond flour
  2. Make ready 4 bananas
  3. Take 4 eggs
  4. Prepare 1/3 cup almond milk
  5. Take 1 tbsp cinnamon
  6. Take 1/2 tbsp baking powder
  7. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla
  8. Make ready 1/4 brown sugar or honey
  9. Prepare Honey
  10. Take Walnuts or chocolate chips
  11. Make ready 150 grams ghee butter
  12. Get Salt

Steps to make Banana bread:
  1. Start by blending the eggs with ghee butter in the meantime pre heat your oven at 350•F, then add the sugar and keep blending slowly. Then smash bananas with a fork and add it to the blending bowl
  2. Then add almond milk, add the cinnamon, vanilla and the rest of ingredients. Add a touch of salt.
  3. Keep nuts and honey aside for now.
  4. Slice bananas and put them in the bottom of your pan, add honey after that, add walnuts after or chocolate chips. Then you can add the mix and put your cake in the oven.
  5. I made two, one with walnuts and the smaller one with chocolate chips! Enjoy
