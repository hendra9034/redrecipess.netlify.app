The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin bread:
  1. Make ready pumpkin puree
  2. Get brown sugar
  3. Get warm water
  4. Take strong white flour
  5. Take salt
  6. Get sachet fast action dried yeast
  7. Prepare Egg to glaze
  8. Make ready pumpkin seeds

Steps to make Pumpkin bread:
  1. Combine flour, salt and yeast in big bowl.
  2. Mix pumpkin puree, sugar and 100ml of the water.
  3. Add pumpkin mixture to the flour mixture.
  4. Work dough until is soft.
  5. Knead until smooth on a floured surface.
  6. Put back in a bowl and cover with clean tea towel and leave to raise in a warm place.
  7. Preheat oven. 220C or fan 200C
  8. Knock dough back and knead a little. Divide in three and roll to three long sausages so that you can plait them.
  9. Place on baking tray and glaze with beaten egg. Sprinkle with pumpkin seeds
