The ingredients needed to make Easy Peasy Banana Bread:
  1. Get 3 ripe bananas
  2. Make ready 4 tbsp butter (melted)
  3. Take 1/2 c dark brown sugar
  4. Make ready 1/2 c white sugar
  5. Prepare 2 eggs
  6. Get 1 c milk
  7. Get 1 1/2 c self-rising flour
  8. Make ready 1 tsp cinnamon
  9. Make ready 1 tsp anise extract (optional)
