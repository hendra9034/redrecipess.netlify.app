Instructions to make Apple bread pudding:
  1. In a large mixing bowl, add eggs, milk, cinnamon, nutmeg, mix well, add the chopped bread pieces and mix well.
  2. Take a baking pan, grease all the sides with butter, place the above made mixture onto the pan.
  3. In another bowl, add the chopped apples, brown sugar and corn flour, mix well.
  4. Pour the apple mixture on the top of the bread mixture in the pan
  5. Preheat the oven to 350, bake for 50 mins. Remove out and let it cool
  6. Enjoy a slice when ltr. You can reheat once again in microwave before eating.
