The ingredients needed to make Chocolate banana nut bread:
  1. Get 1/2 cup butter
  2. Get 1 cup sugar
  3. Make ready 1 vanilla bean or 2tsp of extract
  4. Take 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  5. Take 2 eggs
  6. Prepare 4 small very ripe bananas
  7. Prepare 2 cups bread flour
  8. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
  9. Make ready 1 tsp baking powder
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup walnuts

Steps to make Chocolate banana nut bread:
  1. Blend butter and sugar until creamed then add vanilla bean and cocoa powder with 4 bananas blend well
  2. Now add eggs one at a time slowly add flour and baking soda and powder mix well fold in the walnuts
  3. Preheat 350 place mix in loaf pan for 1 hour. Check with toothpick and enjoy
