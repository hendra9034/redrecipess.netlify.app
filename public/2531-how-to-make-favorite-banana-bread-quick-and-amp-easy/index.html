The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread (Quick & Easy):
  1. Prepare 4 Bananas, smashed
  2. Make ready 1/3 cup melted butter
  3. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  4. Make ready 1 Egg, Beaten
  5. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla
  6. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  7. Prepare 1 pinch salt
  8. Prepare 1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour

Steps to make Banana Bread (Quick & Easy):
  1. Preheat oven to 360°
  2. In a large bowl, Mix with a wooden spoon, butter into the mashed bananas
  3. Mix in the sugar, egg, & vanilla well
  4. Mix baking soda, salt & flour in well.
  5. Pour mixture into a greased 4×8 loaf pan. Bake for and hour.
  6. Optional, add walnuts, chocolate chips, etc. for additional yumminess
