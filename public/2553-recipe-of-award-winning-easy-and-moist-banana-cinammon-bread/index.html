The ingredients needed to make Easy (and Moist) Banana Cinammon Bread:
  1. Make ready 1 cup all purpose flour,
  2. Make ready 3 ripe bananas,
  3. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda,
  4. Take 1/3 cup milk,
  5. Take 1/3 cup cooking oil/melted butter,
  6. Get 1 tsp cinnamon,
  7. Get Sugar(add it to your liking),
  8. Prepare 1 egg

Instructions to make Easy (and Moist) Banana Cinammon Bread:
  1. In a bowl, Mash your bananas until it reaches a consistency of baby food(no need for perfection),
  2. Add sugar, cooking oil/butter, cinnamon, an egg and whisk them together on a medium high speed,
  3. Then add your milk and whisk for a min,
  4. Sieve your flour and baking soda in the mixture and continue to whisk to form a batter consistency,
  5. Preheat oven at 180Â°c and brush you baking tin with butter or cooking oil,
  6. Pour your batter in the tin and bake for 40min or until you insert a skewer and it comes out clean,
  7. When it's ready, cover the tin with a damp cloth and allow it to cool down.
