The ingredients needed to make Basic wheat bread:
  1. Take 2 cup Wheat flour
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup bread flour
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup Bread flour (reserve for if needed)
  4. Get 2 tbsp granulated sugar (reserve about 1 tsp)
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp olive oil
  6. Get 1 cup milk (1%, 2%, or whole)
  7. Get 1 Egg at room temperature
  8. Take 1 envelope active dry yeast
  9. Take 2 tbsp Honey (optional and only if you want your bread to be a honey wheat bread)
  10. Take 1 tsp Salt

Instructions to make Basic wheat bread:
  1. Warm milk (milk should be warm enough that when you stick you finger in it doesn't burn….heat approximately 40 in microwave so milk is about 110-120°)
  2. Add envelope of yeast with about a tsp of sugar and let sit for about 5 to 8 min. When it's foamy the yeast has bloomed…(if it didn't bloom then either your milk was NOT warm enough or your yeast was old)
  3. To your standing mixer with dough hook add 2 C of wheat flour and 1/4 C of bread flour with 1 tsp salt and 2 TBSP sugar mix together
  4. With mixer on med to med/high (so if your mixer has speeds from 1 to 4 use 3); add 2 TBSP olive oil, 1 egg (slightly beaten) at room temperature, (this is also where you add your 2 TBSP of honey if you want your bread to be honey wheat bread….(NOTE IT DOES NOT MAKE YOUR BREAD SWEET) and pour in your milk mixture and continue mix.
