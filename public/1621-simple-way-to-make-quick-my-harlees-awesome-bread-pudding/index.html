The ingredients needed to make My Harlee’s Awesome Bread Pudding:
  1. Prepare 2 cups milk
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup butter
  3. Prepare 2 eggs slightly beaten
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
  5. Take 1 tsp cinnamon or nutmeg
  6. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  7. Make ready 6 cups soft bread (6 slices)
  8. Prepare Vanilla extract
  9. Take Whipping (heavy) cream if desired
Steps to make My Harlee’s Awesome Bread Pudding:
  1. Heat oven 350
  2. In a 2 qt saucepan heat milk and butter over medium heat until butter is melted and milk is hot
  3. In a large bowl mix eggs sugar cinnamon and salt.
  4. Stir bread in the mixture
  5. Bake for 45 min.
  6. You can whip up the cream to top
  7. She adds a little more cinnamon
