Instructions to make Bread pudding with pecan whiskey sauce:
  1. Slice and arrange bread in a 10"×18" pan. Evenly add 1 cup of raisins and then 1 cup of butterscotch chips and 1 cup of chopped pecans
  2. In a large mixing bowl add 1 cup of Brown sugar 4 cups of milk 1+1/2 tbsp of Mexican vanilla and 3/4 cups of melted butter. Almost forgot to add 5 eggs mix well
  3. Now pour over the bread wash your hands and gently make sure your mixture is evenly done. Place in a 350°oven for 1 hour
