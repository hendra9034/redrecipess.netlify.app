Instructions to make Om Ali/ Umm Ali/ Egyptian Bread Pudding:
  1. In a big mixing bowl add milk and condensed milk and mix well.
  2. Roughly chop the nuts of your choice.
  3. In a heat proof serving dish, layer the palmiers, top with nuts and coconut, then pour the milk over it. Repeat the steps. I divided the dessert in 2 medium dishes.
  4. Top with lots of cream.
  5. Pre heat the oven and place the dish on medium rack for 10 mins with top flame only. (if you have a regular gas oven, give extra 5 mins on the top most rack, keep checking in between)
  6. Remove from oven and let it cool on room temperature, top with more nuts if you like.
  7. Serve as it is or chill for a while.
