The ingredients needed to make Blueberry Zucchini bread:
  1. Make ready 3 cups flour
  2. Prepare 1.5 cups monk fruit sweetener
  3. Make ready 1 tsp baking powder
  4. Get 1/2 tsp baking soda
  5. Make ready 3 eggs
  6. Make ready 3 tsp vanilla extract
  7. Get 1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
  8. Take 3/4 cup Fage 2% Greek yogurt
  9. Prepare 2 cups shredded zucchini
  10. Take 1 pint blueberries
  11. Prepare 1 tbsp cinnamon

Steps to make Blueberry Zucchini bread:
  1. Mix the wet ingredients.
  2. Measure out dry ingredients.
  3. Add wet to dry ingredients. Then mix in the zucchini.
  4. Fold in berries
  5. Pour into 4 small or 2 regular loaf pans
  6. Bake in 350 preheated oven for 50 min. Let cool for 20 min, then remove from pan.
