The ingredients needed to make Banana bread:
  1. Take 300 g flour
  2. Prepare 3/4 tsp salt
  3. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  4. Get 175 g sugar
  5. Prepare 100 g 8% Greek yogurt
  6. Get 170 g butter
  7. Take 2 eggs
  8. Make ready 3 bananas
  9. Get 1 bar chocolate
  10. Get 125 g nuts
  11. Get Vanilla

Instructions to make Banana bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 250 and prepare the pan
  2. Mix flour, salt and soda
  3. In a separate bowl, mix sugar, yogurt, room temperature butter, and vanilla
  4. 1 by 1 add the eggs
  5. Gradually add flour
  6. Add and mix in the bananas
  7. Add mix-ins
  8. Bake for about 50-60 minutes till brown to your likeness at 200-250
  9. Cover with foil and bake for another 10-20 minutes till done
