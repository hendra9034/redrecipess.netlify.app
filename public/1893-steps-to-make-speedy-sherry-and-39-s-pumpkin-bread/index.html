Steps to make Sherry's Pumpkin Bread:
  1. preheat oven 350'
  2. grease 3 LG loaf pans
  3. in large bowl mix sugar,eggs,veg oil,pumpkin, & water
  4. in seperate bowl sift flour,baking soda,baking powder,allspice,nutmeg,cinnamon,cloves,salt
  5. mix dry ingredients into wet ingredients
  6. mix in walnuts & raisins into batter
  7. divide batter evenly into 3 prepared loaf pans
  8. bake for 1 hour or until tester comes out clean
