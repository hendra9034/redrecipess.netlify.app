The ingredients needed to make Gluten Free Banana Bread (2 loaves):
  1. Prepare 1st Batch
  2. Prepare 6 Eggs
  3. Prepare 1 cup Canola Oil
  4. Get 3 Mashed Bananas
  5. Prepare 2nd Batch
  6. Prepare 1 cup Castor Sugar
  7. Get 1 cup Coconut
  8. Take 1 tbsp Cinnamon
  9. Take 2 tsp Bi-Carb
  10. Prepare 2 cup Gluten Free Self Raising Flour
Steps to make Gluten Free Banana Bread (2 loaves):
  1. Preheat oven to 160°F Celcius
  2. Add first batch of ingredients into a large bowl, mix thoroughly
  3. Combine the 2nd batch of ingredients into the first bowl, adding the flour last
  4. Pour mixture into two lined loaf tins
  5. Bake for 50 minutes or until a skewer comes out clean and firm to touch
  6. Wait until completely cooled before removing from tins, can be serve toasted and with butter
