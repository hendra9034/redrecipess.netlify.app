The ingredients needed to make Chocolate chunk banana bread:
  1. Get 1 1/2 large bananas
  2. Make ready 1 cup sugar
  3. Take 1 1/2 cup flour
  4. Take 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  5. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  6. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  7. Get 1 large egg
  8. Prepare 1 stick butter melted
  9. Get 1 pinch salt
  10. Make ready 1 cup chocolate chunks
  11. Make ready 1 tablespoons soy milk
  12. Take 1 teaspoon cinnamon

Steps to make Chocolate chunk banana bread:
  1. Preheat oven at 325 degrees
  2. Spray loaf pan with cooking spray. In a mixing bowl mash bananas with fork. Add egg,vanilla,soy milk,and melted butter and sugar,mix well.
  3. Add flour,baking soda,baking powder,and salt. Mix and then add chocolate chunks.
  4. Pour into loaf pan and bake until done. Let set 10 minutes. Enjoy
