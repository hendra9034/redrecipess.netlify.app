The ingredients needed to make Easy banana bread:
  1. Make ready 3 ripe banana, mashed
  2. Prepare 255 g (3/4 cup) honey
  3. Prepare 1 egg
  4. Make ready 2 tbsps almond milk
  5. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  6. Take 260 g (2 cups) whole wheat flour
  7. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
  8. Get 1 pinch salt

Instructions to make Easy banana bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 170 degrees (325 F)
  2. Mash bananas, add applesauce or greek yogurt, honey, egg, vanilla extract, almond milk. Whisk until well combined.
  3. In another bowl, mix whole wheat flour, baking soda and salt together.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and stir well
  5. Spray loaf pan with cooking spray or wipe down with oil. Pour mix into a loaf pan.
  6. Bake 50 to 55 minutes. Enjoy!
