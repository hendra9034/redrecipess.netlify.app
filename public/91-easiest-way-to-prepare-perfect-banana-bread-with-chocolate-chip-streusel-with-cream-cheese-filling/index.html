The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread with Chocolate Chip Streusel with Cream Cheese Filling:
  1. Take 2/3 cups chocolate chips
  2. Get 1/4 cup light brown sugar
  3. Get 1/2 cup chopped, walnuts
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
  5. Make ready 1 and 1/2 cup All Purpose Flour
  6. Get 3/4 tsp baking soda
  7. Prepare 3/4 tsp baking powder
  8. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  9. Take 3/4 white sugar (granulated)
  10. Prepare 1 stick unsalted butter, room temp
  11. Make ready 1 large egg
  12. Prepare 3 ripe bananas
  13. Prepare 3 tbsp milk
Instructions to make Banana Bread with Chocolate Chip Streusel with Cream Cheese Filling:
  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees Farenheit.
  2. For streusel, stri chocolate chips, brown sugar and walnuts together in a bowl.
  3. Mix all purpose flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt into a medium bowl.
  4. Beat sugar, butter and egg in a separate bowl until fluffy. Add mashed bananas and milk.
  5. Add dry ingredients and blend well.
  6. We're ready to bake them once you spread them evenly on the baking pan. Sprinkle with streusel.
  7. It's 50 minutes baking time for a 9 inch pan. For smaller ones, 40-45 minutes.
