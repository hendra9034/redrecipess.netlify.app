Steps to make White sauce pasta with garlic butter and cheese bread:
  1. Take a pan add water and keep on flame after boiling add salt and oil and add pasta and boil well and drain water and keep aside.
  2. Take a pan add 2 tbsp oil and 2 tbs butter and add garlic fry add chicken fry well add onion fry well add carrot and cook well and add salt red chilli flakes and black pepper, mix herbs and mix well add sweet corns and capsicum mix well and keep aside.
  3. Take a pan add 4 tbsp butter and add garlic and fry well add 4 tbsp flour mix and add milk slowly and mix well and cook add cheese and black pepper chilli flakes and herbs mix well add boiled pasta mix well and add mix vegetable chicken this stage check salt and pepper and ready for serve.
  4. Take a bread slices and 3 tbsp butter and garlic mix well and apply bread slices and keep in tossing and garnish cheese chilli flakes cutting 1 by 2
  5. Take a serving plate and put pasta and cheese garlic butter bread for garnishing with cheese
  6. Ready to serve with friends and family.
