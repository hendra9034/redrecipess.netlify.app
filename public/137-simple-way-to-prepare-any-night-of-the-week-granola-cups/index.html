The ingredients needed to make Granola cups:
  1. Take 1 cup granola
  2. Take 4 bread slices
  3. Get 1/2 cup yoghurt
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp honey
  5. Make ready 4 tbsp milk
  6. Prepare 1 Mango's chunks and 1 banana sliced

Steps to make Granola cups:
  1. Take muffin tins. Grease them.
  2. Take 1 bread slice. Sprinkle 1 tbsp milk over it to make it soft. Now put it in the muffin tin and set it properly.
  3. Repeat the process and prepare all 4 tins.
  4. Bake them on 180 degrees for 20 minutes or till crisp.
  5. Take them out of the oven and let them cool. Now demould them.
  6. Fill 1 spoonfull granola in it(for granola Please check my previous recipe). Now spread yoghurt and drizzle with honey. Prepare all with the same process.
  7. Top it with mango chunks and banana slices. You can use your choice of fruits.
  8. Granola cups are ready to serve.
