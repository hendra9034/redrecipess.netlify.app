The ingredients needed to make Banana bread:
  1. Take 1 3/4 cups flour
  2. Take 1 teaspoon baking powder
  3. Make ready 1 teaspoon baking soda
  4. Make ready 1/2-1 teaspoon cinnamon
  5. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon salt
  6. Make ready 1 stick butter softened
  7. Make ready 3/4 cup brown sugar
  8. Get 2 eggs
  9. Take 3 medium size banana

Steps to make Banana bread:
  1. Mix flour,baking powder, baking soda,salt and cinnamon set aside
  2. Mash bananas add eggs, butter, mix add brown sugar
  3. Add flour mix to wet mix well
  4. Fold in any walnuts chocolate chips etc at this time
  5. Pour into a bread pan (I sprinkled decorating sugar on top)
  6. Bake 350 for 30-45 minutes check at 30 if toothpick is clean done
