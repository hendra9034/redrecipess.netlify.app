The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Yeast Bread:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup warm water
  2. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp instant yeast
  3. Take 2/3 cup warm water
  4. Prepare 2 large eggs
  5. Take 1 1/2 cup pumpkin puree
  6. Get 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup brown sugar
  8. Get 2 tsp salt
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  10. Make ready 1/4 tsp ground ginger
  11. Get 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  12. Prepare 1 dash of cloves
  13. Make ready 6 1/2 cup bread flour ( approximately).

Instructions to make Pumpkin Yeast Bread:
  1. In a large bowl of an electric mixer fitted with paddle attachment. Combine warm water & yeast add milj , eggs, oil, br. Sugar, salt & spices. Mix briefly to combine. Add 4 cups of flour to the bowl & mix until a sticky dough has formed.Switch to dough hook.
  2. With the mixer on low speed gradually add remaining flour until you have a smooth dough that is smooth & elestic and not sticky. Transfer the dough to a oiled bowl. Turning once to coat dough. Cover the bowl & let rise until doubled. About 1 hour.
  3. Turn dough onto lightly floured surface. Divide dough in half. Press each into a retangle about about 1" thick & no longer than 9" long. With long sides of dough facing you roll the dough into a cyclinder. Pressing down to make sure dough sticks to itself.Turn the seam side down. Place loaves into a well greased 9x5" loaf pan.cover and let rise until almost doubled. About 45 minutes.
  4. Preheat oven to 375º . Bake the loaves for about 30 minutes. Transfer to wire rack to cool. Makes 2 - 9" loaves.
