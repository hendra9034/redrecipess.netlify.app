The ingredients needed to make Whole wheat banana bread:
  1. Prepare 4 ripe bananas
  2. Prepare 2 eggs
  3. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup butter
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  7. Get 1/2 cup sugar
  8. Get 1 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup brown sugar
  10. Get 1 1/2 cup milk
