Steps to make Jamaican Cocktail Bread Pudding:
  1. Heat oven at 350 ° F
  2. Grease a med bread pan with butter
  3. Combine bread cubes, cocktail mix and cinnamon in a container.
  4. Place coated cubes and fruits in Greased pan.
  5. Heat and cool milk then mix with all other ingredients for custard.
  6. Pour milk mixture over bread cubes.
  7. Allow to rest for 15-20mins so cubes may absorb the custard.
  8. Bake for 45-50mins
  9. Combine ingredients for glaze over flame in a small saucepan until slightly thicken.
  10. Take pudding from oven, brush with glaze and put to cool.
  11. ENJOY with a scoop of your favorite ice cream :-)
