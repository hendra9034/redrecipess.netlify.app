The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Bread with Cocoa:
  1. Make ready 1 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
  2. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  3. Get 1/4 cup cocoa powder (unsweetened)
  4. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  5. Take 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  6. Get 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  7. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  8. Get 1 cup pumpkin puree
  9. Take 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  10. Make ready 1/4 cup whole milk
  11. Prepare 1/4 cup water
  12. Make ready 2 eggs (beaten)
  13. Take 1 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  14. Take 20 almonds (chopped)
  15. Make ready 1 powdered sugar
Steps to make Pumpkin Bread with Cocoa:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°
  2. Mix together dry ingredients; flower, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt
  3. In a separate bowl beat (lightly) wet ingredients together; pumpkin puree, oil, milk, water, eggs, vanilla extract
  4. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients, mix with a spoon, and add in the chopped almonds (any nut of your choice)
  5. Pour mixture into a bread pan, 8" x 5.5"
  6. Bake for 60 minutes (toothpick test)
  7. Sift powdered sugar over the top, cut, and enjoy!!!
