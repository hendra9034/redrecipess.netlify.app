The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Banana Bread:
  1. Get Eggs
  2. Take Sugar
  3. Make ready Water
  4. Get Milk
  5. Take –29oz Canned Pumpkin
  6. Make ready Ripe Bananas
  7. Make ready Olive Oil
  8. Prepare Flour
  9. Make ready Baking Soda
  10. Take Salt
  11. Get Vanilla
  12. Take Nutmeg
  13. Prepare Cloves
  14. Prepare Cinnamon
  15. Get Ginger
  16. Make ready Mini Chocolate Chips (more if desired)
  17. Get Walnuts (optional)
Instructions to make Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Banana Bread:
  1. Beat eggs and sugar together. Add remaining ingredients EXCEPT chocolate chips and walnuts. Mix well.
  2. Add chocolate chips and walnuts. Put in lined muffin tins or greased bread pans.
  3. Bake in 350 degree oven for @ 20 minutes for muffins, @45 minutes for bread loaf pans.
  4. This makes a lot of bread for sharing.
