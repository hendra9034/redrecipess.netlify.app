The ingredients needed to make Jan's Zucchini Bread:
  1. Make ready 3 eggs
  2. Make ready 3 C flour
  3. Make ready 3 tsp vanilla
  4. Get 1 tsp salt
  5. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  6. Take 1 C oil
  7. Make ready 2 1/2 C sugar
  8. Make ready 2 C grated zicchini
  9. Take 1/4 tsp baking powder
  10. Get 3 tsp cinnamon
  11. Take Nuts and raisins optional
Instructions to make Jan's Zucchini Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease 2 loaf pans (9x5 or 10x4), flour lightly and shake out extra flour. Set aside.
  2. Sift all dry ingredients together in large bowl and set aside.
  3. Beat eggs and sugar together. Add oil, flavoring sand zucchini and mix. Slowly add dry ingredients. Add nuts and/or raisins, if using.
  4. Pour batter into two loaf pans and place in preheated oven. Bake for one hour, or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
