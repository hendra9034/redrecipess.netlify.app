The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread:
  1. Get 1/4 Cup salted butter (soft)
  2. Make ready 1 Cup sugar
  3. Take 2 egg
  4. Make ready 8 oz whipped cream cheese
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon Imitation Vanilla
  6. Prepare 4 bananas
  7. Get 2 1/4 Cup Flour
  8. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  9. Make ready 1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
Instructions to make Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Mix Flour, baking soda and baking powder in a bowl.
  3. In a different bowl mash bananas well, then add eggs and vanilla and mix together.
  4. Now slowly start adding flour mixture in slowly continuing to mix until all is combine and mixed well.
  5. In a third bowl combine butter, eggs, sugar, and cream cheese. Mix until smooth. Then add bananas and egg mixture. Mix it together with a electric mixer.
  6. Pour into greased bread pan, and bake for 75 min or so. Let cool around 5 min.
