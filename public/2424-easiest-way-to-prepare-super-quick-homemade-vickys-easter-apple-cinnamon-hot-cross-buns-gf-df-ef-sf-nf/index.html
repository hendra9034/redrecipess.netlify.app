The ingredients needed to make Vickys Easter Apple Cinnamon Hot Cross Buns, GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Make ready coconut milk, lukewarm
  2. Get fast action yeast
  3. Get sugar
  4. Prepare gluten-free flour / strong white bread flour
  5. Make ready & 1/2 tsp xanthan gum if using gluten-free flour
  6. Prepare salt
  7. Take mixed spice
  8. Take cinnamon
  9. Get large cooking apple, peeled, cored and finely chopped
  10. Prepare sultanas
  11. Prepare mixed peel
  12. Make ready - 100ml apple juice / orange juice
  13. Make ready olive oil
  14. Make ready Crosses
  15. Make ready gluten-free / plain flour
  16. Take water
  17. Get Glaze
  18. Make ready apple juice / orange juice
  19. Make ready caster sugar
Instructions to make Vickys Easter Apple Cinnamon Hot Cross Buns, GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Mix the yeast and sugar into the milk and set aside
  2. Mix the flour, xanthan gum if using, salt and baking spices together in a large bowl. Stir the sultanas, mixed peel and chopped apple pieces in
  3. Make a well in the centre and stir in the apple / orange juice, oil and yeast mixture
  4. Form a sticky but soft dough using your hands. Depending on the flour used you may need to add a little more milk as gluten-free flours need more liquid, 2 -3 tbsp should be plenty
  5. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for 5 - 10 minutes until the dough is smooth. Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover with a damp tea towel and leave in a warm place for an hour or until it has doubled in size
  6. Punch the dough down and knead again for 10 minutes then divide into 12 equal pieces
  7. Roll the pieces into smooth balls and evenly space out on a large baking tray lined with parchment paper. Recover with the damp tea towel and again, leave in a warm place for 45 minutes until doubled in size
  8. Preheat the oven to gas 6 / 200C / 400F
  9. To make the crosses, mix the flour and water to a paste the consistency of butter icing. Place the paste in an icing bag or plastic bag and snip the end off, then pipe crosses on the hot cross buns. It's easier to go along the whole row than do individual crosses
  10. Bake for 20 - 22 mins until the buns are golden and firm
  11. To make the glaze, dissolve the caster sugar in the juice, boil for a minute in the microwave or a small saucepan until thickening, then brush over the hot buns as soon as they come out of the oven. Let the glaze set before you enjoy the buns with safe butter, jam, marmalade, nutella or naked, whatever you fancy!
  12. Will keep for up to 3 days, best enjoyed halved and toasted
