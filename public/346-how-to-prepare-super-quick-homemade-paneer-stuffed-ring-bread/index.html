The ingredients needed to make Paneer stuffed ring bread:
  1. Take 1 cup all purpose flour/ wheat
  2. Prepare 1 tsp sugar/honey
  3. Take 2 tbsp oil
  4. Take 1 tsp dry yeast
  5. Make ready 2 tsp Salt
  6. Prepare Warm water/milk as required to knead the dough
  7. Get 3 cups Paneer cubes
  8. Get 1/4 cup Capsicum chopped
  9. Prepare 1/4 cup Onion chopped
  10. Take 1/2 cup Pizza sauce
  11. Get As needed Processed/Cheddar cheese according to ur taste
  12. Prepare Little Oregano/mixed herb
  13. Prepare 1 tsp Pepper
  14. Take as needed Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions to make Paneer stuffed ring bread:
  1. Add 1tsp dry yeast and 1tsp sugar warm milk. Keep it for 5 mins. Then add salt and 1tbsp oil and 1 cup wheat flour(atta) /maida to make a smooth dough. Knead it nicely and cover. keep it for 4/5 hrs, so that the dough will rise almost double.
  2. Preheat oven to 400Âº F (200C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. - Use a rolling pin to roll the dough out a bit to make it wider (rectangular shape).
  3. Use a knife/pizza cutter to cut thin strips all the way up to 2/3 of the length of the rolled dough.
  4. Spread pizza sauce over the remaining long uncut dough. spread some cheese. Then spread paneer stuffing(fry paneer cubes, capsicum,onion a little in oil adding little salt and pepper).Then sprinkle some oregano,pepper over it.
  5. Separate the dough strips a bit and start folding the dough with the filling all over the strips.
  6. Carefully transfer it to the prepared baking sheet, form into a circle and seal the edges. - Brush the top of the ring with beaten egg/milk and sprinkle Parmesan cheese if desired.
