The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin chocolate bread pudding with caramel sauce:
  1. Prepare 1 cup Heavy cream
  2. Take 3/4 cup Whole milk
  3. Get 3 large eggs
  4. Take 1/4 tsp Ground ginger
  5. Make ready 6 cup Crusty or day old bread, cut into 1 inch cubes
  6. Make ready 1 cup Dark chocolate chips
  7. Take 4 egg yolks
  8. Make ready 3 oz bourbon
  9. Take 1 pinch salt
  10. Make ready 1 cup canned pumpkin
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
  12. Get 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  13. Get 1/8 tsp nutmeg
  14. Take 1 cup toasted pumpkins
  15. Take 1 unsalted butter stick melted
  16. Prepare 4 tbsp sugar (for sabayon)
  17. Take 2 cup heavy cream (for sabayon)
