The ingredients needed to make Banana nut bread:
  1. Take 1/2 cup butter or shortening
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Prepare 2 eggs
  4. Take 21/2 cups flour sifted
  5. Prepare 1 cup chopped nuts
  6. Prepare 3 large bananas put through a sieve about a 11/2 cups
  7. Take 1/2 teaspoons soda
  8. Get 2 teaspoons baking powder
  9. Prepare 3/4 teaspoons salt
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup buttermilk

Steps to make Banana nut bread:
  1. Cream the shortening slowly add the sugar and beat well
  2. Add the eggs one at a time beat thoroughly
  3. Add the bananas and nuts
  4. Alternately add the dry ingredients and the milk
  5. Pour into large loaf pan or 2 small ones
  6. Bake at 350 for 1 hour
