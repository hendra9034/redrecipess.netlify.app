The ingredients needed to make Easy Lunchbox

How to make Banana Bread:

  1. Prepare 2-3 very ripe bananas mashed
  2. Get 1/3 cup oil or melted butter
  3. Get 1 teaspoons baking soda
  4. Make ready Pinch salt
  5. Prepare 3/4 cup sugar
  6. Make ready 1 large egg,beaten
  7. Take 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour

Instructions to make Easy Lunchbox

How to make Banana Bread:

  1. Preheat oven to 180C and butter the loaf pan (i uses a pan that makes six individual mini loafs
  2. Add together the mashed banana and butter together
  3. Mix in baking soda and salt.Stir in the sugar and beaten egg.Mix in the flours
  4. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan. bake for 50 min to 1 hour or until toothpick or knife comes out clean
  5. Remove from the oven and let cool.Make sure that it’s completely cooled before you cut them
