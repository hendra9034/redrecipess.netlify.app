The ingredients needed to make Apple white cheddar grilled cheese:
  1. Get 4 slices whole wheat cinnamon raisin bread
  2. Get 4 slices sharp cheddar cheese 3 oz
  3. Prepare 1 small apple thinly sliced
  4. Make ready 1 thin slice red onion separate into rings
  5. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes optional
  6. Take 1 tablespoon butter

Instructions to make Apple white cheddar grilled cheese:
  1. Layer each of two bread slices with 1 slice cheese
  2. Top with the apple and onoin
  3. If desired sprinkle with pepper flakes
  4. Top with the remaining cheese and bread
  5. Spread outside of sandwiches with butter
  6. In a large skillet toast sandwiches over medium low heat 3 to 5 minutes on each side or until golden brown and cheese is melted.
