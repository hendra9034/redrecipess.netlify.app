The ingredients needed to make Pecan-Cranberry Bread Pudding:
  1. Get 2 large eggs
  2. Get 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  3. Get 1/2 cup butter, melted
  4. Prepare 3/4 cup half & half
  5. Take 1 tsp vanilla
  6. Make ready 1 1/2 cup chopped, toasted pecans, divided
  7. Get 1 1/2 cup fresh cranberries, divided
  8. Make ready 1 loaf (16 ounces) raisin bread, cut into 1-inch cubes
  9. Take Lemon-Vanilla Hard Sauce
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup butter, softened
  11. Prepare 1 tsp lemon zest
  12. Make ready 1 tbsp lemon juice
  13. Make ready 1 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  14. Take 1 tbsp vanilla
Instructions to make Pecan-Cranberry Bread Pudding:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Lightly grease 8x8-inch pan.
  3. In large mixing bowl, stir together eggs, sugar, butter, half & half, vanilla, 1 cup pecans, 1 cup cranberries, and cubed bread.
  4. Spoon into prepared pan. Bake for 25 minutes.
  5. For Lemon-Vanilla Hard Sauce:
  6. Cream butter, zest and juice on medium speed with electric mixer.
  7. Gradually add powdered sugar and vanilla, beating until light and fluffy.
  8. When done baking… Immediately top with Lemon-Vanilla Hard Sauce and allow to melt.
  9. Sprinkle with reserved cranberries and pecans. Serve.
  10. Cooks Tip: Try Lemon-Vanilla Hard Sauce over other warm baked goods such as scones or muffins.
