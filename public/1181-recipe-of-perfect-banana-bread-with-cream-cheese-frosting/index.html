The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread with Cream Cheese Frosting:
  1. Make ready 4 piece banana,mashed
  2. Make ready 2/3 cup butter, melted
  3. Take 1 cup sugar
  4. Take 1/4 cup evaporated milk
  5. Take 2 cup all-purpose flour
  6. Take 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  7. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  8. Make ready 1 cup salt
  9. Make ready 2 piece egg
  10. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  11. Get 1/4 cup evaporated milk
Instructions to make Banana Bread with Cream Cheese Frosting:
  1. Pre-heat the oven 175°C
  2. In a bowl add the mashed banana, butter, sugar and milk.Mix well
  3. In another bowl, mix all the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.Put this mixture to the previous one and mix for about 2minutes.
  4. Gradually add the egg, vanilla extract, and another 1/4 cup of milk.mix for 1minutes in the mixer
  5. Transfer the mixture into the baking pan and bake 30 to 40 minutes or until the bread is fully cook
