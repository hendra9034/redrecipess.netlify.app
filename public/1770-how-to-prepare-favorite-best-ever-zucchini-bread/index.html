The ingredients needed to make Best ever zucchini bread:
  1. Take 2 cup grated zucchini
  2. Prepare 4 large eggs
  3. Take 2/3 cup oil
  4. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 cup sugar
  6. Make ready 3 cup all-purpose flour
  7. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  8. Take 4 1/2 tsp baking powder
  9. Get 1 cup walnuts (optional)

Steps to make Best ever zucchini bread:
  1. preheat oven to 350
  2. mix wet ingredients with sugar
  3. in a separate bowl, mix dry ingredients
  4. add dry ingredients to wet ingredients, mix well
  5. stir in nuts, if desired
  6. divided batter equally into 2 greased loaf pans or one 9X13 pan
  7. bake for 1 hour or til toothpick inserted near center comes out clean
