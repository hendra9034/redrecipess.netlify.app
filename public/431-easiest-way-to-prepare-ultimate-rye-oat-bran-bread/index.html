The ingredients needed to make Rye Oat Bran Bread:
  1. Prepare dry ingredients
  2. Prepare 1 cup bread flour
  3. Get 1 cup rye flour
  4. Get 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup oat bran
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp sugar
  7. Take 1 tbsp dry milk
  8. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  9. Prepare 3 tbsp butter
  10. Make ready liquids
  11. Get 3/4 cup plain kefir + 1 egg
  12. Take 1/2 cup milk
  13. Prepare yeast dispenser
  14. Get 2 tsp bread machine yeast
Steps to make Rye Oat Bran Bread:
  1. Put all the dry ingredients in the bread machine in the order listed
  2. Add liquids
  3. Put yeast in the yeast dispenser
  4. Set bread machine for small loaf light crust
