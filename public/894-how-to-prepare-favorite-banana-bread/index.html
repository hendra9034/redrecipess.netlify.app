The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread:
  1. Get 3 very ripe bananas
  2. Take 1/3 cup melted butter
  3. Prepare 1 teaspoon baking soda
  4. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon salt
  5. Take 3/4 cup brown sugar
  6. Get 1 large egg beaten
  7. Take 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  8. Get 1 1/2 cup white flour

Steps to make Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 and butter a 4x8 loaf pan
  2. Mash banana and add melted butter
  3. Mix in baking soda, salt, brown sugar, egg, vanilla extract and flour
  4. Put into loaf pan and bake for 50-60 minutes
