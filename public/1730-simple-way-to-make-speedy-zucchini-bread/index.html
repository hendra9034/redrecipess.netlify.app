The ingredients needed to make Zucchini Bread:
  1. Prepare 3 large eggs
  2. Take 1 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  3. Make ready 2 cup zucchini, grated
  4. Get 2 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Take 3 cup all-purpose flour
  6. Make ready 2 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  7. Take 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  8. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking powder
  10. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  11. Take 1 cup chopped walnuts
  12. Get 1/2 cup raisins
  13. Take 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
Steps to make Zucchini Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 325°F
  2. Mix eggs with sugar
  3. Add zucchini, melted butter, and vanilla extract
  4. Mix flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, baking soda, baking powder and salt
  5. Add dry ingredients to egg mixture
  6. Add nuts and raisins
  7. Pour in pans and bake for 60-70 minutes
