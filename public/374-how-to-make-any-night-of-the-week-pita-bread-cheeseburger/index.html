The ingredients needed to make Pita Bread Cheeseburger:
  1. Prepare 2 slices mini pita Bread whole wheat
  2. Take 1-1/3 pound ground sirloin formed into 4 patties
  3. Get to taste iceberg lettuce
  4. Prepare 4 slices provolone cheese
  5. Make ready 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  6. Prepare 2 tablespoons tomato ketchup
  7. Prepare 1/2 large kosher pickle sliced lengthwise
  8. Make ready 1 teaspoon yellow prepared mustard
  9. Get as needed ground black pepper
  10. Take as needed salt
  11. Get as needed sliced tomato
Instructions to make Pita Bread Cheeseburger:
  1. Preheat oven 400 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Season the meat and form patties. I salted and peppered the pan underneath the patties.
  3. Bake till done add cheese slices on top.
  4. Slice carefully the two pita Bread slices. Open like a bun and build the cheeseburger with the condiments
  5. Slice in half and serve. I hope you enjoy!
