The ingredients needed to make Bread pizza:
  1. Make ready 4 slices whole wheat bread
  2. Prepare 3-4 tsp butter
  3. Prepare 4tsp pizza sauce
  4. Make ready 4 tsp chopped onions, capsicum,
  5. Get 1/4 cup olives
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
  7. Take 1/2 tsp oregano,chilli flakes
