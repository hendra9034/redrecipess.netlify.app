The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin bread:
  1. Prepare 2 cup flour
  2. Prepare 1 cup brown sugar
  3. Take 1/4 cup sugar
  4. Prepare 2 large eggs
  5. Take 2 egg whites
  6. Take 1 cup vegetable oil
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup milk
  8. Prepare 2 tsp backing soda
  9. Get 2 pinch salt
  10. Get 3/4 tsp allspice
  11. Make ready 3/4 tsp ground cloves
  12. Take 3/4 tsp nutmeg
  13. Take 1/2 tsp Pumpkin pie spice
  14. Get 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  15. Take 1 can Pumpkin pureed (15oz)

Instructions to make Pumpkin bread:
  1. Pre hit the oven at 350 ° , and grise your backing pans or backing pan.
  2. In an small bowl sift together the flour , backing soda , salt , ground allspice, ground cloves, nutmeg, pumpkin pie spice, and cinnamon.
  3. in a larger bowl with an electric mixer mix together the eggs and egg whites with the brown sugar and sugar. mix until it's a little bit of fluffy.
  4. Keep mixing and add the oil. Keep mixing till well combine.
  5. Add the dry ingredients and the milk , alternating between the milk and the flour mixture. Mix well till combine.( Note you should have a fluffy mixture. )
  6. Add the can of pumpkin puree and mix well till combine. Bake for 30-40 min., or till the toothpick comes out clean .
