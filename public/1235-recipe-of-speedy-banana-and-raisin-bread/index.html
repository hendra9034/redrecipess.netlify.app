The ingredients needed to make Banana and Raisin Bread:
  1. Prepare 3 pcs banana
  2. Make ready 2 small boxes of raisins
  3. Prepare 1 + 1/2 cups flour
  4. Make ready 1 tsp yeast
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp sugar
  7. Take 2 eggs
  8. Get Fresh milk
  9. Prepare Melted butter
Instructions to make Banana and Raisin Bread:
  1. Dissolve the yeast in a bit of warm water. When it is fluffy mix it. Then combine all the ingredients. And mix properly. Grease the ceramic with butter and pour mixture.
  2. Heat oven at 250°C (480°F) and bake it for 40 mins. Insert a toothpick. If the toothpick comes out clean it's ready.
