The ingredients needed to make Strawberry-Banana Bread:
  1. Make ready 1 C chopped strawberries
  2. Get 2 bananas; chopped
  3. Take 2 eggs
  4. Prepare 1.5 C flour
  5. Make ready 1 C sugar
  6. Get 1/2 C vegetable oil
  7. Prepare 1 t baking soda
  8. Prepare 1 t vanilla extract
  9. Make ready 1/4 t kosher salt

Instructions to make Strawberry-Banana Bread:
  1. Whisk dry ingredients together.
  2. Combine strawberries, bananas, oil, vanilla, and eggs together in a mixing bowl. Mix until smooth.
  3. Slowly combine wet ingredients into dry ingredients until smooth.
  4. Bake at 350° for 1 hour.
