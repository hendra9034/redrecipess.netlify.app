The ingredients needed to make Brad's kefta kabobs:
  1. Take 1 lb ground beef
  2. Take 1 lb ground lamb
  3. Take 1 1/2 tsp turmeric
  4. Get 1 tsp each garlic powder, Chile powder, cumin, sea salt
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp white pepper
  6. Make ready .5 oz chopped cilantro
  7. Take .5 oz chopped fresh mint leaves
  8. Prepare Hummus
  9. Make ready Thin sliced shallots
  10. Get Thin sliced tomato
  11. Prepare Crumbled goat cheese
  12. Take Pita flatbread or naan bread

Instructions to make Brad's kefta kabobs:
  1. Mix meat, spices, cilantro, and mint in a lg mixing bowl. Form into thin long patties. Grill over charcoal until cooked.
  2. In a hot dry pan cook bread on each side until warm and soft.
  3. Spread plenty of hummus on bread. Top with shallot, tomato, and goat cheese. Serve immediately. Enjoy.
