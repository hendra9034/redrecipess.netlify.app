The ingredients needed to make Carol's 100% Organic Whole Wheat Bread:
  1. Prepare 8-8 1/2 cups organic whole wheat flour
  2. Get 3/4 oz Active Dry Yeast
  3. Take 4 teaspoon salt
  4. Make ready 3 cups water
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup dark molasses
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup vegetable oil
Steps to make Carol's 100% Organic Whole Wheat Bread:
  1. Start with organic natural product 1. In a large mixer bowl. Add 3 1/2 cups of flour, yeast and salt. Mix well
  2. Small pan on low. Add 3 cups water, 1/2 cups molasses and 1/4 cup oil. Using a thermometer keep checking temp until between 120°F and 130°F. Then add warmed liquid to the dry mixture slowly, stir setting on the mixer.
  3. Slowly add 5 cups of flour. Switch to the dough hook when necessary. Knead the dough on the low setting for 5 to 8 minutes.
  4. Proof to double the size with rise in a large greased bowl.
  5. Punch down dough. Divide into 2 rounds. Place each load in 9 inch round cake pan. Slice cuts on top or not.
  6. Proof again to double the size.
  7. Bake at 375°F for 40 to 45 minutes.
  8. Rack and cool
