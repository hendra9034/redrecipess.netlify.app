The ingredients needed to make Honey and Whole Wheat Bagels:
  1. Prepare 220 grams Bread (strong) flour
  2. Prepare 30 grams Whole wheat flour
  3. Prepare 15 grams Honey
  4. Get 4 1/2 grams Salt
  5. Take 133 grams plus Soy milk (unprocessed)
  6. Get 2 grams Instant dry yeast
  7. Take For kettling
  8. Prepare 1 liter Hot water
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp Honey
Instructions to make Honey and Whole Wheat Bagels:
  1. Put all ingredients into a bowl and knead by putting your weight into it.
  2. After 10 to 15 minutes of kneading, divide into 5 portions and let rest for 10 minutes.
  3. Roll out until long and thin. Roll up tightly from the edge nearest you and seal the seam tightly. Roll into a 20 cm stick.
  4. Press down one end of the roll to flatten it. Twist the roll twice, and fold the non-flattened edge inwards. Close the seam well and let rise for 30 minutes.
  5. Bring water to a boil in a frying pan and add the honey. Boil both sides for 30 seconds each.
  6. Pat the bagels dry. Line on a baking tray and bake for 16 to 18 minutes in a 210â„ƒ oven.
  7. Enjoy with a smear or cream cheese or as a sandwich.
