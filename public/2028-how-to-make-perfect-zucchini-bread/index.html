Steps to make Zucchini Bread:
  1. Grease & Flour two 9-inch loaf pans
  2. Preheat oven to 350
  3. In large bowl, mix flour, salt, soda, powder, cinnamon & nutmeg.
  4. Add eggs, sugars, oil & vanilla.
  5. Mix thoroughly.
  6. Fold in zucchini.
  7. Split batter evenly into the two prepared loaf pans.
  8. Bake 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into center comes out clean.
  9. (for four small loaves, use 7-inch pans & Bake for 35-45 minutes)
