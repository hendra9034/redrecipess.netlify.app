Steps to make low calorie corn bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 400°F
  2. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl. Set aside.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk all wet ingredients together.
  4. Pour wet ingredients in bowl with dry ingredients. Stir until just combined.
  5. Pour into greased muffin pan, bread pan, or round cake pan.
  6. Bake for 20 to 30 minutes.
