The ingredients needed to make Homemade white bread:
  1. Prepare 2.5 cup All purpose flour
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp Milk powder
  3. Take 1 tbsp Sugar
  4. Take 1 + 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  5. Take 1/2 TSP salt
  6. Make ready 1 cup warm water
  7. Get 4-5 tbsp Refind oil,butter
