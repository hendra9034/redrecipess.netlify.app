The ingredients needed to make Banana bread:
  1. Make ready 2 bananas
  2. Make ready 1 cup flour(I used cornflour and wholewheat flour 50:50)
  3. Take 2 tbsp sugarless(dutch)cocoa powder
  4. Get 2 tsp cinnamon
  5. Get 1/2 cup nuts(I used only walnut)
  6. Take 1 tsp oil(olive/coconut or any other type of oil)
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup water
  8. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla essence
  9. Make ready 1/2 packet baking powder
  10. Get 1 pinch salt
  11. Get 2 tbsp sweetener(I used eritrit)

Instructions to make Banana bread:
  1. Smash the bananas with a fork in a large bowl.
  2. Then add all the other ingredients to the banana and mix them well.
  3. Take this mix into a tray which is covered with baking paper and a bit of butter or oil. Bake it for about 30 minutes at 200°C.
