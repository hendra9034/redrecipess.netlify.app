The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin bread with Maple Cream Cheese filling:
  1. Make ready 1 Filling: 2 pkg (8oz each) cream cheese, softened
  2. Make ready 1/4 sugar
  3. Get 1 egg
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp milk
  5. Take 1 tsp maple extract OR 1T maple syrup
  6. Get 1 Bread: 3c. sugar
  7. Get 1 3/4 cup pumpkin puree OR 1 can solid-packed pumpkin
  8. Make ready 1 can canola oil
  9. Make ready 1 cup water
  10. Make ready 4 eggs
  11. Make ready 4 cup all-purpose flour
  12. Prepare 4 tsp pumpkin pie spice
  13. Get 2 tsp baking soda
  14. Get 1 tsp baking pwdr
  15. Take 1 tsp salt
  16. Take 1/2 cup chopped pecans or walnuts

Instructions to make Pumpkin bread with Maple Cream Cheese filling:
  1. Make filling by beating cream cheese, sugar, egg & milk in small bowl until creamy. Add maple flavoring & blend. Set aside.
  2. In large bowl, beat sugar, pumpkin, oil, water & eggs. In another bowl, mix flour, baking soda, baking pwdr, salt & spice.
  3. Gradually add dry ingredients to wet ones. Stir in nuts
  4. Pour half batter into three greased/lightly floured 8x4in loaf pans
  5. Spoon filling over the batter. Use a spatula to spread evenly.
  6. Add remaining batter, making sure to the cover the filling
  7. Bake @ 350° for 50min or until toothpick inserted near center comes out of clean. Cool 10min in pans before removing to wire rack to the cool completely.
  8. Store in fridge wrapped in plastic wrap
