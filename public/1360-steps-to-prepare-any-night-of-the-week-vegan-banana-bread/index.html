The ingredients needed to make Vegan Banana Bread:
  1. Take 1 1/2 cups flour
  2. Take 5 ripe bananas
  3. Get 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking soda
  5. Make ready 3 tbsp cocoa powder
  6. Make ready 1 pinch salt
  7. Make ready 1/4 cup coconut sugar
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp coconut oil
  9. Take Nuts

Instructions to make Vegan Banana Bread:
  1. Mix the flour, the baking soda, the baking powder the pinch of salt and the cocoa powder in a bowl.
  2. Smash the ripe bananas in other bowl and add the sugar and the coconut oil.
  3. Add the dry ingredients to the banana mix little by little.
  4. Mix everything and add the nuts. Put all of that in a mold for baking.
  5. Preheat the oven 180C / 350F
  6. Put the mold in the oven and wait 45-60 min.
  7. Let it get cold and enjoy!
