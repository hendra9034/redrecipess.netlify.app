The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread (Moist) w Whey Protein:
  1. Prepare 3 banana
  2. Prepare 2 eggs
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup olive oil
  4. Prepare 2 tablespoons honey
  5. Get 30 gr whey protein
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup yogurt
  8. Get 1/4 cup chia seeds
  9. Take 1 teaspoon baking powder
  10. Take 1 1/2 cup flour
Steps to make Banana Bread (Moist) w Whey Protein:
  1. Pre heat oven 170C
  2. Smash bananas until smooth
  3. Mix all ingredients in a bowl and mix
  4. Pour the mixture into the tray and put it in the oven.
  5. Heat the mixture for 50-55 min
  6. Enjoy!
