The ingredients needed to make Bread Zucchini Coins:

  2. Prepare 2 large Zucchini
  3. Make ready 1 small boiled and mashed potato
  4. Make ready 3 spoons mashed paneer
  5. Make ready 2-3 crushed Garlic
  6. Make ready 1 small onion finely chopped
  7. Get 1 teaspoon All-purpose flour
  8. Make ready 4-5 green chillies chopped.
  9. Take to taste Salt
  10. Get as needed Bread crumbs (you can make from the edges of the bread)
  11. Take 4 tbsp olive oil

Steps to make Bread Zucchini Coins:
  1. Grate Zucchini
  2. Cut the bread slices with help of cutter.
  3. Toast the bread slices on pan. Keep the flame low.
  4. Take another pan add olive oil. To it add garlic and saute for one min. Now goes onions. Saute them for about 2 mins.
  5. Now add grated Zucchini and saute it is cooked. It will take 3 to 4 mins as Zucchini cooks fast. Add salt and pepper also.
  6. Now add one spoon of All-purpose flour and cook.
  7. Take out in one plate and let it come to room temperature. Add paneer and potatoes and bread crumbs.
  8. Now apply this mixture on all the toasted bread. Keep a pan add oil. And put in on the pan with mixture facing down. Keep flame medium low.
  9. Roast it well till the mixture is golden brown.
  10. Serve immediately with some chutney or Ketchup as bread might get soggy.
