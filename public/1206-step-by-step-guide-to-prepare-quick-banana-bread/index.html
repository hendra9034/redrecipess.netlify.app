The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread:
  1. Get 2 overly ripe bananas, mashed
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp butter, melted
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  4. Prepare 1 egg, beaten
  5. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  6. Make ready 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  7. Get 2/4 tsp baking powder
  8. Take 2/4 tsp baking soda
  9. Make ready 2/4 tsp cinnamon powder
  10. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  11. Take 1/3 cup dried cranberries
  12. Take 1/3 cup walnuts

Instructions to make Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 175 degrees.
  2. Mix banana, sugar, butter and egg until well combined. Add vanilla and mix.
  3. Add all the dry ingredients flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon powder and salt. Mix until well combined. Do not over mix so that the bread is not too dense.
  4. Add the dried cranberries. Mix until thoroughly incorporated.
  5. Grease the pan and pour in the batter. Top with walnuts and baked for 35-45 minutes until top is golden brown.
