The ingredients needed to make Easy Banana Bread:
  1. Make ready 3 ripe bananas
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup melted butter
  3. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  4. Make ready Pinch salt
  5. Make ready 3/4 cup sugar
  6. Prepare 1 large egg, beaten
  7. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  8. Take 1.5 cups AP flour

Instructions to make Easy Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C), and butter a 4x8-inch loaf pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until completely smooth. Stir the melted butter into the mashed bananas.
  3. Mix in the baking soda and salt. Stir in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract. Mix in the flour.
  4. Pour the batter into your prepared loaf pan. Bake for 50 minutes to 1 hour at 350°F (175°C), or until a tester inserted into the center comes out clean.
  5. Remove from oven and let cool in the pan for a few minutes. Then remove the banana bread from the pan and let cool completely before serving. Slice and serve. (A bread knife helps to make slices that aren't crumbly.)
