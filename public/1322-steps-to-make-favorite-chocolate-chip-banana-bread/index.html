The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Chip Banana Bread:
  1. Take 120 g all purpose flour
  2. Get 10 g Baking powder
  3. Prepare 100 g unsalted butter
  4. Take 100 g sugar
  5. Prepare 1 egg
  6. Take 2 overripe banana (mash 1 and1/2 banana. 1/2 banana for the topping)
  7. Get 70 g Chocolate Chip

Steps to make Chocolate Chip Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 340 degrees F (170 degrees C).Place the parchment paper in a 9inch×3inch (about 18cm × 7.5cm) loaf pan.
  2. Shift flour and baking powder together. In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugar. Stir in Egg third at a time, Mix 1 and 1/2 mashed banana.
  3. Combine dry ingredients with a spatula. Add chocolate chip and mix together.
  4. Pour the mixture into the pan. Place the remaining banana on the top.
  5. Bake for about 30 to 40 minutes until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean. If you it starts to brown on the top, cover with aluminum foil.
