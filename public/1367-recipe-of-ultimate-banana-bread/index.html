The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread:
  1. Get 1 1/2 cup flour
  2. Take 1 cup sugar
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup butter
  4. Prepare 2 eggs
  5. Get 4 bananas
  6. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  7. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Steps to make Banana Bread:
  1. Whisk Butter and sugar until creamy
  2. Add 2 beaten eggs, bananas, and vanilla extract
  3. Mix flour, baking soda and salt separately then mix with the banana batter
  4. Put in the greased pan, bake in 175 degree for 55-60 minute or until a skewer inserted come out clean.
