The ingredients needed to make pumpkin bread:
  1. Take 3 cup sugar
  2. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking powder
  3. Make ready 2 tsp baking soda
  4. Take 2 tsp salt
  5. Take 1 tsp ground cloves, cinnamon, nutmeg
  6. Prepare 1 cup oil
  7. Make ready 4 eggs
  8. Get 3 1/2 cup flour
  9. Make ready 2 cup pumpkin
  10. Make ready 1 cup raisins ( soaked and drained)

Steps to make pumpkin bread:
  1. mix oil, eggs and pumpkin
  2. mix all dry ingredients
  3. add to wet ingredients slowly
  4. mix in raisins
  5. grease and flour 3 coffee cans. ( one pound size)
  6. add mix to can about 2/3 way up
  7. bake @ 350 for 1 hour
