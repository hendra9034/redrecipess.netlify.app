The ingredients needed to make Impossible Banana Bread Coffee Cake:
  1. Get 3 large very ripe bananas smashed (1 1/3 cups)
  2. Take 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup milk
  4. Prepare 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  5. Take 1 tsp cinnamon
  6. Get 3 eggs
  7. Make ready 2 and 2/3 cups original Bisquick mix
  8. Get 3/4 cup chopped walnuts or pecans
  9. Make ready Streusel topping
  10. Take 1 cup Bisquick mix
  11. Get 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or oecans
  12. Take 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  13. Take 6 tablespoons butter, cut into small pieces
  14. Prepare If desired Serve with
  15. Take Maple syrup
Steps to make Impossible Banana Bread Coffee Cake:
  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Prep a 9 “ x 13 “ pan with cooking spray or shortening
  2. In a large bowl, stir mashed bananas, sugar,milk, oil, eggs and cinnamon. Stir in 2 2/3 cups Bisquick mix and 3/4 cup nuts, fold in the bananas. Pour mixture into pan.
  3. In a medium bowl, mix the streusel ingredients. Using two forks or a pastry blender blend the streusel until it is crumbly. Sprinkle over mixture in pan.
  4. Bake 30-36 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. Drizzle with maple syrup before serving.
  5. Make ahead Directions; Make as directed thru step 3. Cover and refrigerate up to 12 hours. Uncover and bake as directed.
