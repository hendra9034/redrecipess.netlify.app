The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Zucchini Bread:
  1. Prepare 2 cup granulated sugar
  2. Prepare 1 cup vegetable oil
  3. Make ready 3 large eggs
  4. Get 3 tsp vanilla
  5. Take 2 1/2 cup flour
  6. Take 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  7. Take 1 tsp salt
  8. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  9. Get 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  10. Prepare 1/4 tsp baking powder
  11. Take 2 cup shredded zucchini

Steps to make Chocolate Zucchini Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. In a large bowl, beat eggs, oil, sugar, and vanilla.
  3. Mix in remaining ingredients, blending until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into 2 lightly greased loaf pans and bake for 50-55 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into center comes out clean.
