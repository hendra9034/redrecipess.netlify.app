The ingredients needed to make Whole wheat banana bread:
  1. Get 1 3/4 cups whole wheat flour
  2. Take 1/2 cup honey
  3. Prepare 1/3 cup coconut oil
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup milk
  5. Take 2 eggs
  6. Prepare 1 cup mashed bananas
  7. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  8. Take 1 tsp vanilla
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  10. Make ready 1/2 tsp cinnamon
