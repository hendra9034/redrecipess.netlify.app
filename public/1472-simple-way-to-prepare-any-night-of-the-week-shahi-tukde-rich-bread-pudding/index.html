The ingredients needed to make SHAHI TUKDE (rich bread pudding):
  1. Take 7 bread cut triangles (toasted it with or without ghee)
  2. Make ready (2 cups milk few saffron strings) Cream milk
  3. Take 2 cardamom
  4. Take 1 tbsn sugar
  5. Take 2 tbsn cornflour dissolved with milk
  6. Prepare Sugar syrup:
  7. Get 1 cup sugar (can add less)
  8. Take 1 cup water few strings
  9. Prepare Saffron,
  10. Make ready 2 cardamom and pinch of yellow colour
  11. Get Mawa milk:
  12. Make ready 1 tbsn ghee
  13. Take 1/2 cup milk
  14. Get 2 tbsn milk powder
  15. Get 1 tbsn cream
  16. Prepare 1 tsp sugar

Steps to make SHAHI TUKDE (rich bread pudding):
  1. Make the sugar syrup with the above ingredients for sugar syrup. Now make cream milk(like custard) boil 2 cup of milk with saffron, cardamom, sugar then add the dissolved cornflour and mix well till little thick and creamy.
  2. Now make mawa milk… heat ghee on the pan and add milk, let it boil.. now mix milk powder, then add cream and sugar and mix.remove from heat.
  3. ASSEMBLE the toasted bread in deep dish, coat the bread with warm sugar syrup (all syrup)now pour the warm cream milk.
