The ingredients needed to make Strawberry zucchini bread with Walnut streusel:
  1. Make ready 2 cups baking flour or whole wheat
  2. Make ready 1 teaspoon baking powder
  3. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon salt
  4. Prepare 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon
  5. Take 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  6. Get 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  7. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon Ginger
  8. Make ready 1 cup grated zucchini
  9. Get 1/2 cup sliced strawberries
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup pure maple syrup
  11. Make ready 1/4 cup canola oil
  12. Make ready 1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
  13. Take 1 egg
  14. Take 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  15. Get 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
  16. Get 1 tbsp brown sugar

Instructions to make Strawberry zucchini bread with Walnut streusel:
  1. Preheat oven to 350. Spray a 9 by 4 inch bread pan with nonstick spray.
  2. Grate zucchini on cutting board or in bowl. Place zucchini on paper towel and squeeze to drain some of the liquid to prevent bread from getting too wet
  3. In a large bowl, combine all dry ingredients and mix well. In a small bowl, combine all wet ingredients, adding zucchini and strawberries last. Add in dry ingredients to wet, folding and slowly until fully incorporated.
  4. Combine walnuts and brown sugar in a small bowl and sprinkle on top of bread. Bake for 45 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. Remove from oven and let bread sit in pan for 10 to 15 minutes then transfer to a cooling rack.
