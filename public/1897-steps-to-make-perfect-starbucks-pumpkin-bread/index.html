The ingredients needed to make Starbucks Pumpkin Bread:
  1. Make ready 1 cup sugar
  2. Make ready 1 cup brown sugar
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup smooth applesauce
  4. Take 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  5. Take 3 eggs
  6. Make ready 1 can pumpkin
  7. Prepare 1 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  8. Take 1 1/2 cup wheat flour
  9. Get 2 tsp ground cloves
  10. Get 4 tsp ground cinnamon
  11. Get 2 tsp ground nutmeg
  12. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  13. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  14. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking powder

Instructions to make Starbucks Pumpkin Bread:
  1. preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Butter and flour two 9x5x3 loaf pans or muffin pan
  3. Beat sugar and oil in a large bowl to blend
  4. Mix in eggs, pumpkin, and applesauce
  5. Sift flour, cloves, cinnamon, nutmeg, baking soda, salt, and baking powder into another large bowl
  6. Stir into pumpkin mixtures
  7. Divide batter among pans
  8. Bake for 1 hour, or until toothpick comes out clean
