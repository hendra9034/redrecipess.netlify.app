The ingredients needed to make Multi grain Cheeza Bread:
  1. Take 1/4 Cup Soya Flour
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup ragi Flour
  3. Take 1/4 cup rajgeera Flour
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup whole wheat Flour
  5. Prepare 1 cup all purpose Flour, extra for dusting
  6. Prepare 1 and 1/2 tsp fresh Yeast
  7. Make ready 1 and 1/2 tbsp Sugar
  8. Take 3/4 tsp Salt or according to taste
  9. Take 1/2 tsp Garlic powder
  10. Take 1/2 cup lukewarm Water
  11. Get 1/2 tsp Onion powder
  12. Get 1/2 tsp mixed herb
  13. Prepare 2 tbsp grated Carrot
  14. Make ready 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
  15. Take 2 tbsp thinly grated Capsicum
  16. Make ready 1/2 cup milk warm
  17. Make ready 1 Egg beaten
  18. Get 2 tbsp soft Butter
  19. Take For Cheese Coating:
  20. Prepare 1/4 cup melted butter
  21. Get 1/4 cup Milk
  22. Take 1 Egg beaten
  23. Prepare 4 gatlic Cloves mince
  24. Prepare 1/2 tsp Oregano
  25. Take 1/4 tsp dry thyme
  26. Prepare 1/4 tsp dry basil
  27. Take 1/2 tsp fresh ground Pepper
  28. Make ready 2 tbsp chives
  29. Take 2 tbsp fresh nicely chopped corriender
  30. Make ready 2 cups grated cheddar cheese
Steps to make Multi grain Cheeza Bread:
  1. For dough, mix all flour in a deep mixing bowl add salt, garlic, onion powder mixed herb, grated carrot and capsicum and cheese and mix.
  2. In a small bowl add sugar dry yeast and lukewarm water and left to dissolve and bubbling.
  3. Now add dissolved yeast, milk and beaten.egg in flour mixture, and knead smooth and soft dough.
  4. Now cover and left it for an hour to double in size.
  5. When dough double in size, divide the dough in 4 equal pieces and divide the pieces in 5 to 6 equal size small balls.
  6. Now a medium bowl mix melted butter, milk, beaten egg, minced garlic, pepper, oregano, thyme, basil, chives, chopped corrinder and mix well.
  7. Greased the bount cake dish with butter.
  8. Now dip each ball of dough in melted butter and egg mixture, coat with grated cheddar cheese nicely and place in greased bount cake dish.
  9. Cover the pan with wrap and left the dough rise for 30 to 45 minutes.
  10. Now preheated oven at 180*c.
  11. After rising greased the rise dough with remaining egg and butter mixture and bake 25 to 30 minutes till dark brown in colour.
  12. Now left out pan sprinkle grated cheese over the top and again bake for 5 minutes or till cheese melt.
  13. Now remove pan from oven and let cool in the pan for 5 to 10 minutes.
  14. Remove bread from pan and serve with dipping sauce.
