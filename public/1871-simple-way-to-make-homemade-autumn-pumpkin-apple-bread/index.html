The ingredients needed to make Autumn Pumpkin Apple Bread:
  1. Get Topping
  2. Make ready 1 tbsp flour
  3. Take 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  4. Prepare 5 tbsp sugar
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp butter or margarine
  6. Take Bread
  7. Get 3 cup flour
  8. Make ready 2 tsp baking soda
  9. Prepare 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  10. Make ready 1/4 tsp ground allspice
  11. Prepare 3/4 cup vegetable oil
  12. Take 4 large eggs
  13. Make ready 3/4 tsp salt
  14. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  15. Take 1 tsp ground cloves
  16. Take 16 oz can of pumpkin
  17. Get 2 cup granulated sugar
  18. Make ready 2 apples (peeled and chopped)

Steps to make Autumn Pumpkin Apple Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and grease two 9" x 5" x 3" loaf pans.
  2. Combine all topping ingredients and cut together tho form a crumb - like mixture.
  3. Sift flour, salt, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and allspice together, set aside.
  4. In another bowl, mix together pumpkin, oil, sugar and eggs with mixer. Add flour mixture to pumpkin mixture and mix well.
  5. Fold in apples.
  6. Divide batter between pans and sprinkle topping evenly over each. Bake on MIDDLE RACK for 50 minutes. Cool in pans for 45 minutes, then remove to cooling racks.
