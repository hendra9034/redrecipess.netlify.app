The ingredients needed to make Zucchini Flat Naan Bread  نان کدویی:
  1. Take 3 cups all purpose flour
  2. Prepare 1 large zucchini, grated
  3. Take 2 eggs
  4. Prepare 1 onion, finely chopped
  5. Make ready 30-40 g Cheese (feta cheese, mozzarella cheese or cheddar cheese)
  6. Prepare 40-50 ml water
  7. Prepare 2 tsp salt
  8. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  9. Make ready 1 tsp paprika
  10. Get 1.2 tsp chilli flakes
  11. Take Vegetable oil
Steps to make Zucchini Flat Naan Bread  نان کدویی:
  1. Ingredients
  2. Grate the zucchini and squeeze to release the juice. Add chopped onion, eggs, cheese and season with spices. M
  3. Mix all together. Then add flour, baking powder and combine with hand. Gradually add water to the batter
  4. And knead it for 2-3 minutes. If the batter is sticky add some flour. cover the dough and allow to rest for 1 hour.
  5. After an hour, place the dough on a floured surface and divide the dough into 8 pieces.
  6. Roll each dough into a small ball. On a floured surface, using a rolling pin and roll it into a flatten circle. Heat a little bit oil in a pan and add in flattened dough.
  7. Once one side fried (3-4 minutes) flip over and fry other side.
