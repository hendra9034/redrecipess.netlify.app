The ingredients needed to make Banana Oatmeal Bread with Pancake Mix:
  1. Take 2 cups pancake mix
  2. Get 1 cup oats
  3. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  4. Prepare 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  5. Make ready 3/4 cup sugar
  6. Take 3/4 cup milk
  7. Take 1/4 stick butter, melted
  8. Prepare 3 medium size eggs
Steps to make Banana Oatmeal Bread with Pancake Mix:
  1. Preheat oven for 150 degrees C.
  2. Mix all the ingredients.
  3. Grease pan with butter or vegetable oil.
  4. Fill in the Pan then bake for 30-40mins, or until done.
