The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread:
  1. Get 1/2 cup softened butter
  2. Take 2/3 cup sugar
  3. Take 2 eggs, well beaten
  4. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  6. Get 2 cups AP flour
  7. Take 3 large over ripe bananas
  8. Get 1 tsp vanilla
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp nutmeg

Instructions to make Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°
  2. Beat butter and sugar together until fluffy
  3. And beaten eggs and mix
  4. Mix soda into 3 Tbsp hot water and add to sugar mix. Blend well.
  5. Add 1 cup flour and salt, blend.
  6. Mash bananas and add to mixture, blend.
  7. Add remaining flour and vanilla. Blend well.
  8. Beat sugar, butter and eggs until well mixed.
  9. Pour into greased loaf pan.
  10. Bake for 45 minutes
  11. Cool in pan, then move to wire cooling rack until cooled completely.
  12. Store in an airtight container.
