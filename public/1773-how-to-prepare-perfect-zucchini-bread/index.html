The ingredients needed to make Zucchini bread:
  1. Take 3 eggs
  2. Take 2 cup sugar
  3. Prepare 1 cup oil
  4. Get 2 tsp vanilla
  5. Get 2 cup coarsely shredded unpeeled zucchini
  6. Prepare 8 1/4 oz can crushed pineapple, drained well
  7. Get 3 cup flour
  8. Make ready 2 tsp baking soda
  9. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  10. Take 1/2 tsp baking powder
  11. Take 1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  12. Prepare 3/4 tsp nutmeg (or skip nutmeg and use 2 tsp cinnamon instead of 1 1/2 tsp)
  13. Take 1 cup finely chopped walnuts (optional)

Instructions to make Zucchini bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Lightly grease two 9x5 loaf pans; set aside.
  3. In a bowl, beat eggs with electric mixer until frothy; beat in sugar, oil and vanilla and continue beating until mixture is thick and foamy. Stir in zucchini and pineapple.
  4. In another bowl, stir together flour, baking soda, salt, baking powder, cinnamon (nutmeg) and nuts. Add to zucchini mixture and stir just until all flour is moistened.
  5. Spoon batter into prepared pans. Bake 50-60 minutes. Cool on wire rack for 10 minutes, then turn out onto rack to cool completely.
