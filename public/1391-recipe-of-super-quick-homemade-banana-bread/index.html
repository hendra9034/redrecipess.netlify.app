The ingredients needed to make Banana bread:
  1. Get 4 bananas
  2. Take 125 g butter
  3. Make ready 150 g brown sugar
  4. Make ready 175 g flour
  5. Prepare 2 eggs
  6. Get 1 table spoon of cinnamon
  7. Take little bit of nutmeg
  8. Make ready 1 table spoon of baking powder
  9. Make ready 50 g cashews
  10. Prepare 70 g chocolate chips

Instructions to make Banana bread:
  1. Melt the butter and wait a bit to cool down a bit.
  2. Mash the bananas, add brown sugar and eggs.
  3. In the banana mix add melted butter and cinnamon and nutmeg.
  4. Add flour and baking powder and mix well.
  5. Add cutted cashews and chocolate chips.
  6. Bake at 160℃ for 55 minutes. When cold can put some powdered sugar on top.
