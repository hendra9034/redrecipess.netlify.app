The ingredients needed to make Samhain Pumpkin Bread:
  1. Make ready 1 3/4 cup flour
  2. Get 1 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Get 3/4 tsp salt
  4. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
  5. Take 1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  6. Get 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  7. Get 1/2 tsp allspice
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground cloves
  9. Take 15 oz pumpkin puree
  10. Take 1/3 cup water, juice, or pumpkin spice coffee cooled
  11. Prepare 2 eggs
  12. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  13. Get 5 drops almond extract (optional)
  14. Take 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  15. Make ready 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or nut of your choice (optional)
Instructions to make Samhain Pumpkin Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease two standard size loaf pans.
  2. In a large bowl, sift/stir together dry ingredients (flour, sugar, salt, baking soda, and spices).
  3. In medium bowl whisk together the pumpkin, water/ juice/ or coffee (whatever you are using), eggs, vanilla, almond extract if using, and vegetable oil until combined.
  4. Gradually add the wet mixture into the dry mixture, whisking while you pour, keep whisking until everything is well mixed. - Fold in nuts if desired. - Pour batter into prepared pans.
  5. Bake until top is golden, loaf springs back when lightly pressed, and toothpick inserted in middle comes out clean…about 50 to 60 minutes, oven times vary.
  6. Remove from oven and allow to cool in pans of 15-20 minutes before removing loaf from pans.
  7. Serve, Share, Enjoy!
