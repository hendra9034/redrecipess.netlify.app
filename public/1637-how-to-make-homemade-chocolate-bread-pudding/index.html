The ingredients needed to make chocolate bread pudding:
  1. Make ready 1 loaf cinnamon bread
  2. Prepare 3 cup chocolate milk
  3. Take 4 eggs
  4. Take 1/4 cup brown sugar
  5. Take 2 tsp vanilla
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp powdered sugar

Instructions to make chocolate bread pudding:
  1. preheat oven to 350
  2. butter a pan
  3. mix together in bowl: choc milk, eggs, brown sugar, and vanilla
  4. tear bread into cubes. pour milk over bread cubes
  5. pour into pan. press down slightly.
  6. bake at 350 for 45-55 minutes
  7. dust with powder sugar just before serving
