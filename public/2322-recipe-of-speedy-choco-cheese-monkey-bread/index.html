Instructions to make Choco-cheese Monkey Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°. Open biscuits. Separate and flatten each one out.
  2. Cut cream cheese into cubes
  3. Mix granualted sugar and cinnamon together. Set aside.
  4. Put one cube of cream cheese in the middle of biscuit and cover with a few chocolate chips. Fold over dough and roll in a ball. Set aside. Do this until all biscuits are filled and rolled.
  5. Roll the dough filled balls in sugar cinnamon mix until they are completely covered.
  6. Next put brown sugar and butter in microwave safe bowl and microwave for 70 sec. Stir halfway through time.
  7. Spray a bunt pan with Pam. Sprinkle with walnuts. Layer the dough filled balls along the walnuts. Drizzle brown sugar/butter melt over dough balls. Add more dough balls on top and drizzle more "melt". Bake for 35 minutes at 350°.
  8. Let it cool for 10 mintues and then flip onto plate. Enjoy.
