The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread:
  1. Make ready 4 bananas, overripe
  2. Get 2 eggs, beaten
  3. Get 250 g flour
  4. Take 100 g brown sugar
  5. Prepare 60 g softened butter
  6. Take 1 teaspoon baking soda
  7. Take 1 teaspoon salt
  8. Prepare 30 g pecan nuts

Steps to make Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 185°C, lightly grease a loaf pan
  2. Mix flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In separate bowl, mix butter and sugar. Stir in eggs, mashed banana, and nuts.
  4. Pour banana mixture into flour mixture and stir. Pour batter into loaf pan and bake for 75 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
