The ingredients needed to make Nutty Chocolate Chip Banana Bread:
  1. Get 3 large ripe bananas
  2. Prepare 1 cup white granulated sugar
  3. Take 1 egg
  4. Take 4 Tbsp. butter (melted and cooled)
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 cup flour
  6. Make ready 1 tsp. salt
  7. Prepare 1 tsp. baking soda
  8. Take 1 cup Ghirardelli dark chocolate chips
  9. Take 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Steps to make Nutty Chocolate Chip Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
  2. Mash bananas in large bowl.
  3. Beat in sugar, egg, and butter.
  4. In another bowl, stir together flour, salt, and baking soda.
  5. Add to dry mixture and stir until smooth.
  6. Add chocolate chips and stir.
  7. Pour into lightly greased mini loaf trays or large baking dish.
  8. Sprinkle desired amount walnuts on top of batter.
  9. Bake for 40-45 minutes for mini loaf pans, 55-60 minutes for large baking dish.
  10. Enjoy!
