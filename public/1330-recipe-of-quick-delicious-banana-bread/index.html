The ingredients needed to make Delicious banana bread:
  1. Get 2-3 overripe bananas
  2. Make ready 1/3 cup butter
  3. Prepare 1 teaspoon baking soda
  4. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon salt
  5. Get 3/4 cups brown sugar (or regular sugar)
  6. Get 1 large egg
  7. Get 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  8. Make ready 1 1/2 cup flour all purpose
  9. Get Optional things that make your banana bread extra special:
  10. Get 1 cup cooking oats
  11. Make ready 1/3-1/2 cup vanilla Greek yogurt
  12. Make ready 1 bit of honey
  13. Prepare Cinnamon

Instructions to make Delicious banana bread:
  1. Mix all dry ingredients together. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mash bananas in separate bowl
  3. Mix all ingredients together
  4. Pour mixture into a greased loaf pan.
  5. Bake for 45 or more
  6. Let cool
  7. Enjoy!
