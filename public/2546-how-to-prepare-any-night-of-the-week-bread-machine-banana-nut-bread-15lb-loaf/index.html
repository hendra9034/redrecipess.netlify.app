The ingredients needed to make Bread Machine - Banana Nut Bread (1.5lb Loaf):

  2. Get 2 tbsp Milk (warmed in microwave to about 80 F)
  3. Take 3 Mashed medium banana
  4. Make ready 2 large Eggs
  5. Make ready 2 1/2 cup All purpose flour
  6. Take 1 cup Sugar
  7. Get 2 1/2 tsp Baking power
  8. Get 1/2 tsp Baking soda
  9. Take 1 tsp Salt
