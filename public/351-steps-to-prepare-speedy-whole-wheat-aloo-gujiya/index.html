The ingredients needed to make Whole wheat aloo gujiya:
  1. Prepare 1 cup whole wheat flour
  2. Prepare 2 spoon rava
  3. Get 1/4 cup oil
  4. Make ready 1 tsp ajwain
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  6. Prepare For filling
  7. Take 4 boiled and grated potato
  8. Prepare 3-4 chopped green chilli
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp chopped green coriander
  10. Get 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  11. Make ready 1 tsp garam masala
  12. Make ready 1/2 tsp dry mango powder
  13. Make ready 2 tbsp rice flour or bread crumbs
  14. Get 2 tbsp white sesame seeds
  15. Prepare For garnishing.
  16. Take as needed Tomato sauce
  17. Make ready as needed coriander leaves

Gujiya is a very popular sweet prepared for the festival of holi. It has a flaky crust made with All-purpose flour: All-purpose flour gives a crumbly crust. I have never tried using whole wheat flour for the dough. Oil: Enough oil is added to make crusty.

Instructions to make Whole wheat aloo gujiya:
  1. Mix flour,rava,oil,salt and ajwain and make a stiff dough keep it covered for rest
  2. Mix all the ingredients of filling except sesame seed and keep in fridge
  3. After 10 mins take out potato filling and make small balls from it
  4. Take a portion of dough and roll it on cut small size roundles with cutter
  5. Keep potato balls on one side and fold like gujiha
  6. Stick sesame seeds on outer side and deep fry it
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and tomato sauce
