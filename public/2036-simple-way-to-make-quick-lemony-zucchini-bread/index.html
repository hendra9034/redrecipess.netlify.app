Instructions to make Lemony Zucchini Bread:
  1. Combine first 6 ingredients.
  2. In another bowl whisk the eggs, milk, oil, lemon juice and lemon extract.
  3. Stir in dry dry ingredients until moistened. Fold in the zucchini, poppy seed and lemon peel. Pour into 2 greased 9x5x3 loaf Bake at 350 50-55 minutes or a toothpick in the centre comes clean. Cool for 10 minutes before removing from the pans.
