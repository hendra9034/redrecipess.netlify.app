Instructions to make 2 ingredient pumpkin bread/cake:
  1. preheat oven to 350
  2. combine pumpkin and cake mix
  3. i use a bundt pan but you could use any cake or bread pan. spray it with Pam or grease it, fill it and bake!
  4. add any spices you like, my favorite is 1 or 2 tsp vanilla and 1 tsp or more pumpkin pie spice
  5. bake for 55 minutes to an hour.
  6. you could use spice cake or white or yellow cake mix too…chocolate will overpower the pumpkin taste but is still really good!!!
  7. i drizzle with caramel or frost with cream cheese frosting or sometimes it goes so fast i don't even get a chance!
