Instructions to make Mom's Zucchini Bread:
  1. Beat eggs till light. Add sugar, oil, zucchini & vanilla , mix well.
  2. Combine flour, salt, soda, baking powder & cinnamon. Add to egg mixture, stir well, add nuts.
  3. Pour into greased 9x5x3 pan.
  4. Bake at 350 for 1 hour.
