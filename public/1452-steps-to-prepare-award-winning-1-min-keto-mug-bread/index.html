The ingredients needed to make 1 min keto mug bread:
  1. Prepare 4 tbsp almond meal/flour
  2. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking powder
  3. Get 1 egg
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp olive oil
  5. Make ready Pinch salt

Instructions to make 1 min keto mug bread:
  1. Add all ingredients to mug and mix, will be very wet
  2. Microwave HIGH 1 min
  3. Tap the top to make sure it’s cooked, if not, then add another 20-30 seconds
  4. Turn mug upside down and let bread cool before slicing
  5. Can be toasted if desired for crispier texture
