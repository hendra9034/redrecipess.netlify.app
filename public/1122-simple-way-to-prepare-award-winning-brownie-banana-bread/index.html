The ingredients needed to make Brownie Banana Bread:
  1. Take 3-4 very ripe bananas
  2. Get 1/3 cup melted butter
  3. Get 1 teaspoon baking soda
  4. Take 1/4 teaspoon salt
  5. Make ready 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  6. Make ready 1 large egg beaten
  7. Get 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  8. Take 1/2 cup corn oil
  9. Prepare 1 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  10. Make ready 3/4 cup Hershey's Cocoa
  11. Take 1/2 cup Hershey's chocolate syrup

Steps to make Brownie Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 and butter a 4x8 loaf pan
  2. Mash banana and add melted butter
  3. Mix in baking soda, salt, sugar, egg, vanilla extract, flour, oil, and cocoa
  4. Add chocolate syrup and mix well
  5. Put into loaf pan and bake for 50-60 minutes
  6. Serve warm or let cool.
  7. OPTIONAL: Add nuts if desired
  8. Serving suggestion: Slice banana over each slice and dust with powdered sugar for a decadent dessert
