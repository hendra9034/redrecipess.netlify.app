The ingredients needed to make Vegan bread pudding:
  1. Prepare stale bread, cubed or shredded to pieces
  2. Prepare dried fruit (a mixture - add mixed peel too if you have it)
  3. Get brown sugar
  4. Take ground flax seed
  5. Prepare mixed spice (mix of cinnamon, ground ginger, nutmeg, with a little allspice and cloves)
  6. Make ready plant milk (any kind)
  7. Take sunflower oil
  8. Prepare granulated sugar (white or brown) for sprinkling

Steps to make Vegan bread pudding:
  1. Add the bread, fruit, sugar, flax and spices to a large mixing bowl and stir together.
  2. Stir in the milk and oil, and set this aside for an hour to soak. Add more milk if needed as the bread will absorb a lot of moisture.
  3. Heat the oven to 160C. Grease or line a square baking tin or oven dish, and spoon in the mixture, pressing it down firmly with the back of the spoon. Sprinkle very generously with granulated sugar and bake for 1 hour.
