The ingredients needed to make Zucchini Bread est Délicieux:
  1. Make ready Dry Ingredients
  2. Prepare 1.5 cups flour (all purpose or cake if ur fancy)
  3. Get .75 teaspoon baking powder
  4. Prepare .5 teaspoon baking soda (gives the nice brown color)
  5. Prepare 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  6. Get 1 teaspoon nutmeg (lil extra to taste of u like)
  7. Make ready (Possibly add.25 tsp ginger?)
  8. Get .75 teaspoon salt
  9. Prepare Not dry ingredients ;-)
  10. Prepare 1 cup grated zucchini (try to dry off the gratings)
  11. Take .75 cup brown sugar
  12. Get .5 cup granulated sugar
  13. Take .5 cup vegetable oil
  14. Take .5 cup melted butter (a stick)
  15. Take 1.5 teaspoons vanilla extract
  16. Prepare 2 eggs
  17. Take 1/2 cup milk
  18. Take Other optional things
  19. Prepare 1 cup chopped nuts (walnuts probably)
Instructions to make Zucchini Bread est Délicieux:
  1. Preheat oven to 350. Grease a loaf pan.
  2. Heads up: I use a strong spatula to do all my mixing. Just fyi. Anyways…
  3. I blot the zucchini dry after shredding. Then I "roast" it in the oven for about 10 min on fresh paper towels while I prepare the other ingredients.
  4. Combine/mix dry ingredients in large bowl. Make a well in the center(for uniformity in the mix supposedly). You will pour your "not dry" ingredients into the well later.
  5. Combine/mix "not dry" ingredients in another bowl. I combine the sugars first then add the warm melted butter. Followed by the zucchini. Then everything else. Don't use a blender.
  6. Pour "not dry" bowl contents into the well of the dry bowl. Don't add until you're ready to bake. Mix them together - not with a blender. Don't over mix. That's bad. Makes gluten. Gluten makes bread yucky.
  7. Pour said mixture into greased pan. Bake about…. 50-60 min(50/55 at a strong 350). Knife through center to test doneness.
