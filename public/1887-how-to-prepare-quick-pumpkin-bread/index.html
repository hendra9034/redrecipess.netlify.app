The ingredients needed to make pumpkin bread:
  1. Make ready 1 can (15 oz.) pumpkin
  2. Make ready 4 eggs
  3. Get 1 cup vegetable oil
  4. Prepare 2/3 cup water
  5. Make ready 3 cup white sugar
  6. Take 3 1/2 cup white flour
  7. Take 2 tsp baking soda
  8. Take 1 1/2 tsp salt
  9. Prepare 1 tsp cinnamon
  10. Make ready 1 tsp nutmeg
  11. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground cloves
  12. Prepare 1/4 tsp ginger

Instructions to make pumpkin bread:
  1. pre heat oven to 350* F. grease and flour 3 7x3 loaf pans
  2. mix pumpkin, egg, oil, water, and sugar. in separate bowl mix all other ingredients. mix wet and dry then pour into pans
  3. bake for 50 minites, til knife inserted comes out clean
  4. freezes well
