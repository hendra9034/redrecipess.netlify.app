The ingredients needed to make Vegetable Finger:
  1. Prepare 2 potatoes
  2. Make ready 1 capsicum chopped
  3. Take 2 carrot chopped
  4. Get 1/2 cup Beans
  5. Make ready to taste Salt
  6. Get 2 teaspoons cumin powder
  7. Prepare 2 teaspoons red chilli powder
  8. Take 1/2 cup Bread cramb
  9. Get 4 tablespoons cornflour
  10. Get 2 tablespoons flour
  11. Make ready 4 tablespoons white oil
Steps to make Vegetable Finger:
  1. Boil, peel, and mashed potatoes. add salt. Chop carrots, beans, capsicum into very fine pieces.
  2. Heat 2 tablespoon oil, Fry the carrot, Beans, Capsicum well. Add mashed potatoes. Now add 2 teaspoons cumin powder, Red chilli powder, sprinkle salt to taste.
  3. Take a small amount of the mixture in our palm and roll it to a shape of a finger depending upon the size that we prefer. - Repeat the same for all of the mixture.
  4. Coat each veg finger well with flour and then coat it with cornflour. Then coat it with breadcrumbs.
  5. Now heat enough oil in a kadhai or deep bottom pan. Once the oil is hot gently slide the veg fingers and fry them well on medium-high heat until golden and crisp. Take them out and drain them on absorbent paper or kitchen towel.
  6. Healthy veg fingers are ready to be served.
