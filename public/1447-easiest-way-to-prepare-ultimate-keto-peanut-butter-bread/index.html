The ingredients needed to make Keto Peanut Butter Bread:
  1. Get 1 cup Peanut Flour
  2. Make ready 8 large egg yolks or 4 eggs
  3. Make ready 12 oz cream cheese
  4. Prepare 1 TBSP vanilla
  5. Make ready 8 TBSP Kerrygold Butter
  6. Take 2 tsp baking powder
  7. Get 2-4 TBSP Brown Sugar Truvia depending on taste
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
