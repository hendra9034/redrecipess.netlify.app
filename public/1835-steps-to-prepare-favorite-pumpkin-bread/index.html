The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin bread:
  1. Take 1 can (16 ounces) - pure pumpkin puree or 2-3 cups mashed fresh cooked pumpkin
  2. Make ready 1 2/3 - cups sugar
  3. Take 2/3 cup - vegetable oil
  4. Make ready 2 tsp - vanilla
  5. Get 4 large - eggs
  6. Take 3 cups - AP flour or wheat flour
  7. Take 2 tsp - baking soda
  8. Take 1 tsp - salt
  9. Prepare 1 tsp - ground cinnamon
  10. Get 1/2 tsp - ground cloves
  11. Prepare 1/2 tsp - baking powder
  12. Make ready 1 cup - rough chopped walnuts (optional)

Steps to make Pumpkin bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Spray the bottoms of two loaf pans with cooking spray
  3. Mix the pumpkin, sugar, oil, vanilla, and eggs in a large bowl.
  4. Mix the rest of the ingredients and then add to the wet and stir until combined.
  5. Pour into the pans.
  6. Bake for 50 – 60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  7. Cool completely before cutting
  8. Enjoy :)
