Steps to make Monkey Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Cut biscuits in 1/4 squares.
  3. Mix sugar and cinnamon into bowl. Dip biscuits squares into the mixture.
  4. Melt brown sugar and butter in a boiler. Bring to a boil.
  5. Grease pan with shortening. Toss biscuit squares into it.
  6. Then pour brown sugar and melted butter over it.
  7. Bake for about 35 minutes
