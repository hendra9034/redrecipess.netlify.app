The ingredients needed to make Lemon Rolls:
  1. Make ready 1.5 cup warm water
  2. Take 1 Tbsp sugar
  3. Take 1 Tbsp yeast
  4. Take 3-4 Tbsp butter room temp
  5. Take Pinch salt
  6. Get 3.5-5 cups White Lilly Bread flour
  7. Take Filling
  8. Prepare Handful butter room temp
  9. Take Handful white sugar
  10. Make ready 3 lemons of zest
  11. Prepare Glaze
  12. Get 3 cups powdered sugar
  13. Prepare Half stick of butter room temp
  14. Get 4-5 Tbsp cream cheese room temp
  15. Take Splash Vanilla
  16. Take Couple tbsp's of lemon juice
Instructions to make Lemon Rolls:
  1. Add yeast and sugar to warm water and proof till bubbly.
  2. Add butter and salt to yeast mixture.
  3. Mix in 3 cups of flour and keep mixing in a little bit of flour till it is pulling away from bowl. Once it pulls away from bowl, throw on floured counter and knead for just a minute into a ball. Throw in greased bowl and cover with towel to rise double. My oven has a bread proof button (so 20 minutes).
  4. After rising throw back onto floured counter and roll out into large rectangle about 1/4 inch. Smear butter all over and throw the sugar all over it. Take the grated lemon peel and toss all over.
  5. Roll it up tight and slice 1.5 inches.
  6. Place in pan a inch apart and let rise again till double.
  7. Bake at 350 for about 20 minutes. Sometimes it can be less or more.(convection oven seems to be tricky) I always watch after 15 minutes and pull them out if the tops is getting too brown.
  8. For the glaze I add all ingredients into the mixer and just let it mix for about 5 minutes, while the rolls are cooling off.
  9. For cinnamon rolls just change the filling to brown sugar and cinnamon on top of the butter spread.
