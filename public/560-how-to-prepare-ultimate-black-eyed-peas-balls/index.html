The ingredients needed to make Black Eyed Peas Balls:
  1. Make ready A. Black Eyed Peas
  2. Make ready 3 C soaked and boiled
  3. Get B. Finely Minced
  4. Prepare 1 medium size brown onion
  5. Prepare 1 big red chillies or few bird eyed chillies
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp cooking oil
  7. Make ready C. Seasonings and extras
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp ground white pepper
  9. Take 1 tbsp rice flour
  10. Make ready 1 tsp baking powder
  11. Get 2 tbsp finely minced herb (any kind - optional)
  12. Prepare 2-3 tsp chicken bouillon / chicken seasoning powder
  13. Prepare D. As needed- Cheese for stuffing (optional)
  14. Take E. Final coating
  15. Prepare 2 eggs - beaten
  16. Get 1 C panko / bread crumbs
Instructions to make Black Eyed Peas Balls:
  1. A - Soak 200 g (will make around 3 cups of tender peas once soaked) black eyed peas for few hours or overnight. Once soaked, cook 3 cups of peas until soft. Transfer to a food processor and pulse until almost fine. No water needed to pulse if using food processor. Alternatively, soft cooked peas can be mashed using potato masher too. Keep aside.
  2. B - Finely mince brown onions and chillies. Saut√© with 1-2 tbsp of oil until translucent.
  3. Combine and mix A, B and C. It should be doughy. Taste and adjust seasoning accordingly. Take a small portion of this peas dough and shape into a small ball. Shape by hand or use a piece of clean and clear plastic to squeeze it round. See pic. If using cheese sticks, cut them small. Take a tbsp or less of peas dough. Flatten it and place cheese at the center, seal and shape into ball.
  4. E - In a wok or deep sauce pan, heat enough oil for deep frying. Roll each ball in beaten eggs and coat evenly in bread crumbs.   5. Serve with homemade sauce or just tomato ketchup and creamy mayonnaise.
