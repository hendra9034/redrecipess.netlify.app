The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread and Butter Pudding:
  1. Prepare french bread
  2. Get unsalted butter
  3. Make ready bananas
  4. Make ready egg yolks
  5. Prepare milk (or 3/4 pint)
  6. Get caster sugar
  7. Prepare good vanilla essence or 1 vanilla pod
  8. Prepare cinnamon stick
  9. Get nutmeg
Instructions to make Banana Bread and Butter Pudding:
  1. Grease a baking dish with butter.
  2. slice the french bread and butter the slices. Slice the bananas. Arrange the bread and bananas in the baking dish.
  3. To make the custard = Heat the milk in a sauce pan with the cinnamon stick and vanilla essence / seeds of vanilla pod. Meanwhile, in a jug, mix the egg yolks and sugar and beat well. Take the milk off the heat just as it begins to boil. Pour into the egg and sugar mix stirring well all the while. When it is mixed well, pour back into the saucepan and simmer for 4-5 mins untill it thickens slightly.
  4. Pour the custard over the bread and banana so level is just below the tip of the bread, keeping some back for serving later on. Sprinkle the nutmeg over the top. Bake for half hour on 175°F. Serve with remaining custard.
