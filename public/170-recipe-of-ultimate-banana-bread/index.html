The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread:
  1. Prepare 2 cups all purpose flour
  2. Get 1/2 tsp baking powder
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking soda
  4. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Prepare 1 tsp cinnamon
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup butter, softened
  7. Make ready 1 cup granulated white sugar
  8. Get 2 eggs
  9. Take 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  10. Make ready 3 mashed bananas
  11. Make ready 1/2 cup chopped walnuts, chocolate chips or almonds- optional

Instructions to make Banana Bread:
  1. Pre-heat oven to 350. Sift flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt together. Set aside.
  2. Cream butter and sugar until fluffy. Gradually add one egg at a time to butter sugar mixture beating each one throughly.
  3. Mash bananas, add to egg mixture. Stir in dry ingredients gradually. When well mixed add walnuts or almonds.
  4. Bake at 350 for 45-60 or until toothpick comes out clean.
  5. Serve warmed with butter.
