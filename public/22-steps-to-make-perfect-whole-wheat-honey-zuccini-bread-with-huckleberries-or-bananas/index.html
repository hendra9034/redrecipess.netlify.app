The ingredients needed to make Whole wheat honey zuccini bread (with huckleberries or bananas):
  1. Get 1 cup softened butter
  2. Take 4 eggs
  3. Take 4 tsp. Vanilla
  4. Get 3/4 cups honey
  5. Prepare 4 c. Grated zuccini (2 cups drained if frozen)
  6. Prepare 2 bananas OR 2 cups berries
  7. Take 4 cups whole white wheat flour
  8. Make ready 2 tsp baking powder
  9. Take 1 tsp. Salt
  10. Take (Optional) 1 cup chocolate chips
  11. Get (Optional) 1 to 2 tsp. Cinnamon
  12. Prepare (Optional) 1 cup of nuts

Healthy Zucchini Bread made with applesauce, whole wheat flour and no oil. This low sugar and low fat quick bread is the best zucchini bread recipe. Freshly grated zucchini and applesauce make this quick bread moist. Please note bake time varies depending on the pan you use!

Steps to make Whole wheat honey zuccini bread (with huckleberries or bananas):
  1. Soften butter. I put it on defrost in the microwave for 20 seconds.
  2. Mash wet ingredients together. If I am using berries I mix those in last. If I use bananas I use a bit less honey.
  3. Mix dry ingredients. I am lazy so I just pile them on the wet ingredients, stir them together on top then mix it all together. Add nuts, berries, and/or chocolate chips now. At this point I taste the batter to see if it is sweet and salty enough.
  4. Grease two bread pans with butter and cut a square of parchemer paper for the bottom
  5. Cook at 350 for 1 hour. Check at 50 minutes. Cook until knife comes out clean.
