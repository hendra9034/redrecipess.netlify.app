The ingredients needed to make Fat free high protein banana quick bread:
  1. Take 100 ml Skimmed milk
  2. Take 1 Banana (around 100 gram)
  3. Get 70 gram mashed chickpea (or replace by same weight of egg)
  4. Get 10 gram Baking Powder
  5. Prepare 10 gram Brown Sugar (Optional)
  6. Make ready 70 gram Whole wheat flour
  7. Get 1 gram salt

Instructions to make Fat free high protein banana quick bread:
  1. In a bowl, combined the mashed banana, skimmed milk, set aside.
  2. Take another bowl, measure out the baking powder, brown sugar whole wheat flour and add just a pinch of salt.
  3. Add wet to dry. Mix and stop when all combine.
  4. Pour the batter inside the well-lined baking tin. To rest the dough, wrap it up with plastic wrap and place it in the fridge for 45 minutes. (Fine if you bake it right away)
  5. While pre-heating your steamer, take out the batter from the fridge.Once water boils, cover your pan with aluminum foil and seal tightly, then place the pan inside the steamer. Make sure you don't let the bottom of the pan touch the water.
  6. Steam-bake for about 35 minutes.
