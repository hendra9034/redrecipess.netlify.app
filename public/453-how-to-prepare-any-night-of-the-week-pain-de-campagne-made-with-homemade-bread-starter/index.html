The ingredients needed to make Pain de Campagne Made With Homemade Bread Starter:
  1. Make ready 160 grams Bread (strong) flour
  2. Get 40 grams Whole wheat flour
  3. Get 80 grams Bread starter made with natural yeast
  4. Prepare 4 grams Salt
  5. Prepare 120 ml or so Water
Instructions to make Pain de Campagne Made With Homemade Bread Starter:
  1. Put all the ingredients in a bowl and knead. When it comes together, round off into a smooth ball, place in a bowl and leave for the 1st proofing.
  2. When the dough has risen, round it off again and put into a well-floured bread rising mold (a banneton) for the 2nd proofing.
  3. Take the risen dough out of the banneton, and slash the surface of the dough in a cross pattern or a single slash.
  4. Bake in a preheated 240°C oven until well browned.
