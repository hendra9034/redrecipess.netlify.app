The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread Cheesecake:
  1. Get Banana Bread:
  2. Get 4 ripe bananas
  3. Take 4 tablespoon vegetable oil
  4. Take 1 egg
  5. Prepare 3 tablespoon caster sugar
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  7. Make ready 200 g flour
  8. Take 1 teaspoon baking soda
  9. Make ready 1 teaspoon baking powder
  10. Make ready 1 teaspoon cinnamon powder
  11. Make ready pinch salt
  12. Prepare Cheesecake:
  13. Get 550 g cream cheese, room temperature, softened
  14. Take 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  15. Get 200 ml whipped cream
  16. Make ready 5 tablespoon sweet condensed milk
  17. Prepare 3 tablespoon white sugar powder

Steps to make Banana Bread Cheesecake:
  1. Preheated oven to 180 deg Celcius
  2. For the banana bread: in a large bowl mash all bananas, add oil, egg, vanilla and sugar. Stir until well combined.
  3. Add in baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, flour and salt. Whisk using hand whisker until well combined, but don't over mixed.
  4. Pour the batter into baking pan, and bake for 30-40 minutes or until the toothpick inserted come out clean.
  5. Let cooled down in a rack.
  6. For the Cheesecake: In a large bowl, add softened cream cheese whisk with powder sugar abd vanilla extract.
  7. Add condensed milk and stir until Incorporated.
  8. In other bowls, using electric mixer, whisk whipped cream about 3-4 minutes. Then add to the cheese cream batter.
  9. After the banana bread cooled down, you can trim the upper side if you want, or just pour the Cheesecake batter above it.
  10. Refrigerated it for 3 hours or overnight for better result.
  11. You can shift cocoa powder if you like. Enjoy!
