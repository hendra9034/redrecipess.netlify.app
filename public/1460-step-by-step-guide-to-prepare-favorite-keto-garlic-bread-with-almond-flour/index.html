The ingredients needed to make Keto Garlic Bread with almond flour   نان سیر با آرد بادام:
  1. Take 1/2 cup almond flour
  2. Make ready 1 cup shredded Mozzarella cheers
  3. Take 2 tbsp plain cream cheese
  4. Make ready 1 tfbsp garlic powder
  5. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  6. Prepare 1 egg
  7. Take 1 tbs butter
  8. Prepare 1 clove garlic
  9. Get 1 tbsp grated parmesan
  10. Get 1 tbsp chopped green onion
  11. Make ready Pinch salt
Instructions to make Keto Garlic Bread with almond flour   نان سیر با آرد بادام:
  1. Ingredients
  2. Preheat the oven to 200C degree. Line a baking tray with parchment paper. - Now, In a bowl, add grated mozzarella, cream cheese, some salt, almond flour, baking powder and garlic powder.
  3. Microwave in high for 40 second or 1 minute. Until cheese melted. Add egg and combine all together. Now you have a medium runny batter (I mean it is soft and not look like a dough).
  4. With a spatula, spread the batter on the tray. You can give a round or square shape to the batter.
  5. Melt the butter in a pan. Add grated garlic, chopped green onion and 1 tbsp Parmesan cheese. Mix together.
  6. Pour the mixture over top of the dough. - Place it in the oven and bake for 15 minutes, until beautifully golden.
