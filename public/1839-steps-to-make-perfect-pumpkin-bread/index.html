The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Bread:
  1. Take 1 2/3 cup all-purpose flour
  2. Prepare 1/3 cup light brown sugar
  3. Prepare 10 oz. canned pumpkin puree
  4. Make ready 1 1/2 cup chopped toasted pecans or walnuts, optional
  5. Get 1 cup granulated sugar
  6. Get 1/2 tsp ground cloves
  7. Prepare 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  8. Get 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  9. Take 1/3 cup canola oil

Steps to make Pumpkin Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 325ºF.
  2. Spray one large loaf pan, about 9” x 5”, with cooking spray
  3. In a large bowl, mix together first seven ingredients
  4. In another bowl, blend the remaining ingredients together
  5. Combine all ingredients and stir well until smooth. (It is a thick batter!)
  6. Spoon into loaf pan and lightly smooth out the top with a spatula
  7. Bake about 1 hour and 15 minutes, until cake tester comes out dry and clean.
  8. Cool thoroughly. It’ll seem like the loaf doesn’t want to come out. Just give it a few good slaps on the bottom of the pan, and it’ll come right out.
