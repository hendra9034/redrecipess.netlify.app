The ingredients needed to make Egg surprise:
  1. Get 6-10 slices of sweet brown bread
  2. Take 6-10 eggs (one for each slice of bread)
  3. Get 1 glass
  4. Prepare 1 small spoon of butter
  5. Take 1 pinch salt
  6. Prepare to taste Rosemary
  7. Make ready to taste Sliced bananas

Instructions to make Egg surprise:
  1. Take glass and press it into the bread to make a circle. Do it to all the slices of bread.
  2. Take your pan and add butter or oil. Next put your bread in then your egg in the center with salt and rosemary. Repeat the process again.
  3. When it's done on one side put the circle (that you cut out and put it on top and flip.
  4. When it's done add sliced bananas. And your done.
