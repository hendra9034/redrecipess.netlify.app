The ingredients needed to make Banana bread:
  1. Take 2 cups flour
  2. Make ready 2 eggs (room temp.)
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  4. Get 2 teaspoon baking soda
  5. Make ready 2-3 cups mashed ripe bananas
  6. Make ready pinch salt
  7. Take 1/2 cup raisin (soaked in red wine for 30 mins. or more and drained)
  8. Prepare 1/3 cup crushed peanut
  9. Get 2 tbsp sugar
  10. Take 1 tsp cinnamon powder
  11. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps to make Banana bread:
  1. Cream the butter and sugar until the color become pale or lemon yellow.
  2. Slightly beat the eggs and set aside.
  3. Mash the bananas, set aside
  4. Add the beaten eggs to butter mixture then bananas. Mix… Then add the flour mixture. (flour, salt and baking soda; sifted) gently fold in.
  5. Add raisins, cinnamon, peanut into batter mixture.
  6. Drop in prepared/greased pan (9 x 4 inch loaf pan) and shake a little to release the air. Then bake for 35-40 minutes at 250°C or until the skewer come out clean when you check.
