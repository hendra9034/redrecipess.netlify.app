The ingredients needed to make Banana Yoghurt Bread:
  1. Take 2 ripe bananas
  2. Take 1/4 cup (60 ml) coconut oil, melted
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup (60 ml) sugar
  4. Take 1 tsp lemon juice
  5. Take 2 eggs
  6. Make ready 2 cups plain greek yoghurt
  7. Get 3/4 cup (180 ml) pancake mix
Steps to make Banana Yoghurt Bread:
  1. Grease your baking dish.
  2. Place bananas in a mixing bowl, and mush with a folk. Add lemon juice.
  3. Preheat your oven 190°/375°.
  4. Add oil and sugar then combine well.
  5. Add eggs and combine well.
  6. Add yoghurt and pancake mix then combine well. If you don't have pancake mix, check my recipe. It's easy to make.
  7. Pour the batter into your baking dish. I used 9"×9" square tin.
  8. Bake about 35 mins or until a toothpick insert the center comes out clean.
  9. You can enjoy it warm or cool.
  10. You can sliced extra banana and add on top for garnish if desire.
