The ingredients needed to make Mocha banana bread:
  1. Get 2-3 Banana
  2. Get 2 Eggs
  3. Take Pain flour 1+3/4 c
  4. Prepare Sugar 1+1/2 c
  5. Prepare 3 tbsp Cocao powder
  6. Take 1/4 c Milk
  7. Make ready 1 tsp Baking soda
  8. Get 2 tsp Vinilla
  9. Take 1/2 c rice bran oil
  10. Take 1/2 tsp Salt
  11. Make ready 2 tbsp Mocha powder
  12. Take Almond slice for  sprinkle befor bake

Steps to make Mocha banana bread:
  1. Warm oven at 180C, used loaf mold and put baking paper inside, put aside
  2. Self pain flour,mocha powder,salt, baking soda, sugar, put aside
  3. In a bowl,Mash banana, set aside
  4. In another bowl, add eggs, vanilla, rice brand oil and milk. Mix well until combine.
  5. Add eggs mixed into banana bowl, mixed well, add mixed flour, and mixed until combine
  6. Pour batter into the loaf mold, and bake in oven at 180C around 1hr or until cook. Check cook by used a stick put into the cake and pull back, the stick have to dry. Its mean cooked.
