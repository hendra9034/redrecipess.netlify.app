The ingredients needed to make Bread pudding 2:
  1. Get 6 slice Bread
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp Melted butter
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup Raisins
  4. Get 4 Eggs, beaten
  5. Take 2 cup Milk
  6. Make ready 3/4 cup White sugar
  7. Make ready 1 tsp Cinnamon
  8. Take 1 tsp Vanilla

Instructions to make Bread pudding 2:
  1. Preheat oven 350.
  2. Break bread into sm. Pieces into 8 in pan. Drizzle melted butter over all and sprinkle w/raisins.
  3. In bowl mix eggs, milk, sugar, cinnamon and vanilla. Mix well. Pour over bread and push down w/fork til bread soaks up mixture.
