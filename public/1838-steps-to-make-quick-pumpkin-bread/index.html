The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Bread:
  1. Get 4 beaten eggs
  2. Make ready 3 cups sugar
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking powder
  4. Prepare 2 tsp baking soda
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Make ready 1 tsp cloves
  7. Prepare 1 tsp cinnamon
  8. Prepare 1 tsp nutmeg
  9. Prepare 2/3 cup water
  10. Take 1 cup mazola oil
  11. Take 3 1/2 cups flour
  12. Get 1 can (15 oz.) pumpkin

Instructions to make Pumpkin Bread:
  1. Mix all ingredients together well.
  2. Put in 2 large greased bread pans. (Or 3 medium pans)
  3. Bake 1 hr @ 325°F.
  4. Check to be sure the top doesn't jiggle.
  5. Enjoy!
