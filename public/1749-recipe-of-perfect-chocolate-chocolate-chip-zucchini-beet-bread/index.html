The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Chocolate Chip Zucchini Beet bread:
  1. Prepare 2 cup all-purpose flour
  2. Get 1/2 tsp baking powder
  3. Take 1/2 tsp baking soda
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Get 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  6. Take 4 large eggs
  7. Take 1 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  8. Take 1/2 cup vegetable oil or canola oil
  9. Get 1/2 cup sour cream
  10. Get 2 tsp vanilla extract
  11. Get 1 cup fresh grated raw beets
  12. Prepare 1 1/2 cup fresh grated zucchini ( seeded )
  13. Make ready 1 1/2 cup chocolate chips (of choice)
  14. Take optional topper:
  15. Make ready 1 tbsp shredded beets
  16. Take 1 tbsp shredded zucchini
Steps to make Chocolate Chocolate Chip Zucchini Beet bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 325°F
  2. Grease 2 smaller sized loaf pans.
  3. In a large bowl sift or wisk together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, cocoa powder and salt. Set aside.
  4. In another bowl wisk together the eggs, brown sugar, vanilla, sour cream and oil.
  5. Pour wet into dry.
  6. Stir until almost combined. Do not overmix.
  7. Squeeze out excess liquid from zucchini.
  8. Add zucchini and beets to the mix.
  9. Gently fold into batter until well distributed.
  10. Fold in chocolate chips.
  11. Divide batter equally between the two loaf pans.
  12. Bake at 325 for 30 minutes. Then sprinkle the optional grated beet and zucchini equally across the tops of each loaf.
  13. Continue baking an additional 25-35 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
  14. Let stand until your able to handle it.
  15. Loosen edges and turn out onto a wire rack to finish cooling. Once cooled enough to eat transfer to a serving dish.
  16. Slice and serve!
  17. Enjoy!
