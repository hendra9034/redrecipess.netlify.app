The ingredients needed to make Mushroom Zucchini Bread Pizza:
  1. Prepare 4 Bread slices
  2. Take 2 tsp Coriander leaves
  3. Prepare 3-5 tsp Zucchini
  4. Prepare 4-6 diced mushrooms
  5. Get as per taste Salt
  6. Get as per taste Black Pepper powder
  7. Get As per taste Cheese
  8. Get As per taste Any Sandwich spread/sauce
  9. Get 2-3 tbsp Sweet corns
  10. Make ready 1/2 chopped onion
  11. Take 2-3 Garlic cloves, chopped
  12. Take As Required Mixed herbs or oregano
  13. Prepare as required Chilli flakes

Instructions to make Mushroom Zucchini Bread Pizza:
  1. Heat oil or butter in a pan (low flame), add diced mushrooms, chopped Zucchini, coriander leaves, and a pinch of salt, pepper, mixed herbs, chilli flakes. Sauté everything for a minute or two. Turn off the flame and keep the mixture aside.
  2. Apply sandwich spread on bread slices. Add this mixture and corns on it.
  3. Then spread some cheese, mixed herbs, and chilli flakes on it. Now spread some butter in a pan (low flame), transfer the loaded bread slice to the pan.
  4. Cover it with a lid. Cook till the cheese melts. And it's ready to eat.
