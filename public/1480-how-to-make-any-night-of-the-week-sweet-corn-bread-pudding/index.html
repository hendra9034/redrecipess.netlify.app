The ingredients needed to make Sweet Corn Bread Pudding:
  1. Make ready 1/2 onion, diced fine
  2. Prepare 1 -ounce unsalted butter
  3. Take 1/2 teaspoon thyme
  4. Take 1/2 teaspoon rosemary
  5. Make ready 1 (15 ounce) can creamed style sweet corn
  6. Make ready 1 cup heavy cream
  7. Make ready 2 eggs
  8. Take 1 teaspoon baking powder
  9. Get 1/2 cup yellow cornmeal, whole grain, stone ground
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  11. Get 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  12. Make ready to taste Ground black pepper
  13. Get 2 cups French bread, cubed - https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/11150460-french-bread
Steps to make Sweet Corn Bread Pudding:
  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a Cast Iron Skillet, sweat onions with butter for 5-10 minutes until translucent then add herbs.
  3. Combine corn, cream, eggs, baking powder, cornmeal, parmesan, salt, and pepper in a large mixing bowl. Add cubed bread and fold to combine. Pour batter into skillet, right on top of the onion mixture. Bake 50 minutes, or until set. Cool slightly before serving.
