The ingredients needed to make Zucchini Bread:
  1. Prepare 1 1/2 cup All purpose flour
  2. Take 1 tsp Ground ginger
  3. Take 1 1/2 tsp Baking powder
  4. Prepare 1/4 tsp Salt
  5. Make ready 1 cup Granulated sugar
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup Vegetable oil
  7. Take 2 Eggs, slightly beaten
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp Fresh lemon juice
  9. Get 1 tsp Freshly grated lemon peel
  10. Take 1 cup Shredded zucchini
  11. Take 1/2 cup Chopped walnuts

Steps to make Zucchini Bread:
  1. Grease and flour 9x5x3 inch loaf pan, put aside.
  2. In large bowl stir together flour, ginger, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  3. Add oil, eggs, lemon juice and peel, shredded zucchini, and chopped walnuts. Stir to blend. Do not over mix.
  4. Spoon batter into prepared pan.
  5. Bake at 350°F for 45 to55 minutes, or until a wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool in pan on wire rack for 5 to 10 minutes.
