The ingredients needed to make banana pumpkin bread:
  1. Take 2 ripe banana
  2. Take 2 egg
  3. Get 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  4. Get 1 1/3 cup canned pumpkin
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup honey
  6. Get 1/2 cup sugar
  7. Make ready 2 1/2 cup flour
  8. Prepare 2 tsp baking powder
  9. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  10. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  11. Get 2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
  12. Get 1 tsp cinnamon

Steps to make banana pumpkin bread:
  1. preheat oven to 350
  2. grease 9x5in loaf pan
  3. mash banana in a large bowl
  4. add eggs, oil, pumpkin, honey, sugar
  5. stir flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, pumpkin pie spice, cinnamon in a seperate bowl
  6. combine flour mix into banana-pumpkin mix while stiring
  7. cook 55 mins, test with toothpick until the toothpick comes out clean
