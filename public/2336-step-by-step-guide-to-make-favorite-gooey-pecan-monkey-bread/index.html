Instructions to make Gooey Pecan Monkey Bread:
  1. Heat oven to 350°
  2. Spray or grease bundt pan
  3. Cut biscuits in quarters
  4. Mix granulated sugar and 1 tablespoon cinnamon in a gallon size zip lock bag. You can add a dash of nutmeg to this mixture for added flavor.
  5. Add buscuit quarters to cinnamon sugar mixture, shake until well coated. Do only a few biscuits at a time to cover evenly.
  6. Add coated biscuits to greased bundt pan.
  7. Melt butter, brown sugar and remaining cinnamon in small saucepan. Add pecans. Pour this mixture over biscuits. I like to mix the biscuits up at this point so the sauce gets all into all the crevices.
  8. Bake at 350° 30-45 mins. Allow to cool 10 minutes, turn pan upside down onto dish
  9. Pull apart. Enjoy!
