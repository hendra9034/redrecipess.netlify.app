Instructions to make Zucchini Nut Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. In a large bowl mix together all dry ingredients and set aside.
  3. In a medium bowl, beat eggs together with oil, water, and lemon juice.
  4. Pour egg mixture and shredded zucchini into dry ingredients and mix well.
  5. Fold in walnuts.
  6. Pour batter into two lightly greased 9-inch loaf pans and bake for 1 hour, or until toothpick inserted into center comes out clean.
