The ingredients needed to make Chocolate chunk cinnamon banana bread:
  1. Get 4 rotten bananas
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup chocolate chips
  3. Get 1/2 cup brown sugar
  4. Make ready 1 tsp nutmeg
  5. Get 1 Tbsp Cinnamon
  6. Get 2 cups whole wheat flour
  7. Take 3/4 tsp baking soda
  8. Take 4 Tbsp softened butter
  9. Get 2 eggs
  10. Make ready 1/3 cup plain yogurt
  11. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla

Instructions to make Chocolate chunk cinnamon banana bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350. Spray a 9×5-inch pan
  2. In a kitchenaid mixer, mix butter, brown sugar, and eggs. Add bananas, yogurt, and vanilla.
  3. Mix dry ingredients together and then pour into mixer. Mix well but do not over mix!
  4. Pour half of mixture in pan, sprinkle chocolate chips, a pinch of brown sugar, and a pinch of cinnamon over it. Then add the remaining mixture on top. Top off with more cinnamon if desired!
  5. Bake for 55 minutes then let stand and cool for 15.
