The ingredients needed to make Outback Steakhouse-style Molasses Brown bread:
  1. Get 300 g warm water
  2. Prepare 5 g instant dry yeast
  3. Prepare 180 g whole wheat flour
  4. Make ready 21 g oil
  5. Prepare 21 g butter
  6. Prepare 80 g dark molasses
  7. Take 85 g honey
  8. Take 180-240 g bread flour
  9. Prepare 7.5 g cocoa
  10. Get 6 g salt
Instructions to make Outback Steakhouse-style Molasses Brown bread:
  1. Mix water, yeast, whole wheat flour, butter, oil, molasses and honey together until smooth.
  2. Mix dry ingredients together, then gradually incorporate with wet mixture.
  3. Cover and let rise for 60-90 minutes
  4. Shape into six smallish loaves, cover and let rise until doubled.
  5. Bake at 190 degrees Celsius (375F) for at least 20 minutes.
  6. Once out of the oven, brush tops with butter and sprinkle with oats, if desired. Bake for another 3 to 5 minutes.
