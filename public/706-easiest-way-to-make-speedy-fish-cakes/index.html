The ingredients needed to make Fish Cakes:
  1. Get 3 tins tuna in salt water (you can also use hake that is boiled first)
  2. Get 1 onion chopped
  3. Make ready 2 slices bread soaked
  4. Get 1 egg
  5. Get 1 handful fresh curly parsley chopped
  6. Get 1 handful spring onion
  7. Prepare 1 handful fresh dhania chopped
  8. Get 1 tsp green chillies
  9. Make ready 1 tsp Salt
  10. Take 1 tsp white pepper
  11. Take 1 tsp nutmeg
  12. Take 1/2 tsp aromat
  13. Take 1/2 tsp chilli powder
  14. Get 1 tsp crushed garlic
  15. Prepare Bread crumbs to coat
  16. Take flour to coat
Steps to make Fish Cakes:
  1. Remove tuna from tin and strain the water. Add to a bowl.
  2. Remove water from soaked bread, break into pieces and add to tuna bowl.
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients - chopped onions, soaked bread, egg, chopped curly parsley, spring onion, chopped dhania, green chillies, salt, nutmeg, aromat, chilli powder, crushed garlic - to the bowl. Mix thoroughly.
  4. Mixture should not be watery. If it is, you can add bread crumbs and flour. Mould into circles and press down gently.
  5. Coat the fish cakes with bread crumbs.
  6. Shallow fry in oil until golden and crispy.
  7. Serve with tomato chutney, rice and mash.
