The ingredients needed to make BANANA BREAD PUDDING:
  1. Make ready 5 bread rolls ( Day old is best )
  2. Get 1 pints milk
  3. Make ready 2 tsp Condensed milk
  4. Get 2 tbsp Dark brown sugar
  5. Take 3 Bananas
  6. Take 1/2 tsp powdered Cinnamon ( optional )

Steps to make BANANA BREAD PUDDING:
  1. In a large Oven Dish about 2 inch Depth, break up the Rolls into the dish.
  2. In a jug mix milk and the condenced milk together, and pour over Bread.
  3. In a bowl mix sugar and banana,s together then add it to the bread mix , turn it over and mix in the banana mixture.if using cinnamon mix in with the sugar and banana mixture.
  4. Cook on 150 for 30 mins , then grill the top, so its browned. Serve Cold with Honey poured on or just by itself
