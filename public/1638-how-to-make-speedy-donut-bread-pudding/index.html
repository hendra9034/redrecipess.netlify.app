Instructions to make Donut bread pudding:
  1. preheat 325°F
  2. cut up donuts and set aside
  3. mix eggs,sugar,milk,vanilla,condensed milk
  4. mix ingredients well and pour into a sprayed 9×13 pan
  5. bake at 325 for 30 min
  6. add Carmel dolce sauce on top
