The ingredients needed to make vegan bread pudding:
  1. Make ready 1 cup vanille soy milk
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup brown sugar
  3. Make ready 1/4 maple syrup
  4. Make ready 1 medium baguette
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp cocoa
  6. Get 100 ml chocolate chips

Instructions to make vegan bread pudding:
  1. preheat the oven 350°F.
  2. mix warm soy milk with sugar and syrup
  3. pour the milk mix onto the chunks made of the baguette and let it soak for five minutes
  4. add the chocolate chips and mix( don't mush it)
  5. place it in an oven dish and sprinkle the cocoa powder on top. put it in the oven for half an hour.
