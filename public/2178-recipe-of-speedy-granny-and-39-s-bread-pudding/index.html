Steps to make Granny's Bread Pudding:
  1. make18 biscuits using directions on a box of biscuits you can make them from scratch if you want. They do not have to be pretty.
  2. after you make your biscuits let em cool then crumble them in a large mixing bowl
  3. add about a cup of sugar you may want to taste it after you get everything mixed.. it's one of those (til it ) recipes
  4. add vanilla
  5. add your eggs
  6. add your milk you may want to add a little more or less. when you mix the ingredients it should have a very moist consistency.
  7. add your chocolate chips.. I have used butterscotch chips and carmel as well but chocolate chips are the best and the original.
  8. mix it all together again..
  9. poor into a well greased 9x13 pan.. I like using an angel food cake pan too.
  10. bake on 350 for 45 minutes to 1hr..
  11. you'll know it's done when its golden brown and a toothpick comes out clean….
