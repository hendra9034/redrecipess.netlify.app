The ingredients needed to make Zucchini bread:
  1. Get 3 cup flour
  2. Prepare 1 1/3 tbsp ground cinnamon
  3. Take 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  4. Get 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  5. Take 1/4 tsp baking powder
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  7. Make ready 3 eggs
  8. Make ready 3/4 cup vegetable oil
  9. Take 1/2 cup honey
  10. Make ready 3 cup zucchini grated

Instructions to make Zucchini bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 325.
  2. Combine all dry ingredients.
  3. Make well in middle of dry ingredients and add everything else except zucchini and nuts. Mix until combined, but don't over do it.
  4. Mix in zucchini and nuts.
  5. Pour into loaf pan and bake 60 to 70 minutes, but your time may vary depending on various factors.
