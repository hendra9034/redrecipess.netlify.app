The ingredients needed to make Whole Wheat Bread:
  1. Take 3.5 cups whole wheat flour
  2. Prepare 1.5 cups white flour
  3. Prepare 1 cup whole rolled oats
  4. Make ready 2 tablespoons golden flaxseed
  5. Make ready 2 tablespoons sunflower seeds
  6. Make ready 3 tablespoons whole milk powder
  7. Get 1 teaspoon salt
  8. Get 1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
  9. Take 1 tablespoon dried instant yeast
  10. Prepare 1 teaspoon sugar
  11. Make ready Warm water
Steps to make Whole Wheat Bread:
  1. Add warm water to the yeast and sugar. Mix well and let it react.
  2. In a dough kneader's bowl, add all the dry ingredients then mix them with the dough hook.
  3. Add the yeast, olive oil, the gradually add the warm water until the dough is well mixed and to the desired consistency.
  4. Place the dough in an oiled pan. Apply olive oil on top of the dough 's surface. Cover with cling wrap. Let it puff over 60 - 90 minutes.
  5. Place in oven for 35 minutes at 180 degrees centigrade.
  6. Let it cool on a wrack then cut to slices.
