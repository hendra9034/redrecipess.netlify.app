The ingredients needed to make Whole wheat French Toast:
  1. Take 3 slice whole wheat bread
  2. Take 1 egg
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup milk
  4. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla
  5. Take 1/2 tbsp ap flour
  6. Make ready 1/2 tbsp brown sugar
  7. Prepare 3 scoop of vanilla icecream
  8. Make ready 3 mints leaves for garnish
  9. Take 1 chocolate syrups for decorate
  10. Make ready 1 icing for topping
  11. Take 40 grams butter ,seperate 20 grams for eggs mixture and 20 grm to fry
Steps to make Whole wheat French Toast:
  1. Cut the edge of 3 pieces of bread
  2. Cut each bread into 4 pieces equally
  3. Whisk egg,sugar,milk,vanilla ,flour,butter
  4. Heat fry pan with little butter ,dip bread into egg mixture
  5. Fry on low heat until light gold,then flip
  6. Arrange to serving plate ,4 pieces per person or more as you desire
  7. Serve with vanilla icecream ,drizzle chocolate,sprinkel some icing on top,garnish with mints :)
