The ingredients needed to make Gluten free Domino's Pizza cheesy bread:
  1. Make ready melted butter
  2. Prepare cornmeal
  3. Make ready shredded mozzarella cheese
  4. Make ready shredded cheddar cheese
  5. Take gouda cheese
  6. Take dry active yeast
  7. Prepare gluten free flour
  8. Prepare salt
  9. Get olive oil
  10. Get sugar
  11. Get garlic powder
  12. Make ready hot water
Instructions to make Gluten free Domino's Pizza cheesy bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a large bowl, combine hot water, yeast, and sugar.
  3. Set aside for 5 to 10 minutes to activate and become foamy.
  4. Mix in oil and salt; slowly stir in gluten free flour.
  5. Combine the gluten free dough and knead until smooth, adding more gluten free flour if it's too sticky.
  6. Cover and set aside for at least 30 minutes to rise.
  7. Roll the dough out into a rectangle and brush with melted butter.
  8. Fill half of your gluten free dough with garlic powder, cheddar, and mozzarella.
  9. Fold other half over cheese and pinch edges together.
  10. Brush with extra butter and sprinkle on extra cheese of choosing.
  11. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes until golden.
