The ingredients needed to make Caramel Monkey Bread:
  1. Make ready biscuits
  2. Get sugar
  3. Prepare cinnamon
  4. Take butter
  5. Get caramel ice cream topping
Instructions to make Caramel Monkey Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Spray 9" pie plate generously with non-stick cooking spray.
  3. Mix cinnamon and sugar in bowl.
  4. Quarter biscuits.
  5. Roll each biscuit quarter in cinnamon/sugar mixture until completely coated, then place in pie plate.
  6. In bowl, melt butter in microwave. Add caramel sauce & microwave another 30 seconds, or until butter & caramel can be blended together.
  7. Poor caramel sauce even over biscuits.
  8. Bake for 30-35 minutes, until evenly browned.
  9. Let rest for 15 minutes, then invert on plate.
