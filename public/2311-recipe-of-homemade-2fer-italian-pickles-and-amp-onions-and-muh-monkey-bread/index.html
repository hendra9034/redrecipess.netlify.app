The ingredients needed to make 2fer Italian pickles & onions and muh monkey bread:
  1. Prepare large cucumber
  2. Make ready white onion
  3. Take bottle white distilled vinegar
  4. Prepare Italian seasoning
  5. Get black pepper corn
  6. Take round loaf sourdough bread (I used hawian)
  7. Prepare shredded mozzarella cheese
  8. Get butter
  9. Get garlic salt(or garlic powder)
  10. Get glass pickle jar

Steps to make 2fer Italian pickles & onions and muh monkey bread:
  1. For the Italian pickles peel cut thinly along with onions a
  2. Add pepper corn and layer cucumber and onion add Italian seasoning hint u can add Cajun spice or garlic salt even cloves and make it ur own;)
  3. Pour vinegar over cucumber and onion coring completely add lid shake and pop in fridge chill for 2 Hours
  4. For Muh monkey bread take a round loaf of sourdough ( I used hawian) and city criss cross across the top
  5. Stuff butter in seams season then stuff mozzarella cheese in all open seams
  6. Bake on 250 so it don't burn til cheese is melted about 12 minutes
