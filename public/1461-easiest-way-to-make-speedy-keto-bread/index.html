The ingredients needed to make Keto bread:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup butter (melted)
  2. Make ready 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  3. Prepare 7 eggs
  4. Get 1 teaspoon baking powder
  5. Prepare 2 cups almond flour
  6. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon xanthium gum
  7. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon salt
  8. Make ready Sweetner (optional)

Steps to make Keto bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 180c
  2. Put the eggs into a bowl and beat for 1-2 mins on high
  3. Add coconut oil and melted butter to eggs, continue beating
  4. Add remaining ingredients. Will become thick
  5. Scrape into a baking pan lined with baking paper
  6. Bake for 45 minutes. (Remove once a skewer comes out of the middle clean)
