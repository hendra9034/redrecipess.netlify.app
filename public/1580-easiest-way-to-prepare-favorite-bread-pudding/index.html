  3. Take 3 EGGS, BEATEN
  4. Take 2 tbsp VANILLA
  5. Make ready 3/4 cup SUGAR
  6. Take 1 CINNAMON
  7. Make ready 1 BUTTER

  2. SPRINKLE CINNAMON AND BUTTER ON TOP.
