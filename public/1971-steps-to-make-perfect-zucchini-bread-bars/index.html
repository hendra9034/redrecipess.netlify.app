Instructions to make Zucchini Bread Bars:
  1. Place shredded zucchini in bowl and place a bowl on the zucchini then weigh down the bowl
  2. Whisk eggs, vanilla, oil in a big bowl
  3. Add salt, ginger, cinnamon, baking powder, baking soda
  4. Mix well
  5. Separate bowl mix sugar and flour
  6. Slowly add flour mixture to egg mixture while mixing
  7. Mix well. Scrape sides of bowl. Mix well
  8. Using a clean and thin cloth, strain zucchini from excess water
  9. Fold zucchini in batter. Fold well to ensure it is throughout the batter
  10. Grease and lightly flour a baking pan
  11. Fold bread into pan and spread it even
  12. Bake 350*F 40-60 minutes til golden brown and a knife coming out clean when pierced
  13. Cool. Cut. Serve
  14. Optional: fold in 1 cup of nuts your choice after folding in zucchini
