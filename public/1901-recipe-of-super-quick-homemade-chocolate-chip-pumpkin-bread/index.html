The ingredients needed to make chocolate chip pumpkin bread:
  1. Take 1/2 cup butter
  2. Take 1 1/4 cup sugar
  3. Get 3 large eggs
  4. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Get 1 cup canned pumpkin (not pumpkin pie filling)
  6. Make ready 1 3/4 cup flour
  7. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
  8. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  10. Take 1/2 tsp each: cinnamon, ground ginger, ground nutmeg
  11. Make ready 1/3 cup milk
  12. Make ready 3/4 cup chocolate chips or raisins
  13. Make ready 3/4 cup chopped walnuts or pecans

Instructions to make chocolate chip pumpkin bread:
  1. heat oven to 350°
  2. grease and flour a 9x5 inch loaf pan
  3. cream butter in large bowl. gradually adding the sugar. add the eggs one at a time. add vanilla extract and pumpkin.
  4. in seperate bowl sift flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, and spices.
  5. blend a third of the flour mixture into the butter mixture. then add a half the milk. alternate additions of the remaining flour mixture and milk, blending well after each addition. fold in the chocolate chips and walnuts.
  6. scrape batter into prepared pan. bake about 50 to 60 minutes.
