The ingredients needed to make Chocolate banana bread:
  1. Prepare A. Dry ingredients
  2. Take 175 grams (1 and 1/4 cup) plain flour - sifted
  3. Get 40 grams (1/2 cup) cocoa powder -sifted
  4. Make ready 1 teaspoon baking soda
  5. Take 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  6. Get 60 grams (1/2 cup) brown sugar
  7. Prepare 40 grams (1/4 cup) caster sugar
  8. Take 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  9. Get B. Wet ingredients
  10. Get 2 overripe Bananas - mash by fork - mixed with a tsp of lemon
  11. Take 115 grams (1/2 cup) or 1 stick) unsalted butter- melted & cool
  12. Make ready 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  13. Make ready 2 large eggs - at room temperature
  14. Prepare 60 ml (1/4 cup) milk - at room temperature
  15. Make ready Other
  16. Get 75 grams (1/2 cup) chocolate chips

Instructions to make Chocolate banana bread:
  1. Grease and line your 9 x 5 inch baking tin with baking or parchment paper. Preheat oven to 170oC (350oF)
  2. Mix dry ingredients by a whisk. In another bowl, mix wet ingredients until combined. Make a well in the middle of your dry ingredients, add wet ingredients and mashed banana. Very gently fold the mixture using a spatula or wooden spoon until the mixture is just combined – be careful not to over mix.
  3. Add 1/4 cup of chocolate chips to the batter. Pour mixture into prepared tin and sprinkle 1/4 cup of chocolate chips on top.
  4. Bake for approximately 45-50 minutes or until a knife inserted into the middle comes out clean. Leave to cool on a wire rack.
