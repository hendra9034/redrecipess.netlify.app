The ingredients needed to make Whole Wheat Bread Machine Rolls:
  1. Make ready 1 cup warm milk
  2. Make ready 1/2 - cup room temperature butter
  3. Make ready 2 eggs
  4. Prepare 1 cup all purpose flour
  5. Get 3/4 tsp salt
  6. Make ready 3 cup whole wheat flour
  7. Make ready 3 tsp instant yeast
  8. Make ready 1/4 to 1/2 cup additional warm milk
Instructions to make Whole Wheat Bread Machine Rolls:
    1. Place all ingredients in order given in your bread machine. Start dough setting. Check after 5 min. To see if you need more liquid or flour. You don't want it too dry. Add the additional milk or flour if needed. I prefer to have a little more moisture.
    1. Remove from pan when cycle is finished. And shape dough pieces into balls of approximately the same size. Place on a greased baking sheet.
    1. If you are freezing, you may do it now for up to a month.
    1. Before baking, let rolls rise under a light kitchen towel, in a draft free area for about 40 min.
    1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Bake rolls approximately 20 to 25 minutes or until golden brown. Remove from oven and let cool slightly. Serve warm or at room temperature.
  6. If you are baking from the from the frozen state you will need to leave out to thaw completely and then let them do the second rising. This will probably take about 12 hours.
