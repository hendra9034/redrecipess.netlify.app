The ingredients needed to make Classic burger: summer delight:
  1. Prepare 400 g minced beef
  2. Make ready 50 g white bread crumbs
  3. Take 1 egg yolk
  4. Take 1/2 red onion finely chopped
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp dried mustard powder
  6. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  7. Get 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  8. Make ready Ground black pepper
  9. Prepare To serve
  10. Take 4 burger buns halved
  11. Prepare 1 round lettuce shredded
  12. Prepare 2 tomatoes sliced
  13. Prepare 1 small onion finely sliced
  14. Prepare 1 pickle finely sliced

Steps to make Classic burger: summer delight:
  1. Prepare barbecue for cooking. In a large bowl mix together all the burger ingredients until well mixed
  2. With damp hands divide the mixture into 4 balls and roll each between your palms until smooth. Flatten each ball out into a large fat disc to thickness of about 3 cm? And pat the edges in to tidy them up
  3. Place the burgers on a plate, cover with cling film, and chill for 30 minutes.
  4. Cook over a hot barbecue for 6 to 8 minutes until the meat is springy to the touch and the edges charred. Toast the buns on the inside to make them crispy
  5. Assemble the burgers and serve. Bon appetite!
