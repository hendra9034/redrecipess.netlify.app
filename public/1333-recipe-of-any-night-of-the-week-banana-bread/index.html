The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread:
  1. Take 2 cups flour
  2. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  3. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  4. Prepare 2 tsp cinnamon
  5. Take 1 egg
  6. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  7. Take 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  8. Prepare 2 tbs milk
  9. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
  10. Make ready 3 banana, mashed
  11. Take 1/2 cup nut (walnut, almond, etc), optional
  12. Make ready Choco chips, optional

Steps to make Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 325F
  2. Mixed well all the dry ingredients
  3. Stir egg, sugar and oil.
  4. Combine wet mixture to dry ingredients. Add in milk, vanilla extract and mashed banana. Stir until well blended.
  5. Mixed in chopped nuts, divide to two small loaves. Sprinkle choco chips on top.
  6. Oven for 50-75 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean. Tent the loaves with aluminum foil if the surface too brown.
