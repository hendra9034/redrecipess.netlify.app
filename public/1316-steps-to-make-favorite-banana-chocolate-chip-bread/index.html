The ingredients needed to make Banana chocolate chip bread:
  1. Prepare 4 over ripe bananas
  2. Prepare 2/3 cup brown sugar
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup oil
  4. Take 2 eggs
  5. Prepare 1 3/4 cup flour
  6. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  8. Get 1 cup chocolate chips
  9. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions to make Banana chocolate chip bread:
  1. Mashed the bananas.
  2. In another bowl, mixed sugar oil and eggs. Mix.
  3. Add the sugar mixture to the mashed bananas. Then put in vanilla extract. Mix.
  4. In a clean bowl mix all the dry ingredients (flour salt and baking powder).
  5. Pour in the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients. Mix.
  6. Add in the chocolate chips.
  7. Preheat oven. And baked in 45 mins at 350 farenheit.
