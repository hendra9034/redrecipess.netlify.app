The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup butter
  2. Take 1 cup sugar
  3. Get 2 eggs, beaten
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp vanilla
  5. Prepare 3-4 bananas, mashed
  6. Make ready 1 1/2 cup flour
  7. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt

Steps to make Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit (325 for glass pan)
  2. Beat sugar with butter
  3. Add eggs, bananas and vanilla
  4. Separately combine flour, baking soda, and salt. Add to wet mixture and mix (donâ€™t overmix)
