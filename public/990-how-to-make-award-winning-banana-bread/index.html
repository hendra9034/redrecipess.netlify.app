The ingredients needed to make Banana bread:
  1. Make ready 3 very ripe medium large banana mashed
  2. Get 80 g melted salted butter
  3. Get 1/2 tsp baking powder
  4. Take 100 g fine granulated sugar
  5. Make ready Large egg (60g)
  6. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
  7. Take 100 g self raising + 110g All-purpose flour

Steps to make Banana bread:
  1. Preheat oven 175C, top and bottom heat
  2. Mixed banana with butter well
  3. Add baking powder and mix again
  4. Add sugar and mix well
  5. Add egg and mix very well then add vanilla extract and mix
  6. Add sifted flour and mix well
  7. Bake in loaf pan (4x8 long loaf) for 50 mins
  8. To add Nutella (optional), pour in half of the batter, the add few spoonful Nutella and give a swirl then add the remaining batter
