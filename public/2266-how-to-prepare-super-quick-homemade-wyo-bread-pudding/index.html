Instructions to make Wyo Bread Pudding:
  1. Pre-heat oven to 350°F
  2. For the pudding layer add the surgar, eggs, cinnamon, & nutmeg together wisking as you add the ingredients so no clumps are made. Then add the vanilla and stir.
  3. In a greased glass baking dish 9" X 13" or 3qt baking dish evenly spread out the bread pieces.
  4. Pour the pudding mixture evenly over the bread in the baking dish. Let sit for 10 min. Then place in the oven for 40 min.
  5. In annother bowl mix the brown surgar, softend butter and chopped pacans. Place to the side
  6. When the timer goes off on the pudding, pull it out of the oven and sprinkle the crust mixture evenly over the pudding and place it back in the oven at 350°F for 10 min.
  7. When the pudding is placed back in the oven, start the sauce.
