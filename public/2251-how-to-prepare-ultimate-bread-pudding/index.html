Instructions to make bread pudding:
  1. preheat oven to 375
  2. stir milk, cream and cinnamon together
  3. stir eggs, sugars and vanilla into milk mix
  4. stir in bread and moisten well.
  5. pour into pan or ramekins
  6. bake 40 min or until browned and puffed
  7. top with whipped cream when cooled.
