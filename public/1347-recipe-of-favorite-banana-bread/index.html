The ingredients needed to make Banana bread:
  1. Get 3 ripe bananas (black or spotty)
  2. Take Pecans (optional)
  3. Make ready 8 tbs butter (1stick, 1/2 cup)
  4. Get 1/2 cup sugar
  5. Take 1/2 cup brown sugar
  6. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla
  7. Make ready 2 eggs
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  10. Make ready 2 cups flour
  11. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  12. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
  13. Get 1/4 tsp salt

Steps to make Banana bread:
  1. Ripen bananas (if needed, 325F, for 15-20minutes, they will be spotty or black, or if using previously frozen bananas, just thaw and they will ripen)
  2. Grease/butter loaf pan. (I use bakers goop, recipe in page)
  3. Cream sugars, butter. Add eggs, one at a time, and vanilla. (In mix bowl)
  4. In separate bowl mash bananas.
  5. In a 3rd bowl mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, nutmeg, cinnamon, and salt with a whisk or fork.
  6. Add banana mixture into butter in the mixer.
  7. Add dry ingredients, do not over mix.
  8. Bake on 300F for 25 minutes (mini loaf) —35 minutes (medium loaf) —1 hr 10 minutes (full size loaf)
