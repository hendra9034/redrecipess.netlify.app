The ingredients needed to make Awesome banana, carmel bread pudding:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup margarine or butter
  2. Take 1 cup horchata dry mix
  3. Make ready 3 large eggs
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup sour cream
  5. Take 1 cup Carmel Macciado creamer
  6. Prepare 1 1/2 cup milk
  7. Take 2 medium banana
  8. Get 1 loaf heavy bread (French, challah)
  9. Get 2 small mini cups of Carmel chocolate creamer

Steps to make Awesome banana, carmel bread pudding:
  1. pre heat oven 325
  2. in large bowl, mix margarine with horchata mix till creamy. add egg one at a time. mix in sour cream.
  3. mix in Carmel Macciado creamer and milk
  4. slice bananas, add to mixture.
  5. slice bread in squares or pull apart. add to mixture til most has been absorbed into bread.
  6. pour all into a greased 9x13 baking pan
  7. pour little creamers on top. put in oven for 60-90 minutes
  8. let cool (if desired) before eating (I liked mine hot)
