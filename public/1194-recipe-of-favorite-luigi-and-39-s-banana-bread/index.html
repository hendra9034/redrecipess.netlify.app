The ingredients needed to make Luigi's Banana Bread:
  1. Take 3/4 cup brown sugar
  2. Take 1/2 cup oil
  3. Make ready 2 eggs
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp milk
  5. Take 3-4 pcs ripe banana
  6. Prepare 2 cups all-purpose flour
  7. Take 1 tspn baking powder
  8. Make ready 1 tspn baking soda
  9. Prepare 1 tspn salt
Steps to make Luigi's Banana Bread:
  1. Whisk together sugar and oil. Add eggs and milk until fully combined.
  2. Mix in mashed bananas.
  3. In a separate bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Mix wet and dry ingredients.
  5. Cook for 56mins at 160 C°.
