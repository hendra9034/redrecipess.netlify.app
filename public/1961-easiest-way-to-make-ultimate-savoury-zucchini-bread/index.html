Steps to make Savoury Zucchini Bread:
  1. In a mixing bowl combine whole wheat flour, oats flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, sugar, garlic powder, onion powder, lemon zest, Italian herbs and chilli flakes. Now in another bowl mix rest of the ingredients (except zucchini) and mix well.
  2. Then combine the wet and dry ingredients and mix well with a spatula. Now fold in grated zucchini. Transfer the batter to the lined baking mould and bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean and the crust becomes nice and golden brown. Take it out and let it cool. Slice it and enjoy.
