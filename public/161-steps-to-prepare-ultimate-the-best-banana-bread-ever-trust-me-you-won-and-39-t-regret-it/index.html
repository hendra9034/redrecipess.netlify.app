The ingredients needed to make The best banana bread ever! (Trust me you won't regret it):
  1. Get 1 cup sugar
  2. Take 1/2 cup butter
  3. Prepare 4 ripe bananas (finely crushed)
  4. Prepare 2 eggs (beaten)
  5. Get 1 1/2 cup flour
  6. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  7. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp vanilla extract or cinnamon (I used cinnamon)
  9. Take This is what I added on mine
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup semi sweet chocolate chips

Instructions to make The best banana bread ever! (Trust me you won't regret it):
  1. Preheat oven 350F
  2. Cream butter and sugar(I used mixers)
  3. Add eggs and crushed banana, mixed together until well combined
  4. Add flour, baking soda,salt, cinnamon to the cream mixture. Stirred until combined but do not over mix
  5. Add walnuts and chocolate chips to the mixture and combined
  6. Pour the mixture to the greased and flour loaf pan
  7. Bake for 55-60 minutes. Keeps well refrigerated.
