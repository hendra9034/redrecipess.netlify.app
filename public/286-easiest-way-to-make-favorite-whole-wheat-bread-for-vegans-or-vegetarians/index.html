The ingredients needed to make Whole Wheat Bread for Vegans or Vegetarians:
  1. Make ready 300 grams Bread (strong) flour
  2. Make ready 100 grams Whole wheat flour
  3. Take 7 grams Beet sugar
  4. Take 5 grams Salt
  5. Take 4 grams Dried yeast
  6. Make ready 260 grams Water
Instructions to make Whole Wheat Bread for Vegans or Vegetarians:
  1. Put all of the ingredients in a bowl and mix.
  2. Leave to proof for 45 minutes at 30℃. After the first rising, divide the dough into desired portions, then let rest.
  3. After shaping, proof for the second time for 25 minutes at 30℃, sprinkle with whole wheat flour, then slash the tops.
  4. Bake for 17 minutes at 200℃.
