Instructions to make Banana wheat choco bread:
  1. Smash banana well with half cup powdered sugar.
  2. Add 1 tbsp melted butter, 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 tsp vanila extract, 1 egg. Beat well.
  3. Add 1 cup wheat flour, 2 tbsp cocoa powder, 1 tsp baking powder, 1/2 tsp baking soda, 1 pinch salt. Mix well. Don’t beat. Add nuts if you wish.
  4. Transfer to loaf pan. Bake at 375 F for 30 mins.
