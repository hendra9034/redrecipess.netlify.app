The ingredients needed to make Strawberry Banana Bread:
  1. Prepare Main
  2. Get 1 1/2 cups chopped fresh strawberries
  3. Take 2 loaf pans
  4. Get First Mix
  5. Prepare 3 bananas
  6. Take 1/2 cup (1 stick) butter
  7. Take 1 cup sugar
  8. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  9. Get 2 large eggs
  10. Make ready Second Mix
  11. Get 1/2 tsp. salt
  12. Prepare 1 tsp. baking SODA
  13. Make ready 2 cups flour
Instructions to make Strawberry Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat oven 375F
  2. Oil loaf pans
  3. Cream sugar and butter together
  4. Add eggs to butter, sugar mix and mix together
  5. Add vanilla, and bananas, you can mash the bananas first to make it easier. Mix well. Mine were soft enough to not need premashing.
  6. Mix flour, salt and baking soda together in a different container.
  7. Slowly add the flour mixture while mixing. Do not overmix.
  8. Clean and chop your strawberries, place in a separate bowl
  9. Add 1 Tbl. Flour to the strawberries, shake to coat.
  10. Fold flour coated strawberries into batter.
  11. Divide batter between loaf pans.
  12. Bake for 15 minutes. Then turn oven down to 350F, bake 30 more minutes
  13. Remove, allow to cool completely before removing from pan!
  14. Once completely cool, invert pan to remove
  15. Serve
