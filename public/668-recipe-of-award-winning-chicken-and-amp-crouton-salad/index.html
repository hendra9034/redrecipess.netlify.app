The ingredients needed to make Chicken & crouton Salad:
  1. Get 3 tbsp plain yoghurt
  2. Prepare 1 tsp Worcester sauce
  3. Take 1/2 tsp English mustard
  4. Take 2 anchovies finely sliced (optional)
  5. Get zest of one lemon
  6. Prepare 1/2 lemon juice
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  8. Take 2 tbsp evoo
  9. Take 15 grams Parmesan
  10. Make ready 1 romaine lettuce, sliced
  11. Prepare 1 chicory or radicchio finely sliced
  12. Make ready 1/2 cauliflower v finely sliced (wafer thin)
  13. Take 1 small red onion, finely sliced (wafer thin)
  14. Prepare 2 chicken breasts (120g each) / tofu or quorn
  15. Get handful rosemary leaves
  16. Take 1 thick slice brown bread, cubed

Steps to make Chicken & crouton Salad:
  1. Put all ingredients from yoghurt to Parmesan in large bowl and mix. Test for seasoning
  2. Put all salad in dressing bowl and mix
  3. Chicken on grease proof paper w salt and rosemary and bash, put in fry pan with little (anchovy) oil. 4 mins per side. at turn, add bread cubes to fry, keep toasting, even after removing chicken. Can sub tofu or quorn for chicken, just rub in rosemary and evoo first.
  4. Load plate w salad, slice chicken to 1cm thick and put on side, add hot croutons, one grate of Parmesan over top
