Instructions to make Cinnamon cream cheese monkey bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 dregrees. Spray a bundt or tube pan lightly with cooking spray.
  2. Cut your block of cream cheese into 24 small squares.
  3. Flatten each cinnamon roll with your fingers and place one square of cream cheese in the center of the flattened roll. Fold the roll around the cream cheese and pinch to seal. Place in prepared pan. Repeat with remaining rolls and cream cheese.
  4. In microwave safe bowl combine butter and brown sugar. Heat for approximately 1 minute until butter is melted. Stir together and pour over the cinnamon rolls.
  5. Bake 35-40 minutes until top is golden brown.Let cool in pan for 5 minutes and then flip pan onto a cake plate or platter.
  6. Drizzle with the frosting that came in the cinnamon roll cans, if desired.Serve warm
