The ingredients needed to make Banana and Chocolate chips bread using a bread machine:
  1. Get 250 g bread flour
  2. Take 20 g brown sugar
  3. Take 30 g butter
  4. Make ready 3 g instant dry yeast
  5. Prepare 3 g salt
  6. Take 210 g banana (about for 2 bananas)
  7. Prepare 60 g chocolate chips
