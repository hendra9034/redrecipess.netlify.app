The ingredients needed to make Easy Gourmet Bread Pudding:
  1. Prepare 1 pints half & half creamer
  2. Prepare 2 eggs
  3. Take 1/2 cup sugar
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Make ready 1 pinch salt
  6. Take 4 slices raisin bread
  7. Make ready 1/4 cup raisins
  8. Prepare cinnamon
Instructions to make Easy Gourmet Bread Pudding:
  1. Preheat oven to 300°F
  2. Beat creamer, eggs, sugar, salt, and vanilla extract until smooth (p.s. half & half won't turn thick like heavy cream does)
  3. Cut bread into cubes and place in bottom of 8x8 or 9x9 baking dish
  4. Pour mixture over bread and layer raisins and cinnamon on top.
  5. Make a double boiler: Set this baking dish with ingredients inside of larger baking dish, and fill larger baking dish with hot water until half way full.
  6. Bake at 300°F for 1.5 hours
  7. Serve hot or cold, tastes like magic!
