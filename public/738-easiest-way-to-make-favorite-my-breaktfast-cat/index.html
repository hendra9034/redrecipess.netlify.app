The ingredients needed to make My breaktfast cat:
  1. Prepare 1 strawberry
  2. Get 6 pretzel sticks
  3. Prepare peanut butter or jelly
  4. Prepare 1 banana
  5. Get 3 raisins
  6. Make ready 1 slice bread

Steps to make My breaktfast cat:
  1. Put the bread in the toaster for 5 min as the head.
  2. Put the peanut butter/jelly on the toast as the face.
  3. Cut the strawberry in half and place them on the top left and right as the ears.
  4. Take the banana and cut 2 slices out of it to were there are 2 circles. Then take the 2 circles and place them in the middle bellow the strawberries as the eyes.
  5. Take 2 of the raisins and put them on the bananas as the pupils and then put the last raisin in the middle of the bananas as the nose.
  6. Take the pretzel sticks and put 3 on each side of the nose as the whiskers.
  7. Now your done!
