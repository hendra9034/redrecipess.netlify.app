The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread in Pancake Mix:
  1. Make ready 2 cups pancake mix
  2. Get 1/3 cup margarine or butter room temp
  3. Take 2 ripe bananas medium size
  4. Get 2 eggs medium size
Instructions to make Banana Bread in Pancake Mix:
  1. Mashed bananas in a bowl then mix butter/margarine.
  2. Followed by 2 eggs one at a time and then pancake mix until the batter is smooth without bubbles.
  3. Baked at 170-180C for about 30-45 minutes depends on what kind of pan you used.
