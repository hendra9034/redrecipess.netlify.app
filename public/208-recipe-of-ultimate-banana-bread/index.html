The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread:
  1. Prepare 1 cup mashed, overripe bananas
  2. Prepare 2 Tbl molasses or apple butter
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup melted butter
  4. Prepare 1 1/3 cups sugar
  5. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  6. Prepare 2 cups all-purpose flour
  7. Prepare 2 eggs
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  9. Make ready 2 tsp baking soda

Steps to make Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Grease a loaf pan
  3. Combine banana and molasses in mixing bowl.
  4. Add the butter and sugar.
  5. Add the eggs one at a time.
  6. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  7. Add the baking soda and salt.
  8. Add the flour, mixing one cup at a time.
  9. Stir until the consistency is smooth and uniform.
  10. Spread batter evenly into loaf pan.
  11. Place in oven and bake for 50 minutes to one hour. Use a toothpick to see if bread is done. If the toothpick is not clean, bake for another 5 to ten minutes.
