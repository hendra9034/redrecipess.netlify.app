The ingredients needed to make Super moist banana bread:
  1. Prepare 6 over ripe bananas about 2 and a half cups
  2. Make ready 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  3. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  5. Get 2 cup flour
  6. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  7. Take 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup chopped pecans optional
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup chocolate chips optional
  10. Prepare 2 eggs

Steps to make Super moist banana bread:
  1. Combine butter bananas sugar and eggs to mixer blend until well blended
  2. In a different bowl add flour salt baking soda and mix well
  3. Add dry mix to wet mix in mixer and add pecans and chocolate and vanilla
  4. Mix until flour is all wet then stop
  5. Pour into a greased baking dish I use a loaf pan
  6. Bake at 350°F for about 50 minutes (until a tooth pick comes out clean)
