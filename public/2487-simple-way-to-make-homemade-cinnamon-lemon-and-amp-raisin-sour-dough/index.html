Steps to make Cinnamon, Lemon & Raisin Sour Dough:
  1. At least three days in advance of making your bread add the flour and water to an airtight tub
  2. Mix well and seal with the lid, leave at room temperature for at least 3 days
  3. Make the loaf by adding the bread flour, water, sugar, lemon zest, cinnamon and sour dough starter to a large bowl
  4. Beat with an electric dough hook or paddle for 5 minutes
  5. Leave to rest for 5 minutes
  6. Fold in the raisins
  7. Shape into a round cob, slash the top and dust with a little gluten free flour
  8. Leave to rest for 30 minutes
  9. Preheat the oven to 220 oC
  10. Bake for 40 minutes
  11. Allow to cool before slicing
