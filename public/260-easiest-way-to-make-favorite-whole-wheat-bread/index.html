The ingredients needed to make Whole wheat bread:
  1. Get 11/2 cups Luke warm water
  2. Get 11/2 cups milk scald
  3. Take 3 tablespoons melted butter
  4. Make ready 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  5. Prepare 11/2 teaspoons salt
  6. Make ready 1 package yeast
  7. Make ready 21/2 cups sifted course whole wheat flour
  8. Make ready 21/2 cups sifted medium whole wheat flour
  9. Take 21/2 cups fine whole wheat flour
