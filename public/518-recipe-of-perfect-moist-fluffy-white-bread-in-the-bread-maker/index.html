The ingredients needed to make Moist, Fluffy, White Bread in the Bread Maker:
  1. Make ready 180 grams ○Strong bread flour
  2. Take 40 grams ○Cake flour
  3. Get 20 grams ○Joshinko (or bread flour)
  4. Take 20 grams ●Sugar
  5. Prepare 4 grams ●Salt
  6. Take 8 grams ●Skim milk powder
  7. Take 16 grams ●Unsalted butter (or margarine)
  8. Make ready 100 ml ◎Non-flavoured soy milk (or milk)
  9. Prepare 75 ml ◎Water
  10. Take 4 grams Dry yeast
Instructions to make Moist, Fluffy, White Bread in the Bread Maker:
  1. Put the ingredients into the bread maker in this order: ◎ ingredients, ● ingredients,○ ingredients then the yeast. Start the dough course.
  2. After making sure the dough has risen enough, remove from the bread maker and separate into 8 equal portions. Punch out the gas, shape the dough into balls and leave to rest for 15 minutes (cover with a damp cloth to prevent drying out.)
  3. Lighly press out some of the gas, reform into balls and line up on a sheet of parchment paper. Make an indent across the center of the bread with a chopstick dusted in flour.
  4. Spray the bread with a little water and leave to proof for a second time. Spray again and then sift over some joshinko flour with a tea strainer or similar.
  5. Preheat the oven to 180°C (pre-heat the baking tray too), then turn the temperature down to 150°C and bake the bread for 12-13 minutes.
  6. Once baked, take the bread out of the oven on parchment paper and leave to cool. Store them in a plastic bag so that they don't dry out.
