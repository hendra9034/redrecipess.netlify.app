The ingredients needed to make Mom's Zucchini Bread:
  1. Prepare 2 c sugar
  2. Get 1 c oil
  3. Take 3 eggs
  4. Get 2 1/3 c flour
  5. Make ready 1 t salt
  6. Make ready 1 t baking soda
  7. Prepare 1/2 t baking powder
  8. Make ready 3 t vanilla
  9. Prepare 2 t cinnamon
  10. Prepare 2/3 c nuts
  11. Take 2 c grated zucchini
Instructions to make Mom's Zucchini Bread:
  1. Mix 1st 3 ingredients.
  2. Add remaining ingredients. Mix well.
  3. Pour into 2 greased and floured loaf pans. (if short on zucchini, add some water – batter is supposed to be pretty thin.)
  4. Bake at 350’ for 45 minutes.
  5. Cool. Remove from pans.
