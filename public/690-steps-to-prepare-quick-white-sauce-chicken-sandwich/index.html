The ingredients needed to make White sauce chicken sandwich:
  1. Take 1 kg chicken (boil into ginger garlic paste,salt and water)
  2. Take 1 cup grated carrot
  3. Make ready 2 medium piece grated green bell pepper
  4. Take 4-5 chopped green chillies
  5. Get 1 packet fresh bread
  6. Take For White Sauce:
  7. Take 2-3 tbspn butter
  8. Get 2 tbspn maida
  9. Take as per taste Salt
  10. Make ready 1 tsp Black pepper powder
  11. Make ready 1/2 cup milk
  12. Take For Garnish-
  13. Get as required Ketchup
  14. Prepare as required cheese slices

Steps to make White sauce chicken sandwich:
  1. Make white sauce add butter to the pan saute 2 tbspn of maida for 1 or 2 mins then add black pepper powder and salt to it, now add milk and let it thicken.
  2. Now to the white sauce add grated carrot, green bell pepper and finely chopped green chillies mix it well.Make sure stove is on low flame.
  3. Now add the boiled shredded chicken to the white sauce (boneless chicken boiled in ginger garlic paste,salt and water than shred it)
  4. Now take slices of bread,apply ketchup add the cooked chicken mixture to it,top with the slice of cheese on it.Lastly close with the slice of bread.
  5. Now add butter on both the sides of bread and grill it toaster or griller pan.Serve hot.
