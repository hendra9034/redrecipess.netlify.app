Instructions to make Bread Pinwheel Pizza:
  1. Take 12 fresh bread slices.
  2. Cut the sides and press with roller.
  3. Take bread slices one-by-one and spread peri peri sauce, cheese slice, and mayones.
  4. Spread meyones on the last bread slice and sprinkle herbs, oregano, white pepper and chilli flakes.
  5. Roll bread with aluminium foil and put in the fridge for 1 hour.
  6. After 1 hour cut the roll and make whee,l apply melted butter on all wheel.
  7. Take hot tawa spread butter & cook all wheel.
