The ingredients needed to make Simple Banana bread:
  1. Prepare 31/2 cups flour
  2. Get 11/2 cups sugar
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp Baking soda
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  6. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla essence
  7. Get 1 tsp cinnamon
  8. Take 4 eggs
  9. Make ready 2/3 cups water
  10. Take 1 cup oil
  11. Make ready 2 cups banana
