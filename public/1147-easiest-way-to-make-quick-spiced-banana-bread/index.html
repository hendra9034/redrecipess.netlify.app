The ingredients needed to make Spiced banana bread:
  1. Take 190 gr plain flour
  2. Make ready 30 gr powdered milk
  3. Take 1 teaspoon baking powder
  4. Make ready 1 teaspoon baking soda
  5. Take 2 teaspoon 5 spice powder
  6. Take 3 eggs
  7. Take 150 gr caster sugar
  8. Make ready 120 gr butter or margarine, melted
  9. Make ready 250 gr banana, mashed

Steps to make Spiced banana bread:
  1. In a big bowl, whisk together flour, powdered milk, baking powder, baking soda & 5 spice powder.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk eggs & sugar until well incorporated.
  3. Whisk in the egg mixture into the flour mixture until just combined.
  4. Add in melted butter & mashed banana then whisk until combined.
  5. Pour the batter into 4 greased & lined mini loaf pan, baked in a preheated oven of 190°C for 35 minutes.
  6. Put on a wire rack to cool, take out the cake then slice as you like.
