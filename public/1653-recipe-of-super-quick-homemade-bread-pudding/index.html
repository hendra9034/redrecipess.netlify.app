The ingredients needed to make Bread pudding:
  1. Prepare 6 slice bread
  2. Take 2 tbsp butter melted
  3. Make ready 4 eggs
  4. Make ready 2 cup milk
  5. Make ready 3/4 cup white sugar
  6. Get 1 tsp cinnamon
  7. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
