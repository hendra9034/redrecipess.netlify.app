The ingredients needed to make French sweet bread:
  1. Take 700 g white flour
  2. Get 1 egg
  3. Prepare 2 spoons sugar
  4. Prepare 1 spoon salt
  5. Get 4 spoons melted buter
  6. Make ready Orange peel (grated)
  7. Get 1 spoon yeast

Steps to make French sweet bread:
  1. Knead the ingredients while adding the milk(8min)
  2. Let it rest for 30min in a warm place
  3. Grease the vessel with some butter and flour.
  4. Make it a shape of medium balls and let some space between theme.
  5. Let it rest for 30 min
  6. Grease the surface of the paste with mixed milk and the egg's yellow.
  7. Put in the oven.on a meduim fire for 20 minute
  8. Decorate it with Poppy and grated coconut.
