The ingredients needed to make Nanny's Bread:
  1. Get 4 cup Whole wheat flour
  2. Make ready 5 cup Unbleached flour
  3. Get 3 envelope Dry yeast
  4. Take 3 tsp Salt
  5. Take 1/3 cup Powdered milk
  6. Prepare 3 cup Warm water
  7. Take 1/3 cup Oil
  8. Make ready 1/3 cup Honey

Instructions to make Nanny's Bread:
  1. Mix flours in large bowl
  2. Mix remaining ingredients and pour over flour
  3. Blend and knead
  4. Oil bowl and flip dough to coat outside with oil
  5. Cover & let rise
  6. Punch down and knead to remove bubbles
  7. Roll tightly into greased loaf pan
  8. Bake 30 minutes at 375°
