The ingredients needed to make Super Dee Duper Zucchini Bread:
  1. Prepare 3 large eggs
  2. Take 3 tsp vanilla extract
  3. Get 2 cup sugar
  4. Make ready 3 cup sifted all purpose flour
  5. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  6. Make ready 1/4 tsp baking powder
  7. Get 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  8. Get 1/2 cup raisins or dried cranberries (optional)
  9. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  10. Make ready 3 cup zucchini, peeled, washed, shredded, deseeded if seeds are large
  11. Get 2 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  12. Get 1 cup melted room temp butter
Steps to make Super Dee Duper Zucchini Bread:
  1. preheat oven to 325°
  2. in a large bowl combine all ingredients, except zuchini n raisins, mix well
  3. fold in zucchini and raisins
  4. pour into 2 lightly greased bread pans.
  5. bake for about one hour… bread will be done when toothpick inserted into center comes out clean..
  6. let cool, slice and enjoy
