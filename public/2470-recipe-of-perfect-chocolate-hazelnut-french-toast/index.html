The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Hazelnut French Toast:
  1. Prepare gluten free bread
  2. Get egg
  3. Prepare dairy free milk
  4. Take icing sugar
  5. Make ready dairy free chocolate and hazelnut spread
Steps to make Chocolate Hazelnut French Toast:
  1. Whisk together the egg, dairy free milk and icing sugar - Spread the chocolate and haelnut spread on the bread and sandwich together
  2. Heat a drizzle of olive oil in a shallow pan - Dip both sides of the sandwich in the egg mix
  3. Fry in the pan for a couple of minutes each side or until golden brown - Cut in half and dust with icing sugar
