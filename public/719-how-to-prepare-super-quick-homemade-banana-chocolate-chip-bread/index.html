The ingredients needed to make Banana Chocolate Chip Bread:
  1. Prepare 4 ripe bananas
  2. Get 85 grams unsalted butter, melted and cooled
  3. Prepare 1/3 cup plain Greek yogurt
  4. Get 220 grams brown sugar (light or dark or a combination)
  5. Make ready 2 eggs
  6. Take 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  7. Make ready 200 grams all-purpose flour
  8. Take 55 grams whole wheat flour
  9. Make ready 1 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  10. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  11. Prepare 135 grams mini chocolate chips or finely chopped chocolate (semisweet or bittersweet)
  12. Take 130 grams walnuts, toasted and coarsely chopped
  13. Take Turbinado sugar (or any coarse sugar) for finishing

Steps to make Banana Chocolate Chip Bread:
  1. Heat oven to 350F. Lightly grease and flour a 9x5 loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl mash the bananas.
  3. Add butter, yogurt, brown sugar, eggs and vanilla. Combine well.
  4. In a medium bowl mix flour, baking soda a salt. Add to banana mix and combine using a spatula.
  5. Gently add the chocolate and walnuts, and mix until incorporated.
  6. Pour batter into loaf pan and spread into an even later. Sprinkle the surface with the coarse sugar.
  7. Bake for 75 minutes until edges of loaf start to pull away from the pan. Transfer to cooling rack and run a thin knife around the edge of the banana bread to separate it from the pan.
  8. After 15 minutes of cooling, unmold and cool completely.
