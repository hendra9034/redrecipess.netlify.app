The ingredients needed to make Banana Nut Bread, Easy & Yummy:
  1. Take Bowl 1, dry mix
  2. Take 2 cup flour, all purpose
  3. Get 1 tsp salt
  4. Get 2 tsp baking soda
  5. Take Bowl 2, moist mix
  6. Get 1 cup butter or margarine
  7. Prepare 2 cup sugar, white
  8. Get 2 cup bananas, mashed
  9. Take 4 each eggs, beaten
  10. Get 1 cup walnuts, chopped

Instructions to make Banana Nut Bread, Easy & Yummy:
  1. Preheat oven at 350°F & grease 2 loaf pans
  2. Sift all ingredients for Bowl 1 and set aside.
  3. In Bowl 2, cream the soft butter with the white sugar, then add the bananas.
  4. Beat the eggs in a cup until they're evenly yellow before adding them to the moist mix.
  5. Chop the walnuts coarsely (or to taste) and add. You can use more if your family is nutty like mine :)
  6. Now, we're ready to add the dry mix, but not all at once. Make sure it's evenly blended.
  7. Divide batter into the 2 loaf pans and bake until a knife inserted in the middle comes out clean. In my old oven, this took about 60-70 minutes, but in the new one, takes only 40-45!
