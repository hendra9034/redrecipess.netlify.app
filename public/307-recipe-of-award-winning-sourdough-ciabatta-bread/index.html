The ingredients needed to make Sourdough ciabatta bread:
  1. Get 380 gr bread flour (13% protein)
  2. Get 100 gr whole-wheat flour
  3. Make ready 420 gr water
  4. Prepare 25 gr olive oil
  5. Prepare 85-100 gr sourdough starter (50%rye 50%BF or 100%BF)
  6. Prepare 10 gr salt
  7. Take Flour for dusting

Steps to make Sourdough ciabatta bread:
  1. Mix all the ingredients in the stand mixer for 25 minutes.
  2. Cover the bowl and let rest for 1 hour. Then perform 3 sets of coil fold (45’ each set)
  3. After 3 sets of coil fold, place the dough inside a box and cover with a lid, and transfer it to the fridge for 12-24 hours.
  4. When ready to bake, remove the vessel from the fridge. Dust the top of the dough liberally with flour. Dust a work surface liberally with flour, too. Turn the dough out onto the prepared work surface. 1. Pat the dough into a rectangle. Cut the rectangle into 6 roughly equal portions. 1. Transfer the portions to the baking tray with parchment paper, to a 475ºF oven for 20 minutes with steam. Lower the heat to 450ºF and bake for 10 minutes more.
  5. Transfer the rolls immediately to a cooling rack, and let cool for at least 20 minutes before cutting.
